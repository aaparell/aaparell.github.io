So, what restaurants are only in Illinois? Here are some of the must-try restaurants:

1. Al's Italian Beef

Al's Italian Beef is a Chicago institution that has been serving up their famous Italian beef sandwiches since 1938. The restaurant's signature dish is made with thinly sliced roast beef piled high on a French roll and topped with sweet peppers or spicy giardiniera. It's a messy but delicious sandwich that you won't find anywhere else.

2. Portillo's

Another Chicago favorite, Portillo's is known for its hot dogs, Italian beef sandwiches, and chocolate cake shakes. The restaurant has been serving up its classic Chicago-style fare since 1963 and has expanded to locations throughout the state.

3. Lou Malnati's Pizzeria

When it comes to deep-dish pizza, Lou Malnati's is a must-visit. The restaurant has been serving up its signature pizza since 1971, and it's become a Chicago staple. The deep-dish pizza is made with a buttery crust, chunky tomato sauce, and lots of cheese.

4. Maid-Rite Sandwich Shop

The Maid-Rite Sandwich Shop has been serving up its loose meat sandwiches since 1926. The restaurant's signature sandwich is made with seasoned ground beef served on a warm bun with mustard, pickles, and onions. It's a simple but delicious sandwich that you won't find anywhere else.

5. Schoop's Hamburgers

Schoop's Hamburgers is a family-owned restaurant that has been serving up its famous burgers since 1948. The restaurant's signature burger is made with two beef patties, American cheese, lettuce, tomato, pickles, and Schoop's special sauce. It's a classic burger that you won't find anywhere else.

6. The Aviary

If you're looking for a unique dining experience, The Aviary is the place to go. The restaurant is known for its creative cocktails and innovative food menu. The Aviary has won numerous awards, including a James Beard Award for Outstanding Bar Program.

Conclusion

Illinois has a diverse food scene that offers something for everyone. From classic Chicago-style hot dogs to innovative cocktails, there are many restaurants that you can only find in the Prairie State. So, next time you're in Illinois, make sure to try out some of these unique dining options.
