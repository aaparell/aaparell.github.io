2021 Men s Dress Shoe Styles A Guide To Looking Sharp And Feeling Comfortable

Written by Christine Sep 25, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of sacrificing comfort for style when it comes to dress shoes? Do you dread the thought of wearing stiff, uncomfortable shoes to formal events? Well, fear not, because 2021 brings a plethora of men's dress shoe styles that offer both fashion and comfort. In this guide, we'll walk you through the latest trends in men's dress shoes and help you find the perfect pair for any occasion.

When it comes to dress shoes, many men are faced with the dilemma of choosing between style and comfort. The last thing you want is to be stuck wearing painful shoes that ruin your night. Fortunately, with the latest trends in 2021 men's dress shoe styles, you can have the best of both worlds.

So, what can you expect from 2021 men's dress shoe styles? This year, we're seeing a focus on versatility, with shoes that can be worn for both formal and casual occasions. We're also seeing a rise in sustainable materials and eco-friendly designs. And, of course, comfort is a top priority, with many brands incorporating features like memory foam insoles and flexible outsoles.

In this guide, we'll cover some of the top 2021 men's dress shoe styles, including loafers, oxfords, and brogues. We'll also discuss the latest trends in colors and materials, and offer tips on how to style your dress shoes for different occasions.

Loafers: The Ultimate in Comfort and Style

Loafers have been a staple in men's fashion for decades, and for good reason. These slip-on shoes offer a level of comfort that is unmatched by traditional lace-up dress shoes. In 2021, we're seeing a resurgence of loafers in a variety of materials and colors.

Personally, my favorite pair of loafers are the Cole Haan Penny Loafers. They are made with a soft leather upper and a memory foam footbed for all-day comfort. They can be dressed up with a suit or dressed down with jeans for a more casual look.

When shopping for loafers, look for materials like suede, leather, and even velvet. And don't be afraid to experiment with different colors and patterns, like leopard print or bold shades of blue.

Oxfords: Classic Style with a Modern Twist

Oxfords are a timeless classic in men's dress shoes, but in 2021, we're seeing some modern updates to this traditional style. One trend we're loving is the use of unexpected materials, like suede or canvas, in place of the traditional leather.

If you're looking for a comfortable and stylish pair of oxfords, I highly recommend the Rockport Total Motion Dress Oxford. They feature a shock-absorbing heel, a flexible outsole, and a moisture-wicking lining to keep your feet cool and dry.

When it comes to styling oxfords, you can't go wrong with a classic suit. But don't be afraid to mix things up with a pair of slim-fit pants and a casual button-down shirt.

Brogues: A Timeless Favorite with a Modern Twist

Brogues are a classic dress shoe style that have been around for centuries. In 2021, we're seeing some updates to this traditional style, with designers incorporating unexpected materials and colors.

One of my favorite pairs of brogues is the Allen Edmonds Strandmok. They feature a distressed leather upper and a full leather lining for maximum comfort. And with their unique color options, like navy and olive, they're sure to make a statement.

When styling brogues, it's all about balance. Pair them with a classic suit for a formal look, or dress them down with a pair of jeans and a sweater for a more casual vibe.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Pair of Dress Shoes

When shopping for dress shoes, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, make sure the shoes fit properly. You don't want to be stuck wearing shoes that are too tight or too loose. Second, look for shoes with features that will make them comfortable to wear for long periods of time, like memory foam insoles or flexible outsoles. And finally, don't be afraid to experiment with different styles, colors, and materials.

Conclusion of 2021 Men's Dress Shoe Styles

In 2021, men's dress shoe styles are all about versatility, sustainability, and comfort. From loafers to oxfords to brogues, there are plenty of options to choose from. When shopping for dress shoes, remember to prioritize comfort and look for features like memory foam insoles and flexible outsoles. And don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and materials to find the perfect pair for any occasion.

Question and Answer

Q: What materials are popular for men's dress shoes in 2021?

A: In 2021, we're seeing a rise in sustainable materials like recycled leather and eco-friendly materials like bamboo or cork.

Q: Are loafers appropriate for formal occasions?

A: Yes! Loafers can be dressed up with a suit for a formal occasion, or dressed down with jeans for a more casual look.

Q: Can oxfords be worn with jeans?

A: Absolutely! Oxfords can be dressed up with a suit, or dressed down with a pair of jeans and a casual shirt.

Q: How do I know if my dress shoes fit properly?

A: Make sure there is about a half-inch of space between your longest toe and the end of the shoe. The shoe should also fit snugly around your heel without slipping.
