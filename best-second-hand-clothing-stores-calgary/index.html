The Best Second Hand Clothing Stores in Calgary: Our Top Picks

One of our favorite second hand clothing stores in Calgary is the famous Value Village. This thrift store chain has multiple locations throughout the city and is known for its vast selection of clothing, shoes, and accessories at affordable prices. Another great option is Plato's Closet, which specializes in trendy and fashionable clothing for young adults.

For those looking for more high-end options, there's Vespucci Consignment. This boutique carries designer clothing and accessories at a fraction of the original cost, making luxury fashion accessible to everyone. Another great option is the upscale vintage store Ruby Slipper, which features carefully curated items from the 1920s to the 1980s.

Tips for Shopping at Second Hand Clothing Stores

Shopping at second hand clothing stores can be a fun and rewarding experience, but it can also be overwhelming if you don't know what you're looking for. Here are some tips for getting the most out of your thrifting experience:

1. Have a clear idea of what you're looking for before you start shopping.

2. Try on everything you're interested in, as sizes and styles can vary widely.

3. Check for any signs of wear or damage before purchasing an item.

4. Don't be afraid to negotiate prices, especially at consignment stores.

The Environmental Benefits of Second Hand Clothing

Shopping at second hand clothing stores isn't just good for your wallet - it's also good for the planet. By buying used clothing, you're reducing the demand for new clothing production, which is a major contributor to greenhouse gas emissions and other environmental issues. Additionally, many second hand clothing stores support local charities and community initiatives, so you can feel good about where your money is going.

How to Care for Second Hand Clothing

Once you've found the perfect second hand items, it's important to take care of them properly to ensure they last as long as possible. Here are some tips for caring for your thrifted clothing:

1. Always check the care instructions on the label before washing.

2. Wash items in cold water to prevent shrinkage and fading.

3. Hang dry or lay flat to dry to prevent damage to delicate fabrics.

4. Store items in a cool, dry place to prevent mold and mildew.

Conclusion of Best Second Hand Clothing Stores in Calgary

Shopping at second hand clothing stores in Calgary is a great way to save money, reduce waste, and find unique and high-quality items. Whether you're looking for vintage finds or contemporary fashion, there's something for everyone at these affordable and sustainable stores. By following our tips for shopping and caring for your thrifted clothing, you can enjoy your new pieces for years to come.

Question and Answer

Q: How can I find the best deals at second hand clothing stores in Calgary?

A: One of the best ways to find deals is to shop during sales or clearance events. Many second hand clothing stores also offer loyalty programs or email newsletters that provide exclusive discounts and promotions.

Q: Are there any cons to shopping at second hand clothing stores?

A: The main con is that you may have to spend more time searching for the perfect item, as second hand stores have a constantly changing inventory. Additionally, some items may be in less-than-perfect condition, so it's important to inspect them carefully before purchasing.

Q: Can I sell my own clothing at second hand clothing stores in Calgary?

A: Yes, many second hand clothing stores offer consignment programs where you can sell your gently used clothing and accessories for a portion of the sale price.

Q: What should I wear when shopping at second hand clothing stores?

A: Wear comfortable and easy-to-try-on clothing, such as leggings and a tank top, so you can easily try on items over your clothes. Additionally, wear slip-on shoes for ease of trying on shoes.
