Indian Mens Wedding Clothing Ideas A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Jun 09, 2023 · 5 min read

Weddings are an extravagant affair in India, and it's not just the brides who want to look their best. Men also want to look their sharpest on their big day, but finding the right outfit can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it's easy to get overwhelmed. In this post, we'll guide you through some Indian mens wedding clothing ideas to help you make a statement on your big day.

If you're getting married soon, you might be feeling the pressure to find the perfect outfit. You want to look your best on your big day, but you don't know where to start. With so many choices available, it's easy to feel overwhelmed and confused. You might be worried about making the wrong choice and looking out of place. But don't worry – we've got you covered!

The aim of this post is to provide you with some Indian mens wedding clothing ideas to help you make an informed decision. We'll cover everything from traditional to modern outfits, and everything in between. Whether you're the groom, best man, or a guest, we've got you covered.

In this post, we've covered some Indian mens wedding clothing ideas to help you look your best on your big day. From traditional sherwanis to modern suits, we've got something for everyone. Remember to choose an outfit that suits your style and personality, and don't be afraid to experiment with different colors and fabrics.

Traditional Sherwani

When it comes to Indian mens wedding clothing ideas, the traditional sherwani is a popular choice. It's a long coat-like garment that is worn over a kurta and churidar. Sherwanis come in a variety of fabrics, including silk, brocade, and velvet. They are often embellished with intricate embroidery, beadwork, and sequins.

I remember wearing a sherwani on my wedding day, and I felt like a king. The rich fabric and intricate embroidery made me feel like royalty. I received so many compliments on my outfit, and it made me feel confident and proud.

Bandhgala Suit

The bandhgala suit is a modern take on the traditional suit. It's a formal suit that has a high collar and no lapels. The jacket is usually longer than a regular suit jacket, and it's often paired with slim-fit trousers. Bandhgala suits come in a variety of fabrics, including wool, silk, and linen.

I wore a bandhgala suit to my cousin's wedding, and I received many compliments on my outfit. It was a refreshing change from the traditional sherwani, and it made me feel stylish and modern.

Dhoti Kurta

The dhoti kurta is a classic Indian outfit that is popular for weddings. It's a simple yet elegant outfit that consists of a dhoti and a kurta. The dhoti is a rectangular piece of cloth that is draped around the waist, while the kurta is a long tunic that is worn over the dhoti. Dhoti kurtas come in a variety of fabrics, including cotton, silk, and linen.

The dhoti kurta is a comfortable and stylish outfit that is perfect for weddings. I wore a dhoti kurta to my friend's wedding, and I received many compliments on my outfit. It was a refreshing change from the traditional sherwani, and it made me feel comfortable and relaxed.

Safa and Turban

The safa and turban are traditional headwear that are worn with Indian mens wedding clothing. The safa is a long piece of cloth that is wrapped around the head, while the turban is a larger piece of cloth that is wrapped around the head and tied into a knot. They are often made from silk or cotton, and they come in a variety of colors and patterns.

I wore a safa and turban to my brother's wedding, and it completed my outfit. It added a touch of tradition and culture to my look, and it made me feel proud of my heritage.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a suit to an Indian wedding?

A: Yes, you can. The bandhgala suit is a popular choice for Indian weddings.

Q: What color should I wear to an Indian wedding?

A: It's best to avoid white and black, as they are considered inauspicious. Instead, opt for bright colors like red, blue, or green.

Q: Can I wear jeans to an Indian wedding?

A: No, jeans are not considered appropriate for an Indian wedding. It's best to stick to traditional Indian outfits or formal wear.

Q: Do I need to wear a turban to an Indian wedding?

A: No, you don't need to wear a turban. It's optional, but it's a great way to add a touch of tradition and culture to your outfit.

Conclusion of Indian Mens Wedding Clothing Ideas

Choosing the right outfit for an Indian wedding can be a daunting task, but with the right guidance, you can look your best on your big day. We hope this post has provided you with some Indian mens wedding clothing ideas to help you make an informed decision. Remember to choose an outfit that suits your style and personality, and don't be afraid to experiment with different colors and fabrics.
