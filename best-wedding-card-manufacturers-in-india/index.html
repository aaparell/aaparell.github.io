1. Parekh Cards: Founded in 1984, Parekh Cards is a leading manufacturer and exporter of Indian wedding cards with a vast collection of designs, colors, and styles to choose from. They also offer customization options to create unique and personalized wedding invitations.

2. Vistaprint: Vistaprint is a popular online platform that offers a wide range of wedding cards in various designs and themes, along with customization options to add your personal touch. They also provide free sample kits to help you choose the right design and paper quality.

3. Indian Wedding Card: Indian Wedding Card is a well-known wedding card manufacturer in India that offers an extensive collection of traditional and modern designs in various colors, styles, and paper quality. They also provide customization options to create bespoke wedding invitations.

4. Regal Cards: Regal Cards is a leading manufacturer and supplier of Indian wedding cards with over 80 years of experience in the industry. They offer a vast collection of traditional and contemporary designs, along with customization options to create personalized wedding invitations.

5. King of Cards: King of Cards is one of the best wedding card manufacturers in India that offers a wide range of designs, colors, and styles to choose from. They also provide customization options to create unique and personalized wedding invitations that reflect your style and personality.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How much does a wedding card cost in India?

A: The cost of a wedding card in India varies depending on the design, paper quality, and customization options. On average, a wedding card can cost anywhere between Rs. 20 to Rs. 500 per card.

Q: Can I customize my wedding card?

A: Yes, most wedding card manufacturers in India offer customization options to create unique and personalized wedding invitations that reflect your style and personality.

Q: How long does it take to receive my wedding cards?

A: The delivery time for wedding cards in India can vary depending on the manufacturer and your location. It's best to check with the manufacturer for the estimated delivery time.

Q: Can I order a sample of my wedding card before placing the final order?

A: Yes, most wedding card manufacturers in India offer free sample kits to help you choose the right design and paper quality before placing the final order.
