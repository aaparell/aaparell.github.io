Question and Answer

Q: How much does it cost to buy a wedding suit in Bangalore?

A: The cost of a wedding suit in Bangalore depends on several factors, such as the fabric, design, and customization. On average, a basic wedding suit can cost between INR 10,000 to INR 20,000, while a designer suit can cost between INR 50,000 to INR 1,00,000.

Q: How long does it take to get a customized wedding outfit in Bangalore?

A: The time taken to get a customized wedding outfit in Bangalore depends on several factors, such as the complexity of the design, the availability of the fabric, and the workload of the tailor. On average, it can take between 2-4 weeks to get a customized wedding outfit.

Q: What should I wear for a summer wedding in Bangalore?

A: For a summer wedding in Bangalore, it's best to wear light and breathable fabrics, such as cotton, linen, or silk. You can opt for light-colored suits, sherwanis, or Indo-western outfits that are comfortable and stylish.

Q: Can I rent a wedding outfit in Bangalore?

A: Yes, there are several men's wedding wear shops in Bangalore that offer rental services for wedding outfits. This can be a cost-effective option for those who don't want to invest in buying an outfit.
