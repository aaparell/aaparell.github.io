What Is The Best Classic Vespa

Written by Eveline Jun 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Vespa scooters are iconic and have been popular worldwide since the 1950s. However, with so many classic Vespa models available, it can be challenging to decide which one is the best. In this article, we will explore the different classic models and help you determine which one is the best for you.

Are you a vintage Vespa enthusiast or a first-time buyer looking for a classic scooter? Do you want a Vespa for commuting, leisure rides, or weekend trips? Answering these questions will help you identify your needs and narrow down your choices.

What is the Best Classic Vespa?

The Vespa PX 150 is widely considered the best classic Vespa model. It was originally launched in 1977 and has been in continuous production until 2017, becoming the longest-running scooter model. Vespa PX 150 is a manual shift scooter with a two-stroke engine, producing 8.2 horsepower, and capable of reaching speeds up to 60 mph. It is a reliable and sturdy scooter that can handle long rides and rough terrains.

As a Vespa enthusiast, I have owned several classic models, including Vespa GS 150, Vespa Super 150, and Vespa Sprint 150. However, the Vespa PX 150 remains my all-time favorite. It is the perfect balance between vintage style, performance, and functionality.

Why is Vespa PX 150 the Best Classic Vespa?

The Vespa PX 150 has several features that make it the best classic Vespa. Firstly, its manual shift adds an element of control and engagement that is missing in modern automatic scooters. Secondly, its two-stroke engine provides a unique sound and power that is hard to replicate in four-stroke engines. Thirdly, its design is timeless, and it has become a symbol of Italian craftsmanship and style.

Another advantage of owning a Vespa PX 150 is the availability of spare parts and mechanics. Since it has been in production for over 40 years, there are plenty of spare parts and mechanics who can service and repair it. Moreover, the Vespa community is vast and active, and you can find support and advice from fellow Vespa enthusiasts.

Should You Consider Other Classic Vespa Models?

While Vespa PX 150 is the best classic Vespa, there are several other models worth considering. Vespa GS 150, Vespa Super 150, Vespa Sprint 150, Vespa Primavera, and Vespa Rally 200 are all classic models that have their own charm and legacy. However, each model has its own strengths and weaknesses, and you should research and test ride them before making a decision.

How to Choose the Best Classic Vespa for You?

Choosing the best classic Vespa for you depends on several factors, including your budget, purpose, and preference. Here are some tips to help you make a decision:

  • Research different classic Vespa models and their specifications
  • Determine your budget and availability of spare parts and mechanics
  • Consider your purpose, whether it is commuting, leisure rides, or weekend trips
  • Test ride different models and identify the one that suits your style and comfort

FAQs about the Best Classic Vespa

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best classic Vespa:

Q: What is the most reliable classic Vespa model?

A: The Vespa PX 150 is considered the most reliable classic Vespa model due to its sturdy body, reliable engine, and availability of spare parts.

Q: What is the fastest classic Vespa model?

A: The Vespa Rally 200 is considered the fastest classic Vespa model, with a top speed of 75 mph.

Q: What is the most expensive classic Vespa model?

A: The Vespa GS 150 is considered the most expensive classic Vespa model, with a price range of $8,000 to $12,000.

Q: Can I customize my classic Vespa?

A: Yes, you can customize your classic Vespa with different accessories and modifications, such as a leather seat, a chrome luggage rack, or a performance exhaust.

Conclusion of What is the Best Classic Vespa

In conclusion, the Vespa PX 150 is the best classic Vespa model due to its manual shift, two-stroke engine, timeless design, and availability of spare parts and mechanics. However, there are several other classic Vespa models worth considering, and your choice depends on your budget, purpose, and preference. By following the tips and advice in this article, you can find the best classic Vespa for you and enjoy the ride of your life.
