What Kind Of Flats To Wear With Dresses A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect pair of flats to wear with your dresses? Do you find yourself constantly changing your shoes before leaving the house? Choosing the right pair of flats to wear with dresses can be a daunting task, but don't worry, we've got you covered!

When it comes to finding the perfect pair of flats to wear with dresses, there are a few pain points that many women experience. Some of these include finding a pair that is comfortable enough to wear all day, choosing a style that complements the dress, and finding a color that matches the dress.

So, what kind of flats should you wear with dresses? The answer depends on the style of dress you are wearing, the occasion, and your personal preferences. In this complete guide, we will explore different types of flats and how to pair them with dresses to create a stylish and comfortable look.

In summary, this article will cover everything you need to know about what kind of flats to wear with dresses. We will discuss different types of flats, how to choose the right pair for your dress, and provide some tips and tricks for creating a stylish and comfortable look.

The Classic Ballet Flat

One of the most popular types of flats to wear with dresses is the classic ballet flat. This timeless style is versatile and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. For a casual look, pair a simple black ballet flat with a flowy sundress. For a more formal event, opt for a metallic or embellished ballet flat to add some sparkle to your outfit.

Personally, I love pairing a bright colored ballet flat with a simple black dress. The pop of color adds some fun to the outfit and makes me feel confident and stylish.

The Pointed Toe Flat

If you're looking for a more sophisticated look, consider a pointed toe flat. This style is great for a formal event or a day at the office. Pair a black pointed toe flat with a shift dress for a classic look, or choose a brightly colored pointed toe flat to add some personality to your outfit.

I recently wore a pair of leopard print pointed toe flats with a black midi dress to a work event and received so many compliments on my outfit. The pointed toe flats added some edge to the classic black dress and made me feel confident and stylish.

The Loafer Flat

If you're looking for a comfortable yet stylish option, consider a loafer flat. This style is perfect for a day of running errands or a casual lunch date. Pair a simple loafer flat with a denim dress for a classic look, or choose a printed loafer flat to add some fun to your outfit.

A few months ago, I paired a pair of metallic loafer flats with a simple white cotton dress for a day of exploring a new city. The flats were comfortable enough to walk around in all day, but still added some style to my outfit.

The Sandal Flat

For a more casual look, consider a sandal flat. This style is perfect for a day at the beach or a summer picnic. Pair a simple tan sandal flat with a maxi dress for a bohemian look, or choose a brightly colored sandal flat to add some fun to your outfit.

I recently wore a pair of embellished sandal flats with a simple white sundress to a beach picnic. The flats were comfortable enough to wear all day, and the embellishments added some sparkle to my outfit.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear flats with a formal dress?

A: Yes, you can definitely wear flats with a formal dress. Choose a style that is embellished or metallic to add some glamour to your outfit.

Q: Can I wear flats with a midi dress?

A: Yes, flats look great with midi dresses! Choose a style that complements the length of the dress, such as a pointed toe or loafer flat.

Q: Can I wear flats with a maxi dress?

A: Absolutely! Sandal flats look great with maxi dresses for a bohemian look, while ballet flats or pointed toe flats can add some sophistication to your outfit.

Q: Can I wear flats with a cocktail dress?

A: Yes, you can definitely wear flats with a cocktail dress. Choose a style that is embellished or metallic to add some glamour to your outfit.

Conclusion of What Kind of Flats to Wear with Dresses

Choosing the perfect pair of flats to wear with dresses can be a challenge, but with this complete guide, you can create a stylish and comfortable look for any occasion. Whether you choose a classic ballet flat, a sophisticated pointed toe flat, a comfortable loafer flat, or a casual sandal flat, make sure to choose a style that complements your dress and makes you feel confident and stylish. Happy shopping!
