What Is The Average Salary For Graphic Designers In The Us

Written by Luffy Jun 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As a graphic designer, you might be wondering what the average salary for your profession is in the United States. It can be challenging to determine the real numbers, especially when you consider different factors such as experience, location, and industry.

According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual wage for graphic designers is $52,110 per year or $25.05 per hour. The lowest 10 percent earn less than $31,720, and the highest 10 percent earn more than $89,210.

In general, graphic designers who work in advertising, public relations, and related services make the highest median annual wage at $55,380. On the other hand, those who work in specialized design services make a median annual wage of $50,040. Freelance graphic designers may earn more or less than this depending on their client base and experience.

My Experience with Graphic Designer Salary in the US

As a freelance graphic designer based in New York City, my salary has varied over the years. When I first started, I was charging around $25 per hour, but as I gained more experience and a better client base, I increased my rates to $50 per hour. Many of my clients are willing to pay more for a designer with a proven track record of producing high-quality work.

Factors that Affect Graphic Designer Salary in the US

Several factors can affect the salary of a graphic designer in the United States, including:

Experience:

As with any profession, the more experience you have, the more you can expect to earn. Entry-level graphic designers in the US can expect to earn around $40,000 per year, while those with several years of experience can earn up to $75,000 per year.

Location:

The location where you work can significantly impact your salary. For example, graphic designers in New York City can expect to earn more than those in smaller cities or towns. The cost of living in your location also plays a role in determining your salary.

Industry:

As mentioned earlier, graphic designers who work in advertising, public relations, and related services tend to make more money. Other industries that pay well for graphic designers include software publishing, wholesale trade, and manufacturing.

How to Negotiate Your Graphic Designer Salary in the US

If you're seeking to increase your graphic designer salary in the US, there are several things you can do to negotiate a higher rate:

- Research the average salary for graphic designers in your location and industry. This will give you an idea of what you should be earning.

- Be confident in your skills and experience. If you believe you're worth more, don't be afraid to ask for it.

- Build a strong portfolio that showcases your best work. This will demonstrate your skills and expertise to potential clients or employers.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I earn more as a freelance graphic designer compared to a full-time employee?

A: It depends on several factors, including your experience, client base, and industry. Freelance graphic designers can earn more than full-time employees, but they also have to spend more time finding new clients and managing their finances.

Q: Is it possible to negotiate my salary as a graphic designer?

A: Yes, it is possible to negotiate your salary as a graphic designer. However, it's essential to be confident in your skills and experience and have evidence to support your request for a higher rate.

Q: What skills do I need to become a successful graphic designer?

A: Successful graphic designers need a range of skills, including creativity, problem-solving, communication, and technical skills such as using design software. They also need to stay up-to-date with industry trends and be willing to learn new skills.

Q: What are some industries that employ a lot of graphic designers in the US?

A: Some industries that employ a lot of graphic designers in the US include advertising, public relations, specialized design services, software publishing, and manufacturing.

Conclusion of Graphic Designer Average Salary in US

In conclusion, the average salary for graphic designers in the US is $52,110 per year or $25.05 per hour. However, several factors can impact your salary, including experience, location, and industry. By negotiating your salary and building a strong portfolio, you can increase your earning potential as a graphic designer in the US.
