Naspers has seen significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue performing well in the future.

FirstRand

FirstRand is a financial services company that operates in South Africa and several other African countries. It is one of the largest banks in South Africa and has a strong presence in the retail, commercial, and investment banking sectors. FirstRand has a solid financial standing and is expected to continue performing well in the future.

Sasol

Sasol is an energy and chemical company that operates in more than 30 countries. It is known for its production of fuels, chemicals, and other products derived from coal, oil, and gas. Sasol has a solid financial standing and is expected to continue performing well in the future, especially as the demand for energy and chemicals continues to rise.
