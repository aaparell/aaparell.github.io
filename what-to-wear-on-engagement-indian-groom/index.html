What To Wear On Engagement Indian Groom A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 24, 2023 · 5 min read

Indian weddings are known for their grandeur and extravagance, and the engagement ceremony is no exception. As an Indian groom, you want to look your best on your engagement day. The outfit you wear is an essential element of your overall look. Choosing the right outfit can be overwhelming, but worry not as we have got you covered. In this post, we will guide you through what to wear on engagement Indian groom.

The engagement ceremony is the first formal event in the wedding journey. It is a day when the couple exchange rings, and the families come together to celebrate. As an Indian groom, you want to look stylish and elegant, but also comfortable. The engagement ceremony is usually held during the day, so you want to choose an outfit that is not too heavy or warm. Additionally, you want to choose an outfit that matches your bride-to-be's outfit.

When it comes to what to wear on engagement Indian groom, there are various options to choose from. The most popular outfit choices for Indian grooms are sherwani, kurta, and bandhgala. These outfits come in various styles, colors, and designs, giving you plenty of options to choose from. It is essential to choose an outfit that suits your taste and personality, but also matches the theme of the engagement ceremony.

To summarize, when deciding on what to wear on engagement Indian groom, keep in mind the time of the day, the theme of the ceremony, and your bride-to-be's outfit. Choose an outfit that is comfortable, stylish, and matches your personality. With these factors in mind, you will surely make a statement on your engagement day.

What to Wear on Engagement Indian Groom: Sherwani

Sherwani is a traditional Indian outfit that has been a popular choice for Indian grooms for decades. It is a long coat-like garment that is usually worn with a churidar or a salwar. Sherwanis come in various designs and colors, ranging from simple to heavily embroidered. You can choose a sherwani that matches the theme of your engagement ceremony and your taste.

Personally, I wore a cream-colored sherwani on my engagement day. It had intricate embroidery on the collar and sleeves, giving it an elegant look. I paired it with a cream-colored churidar and a pair of gold jutti. The outfit was comfortable, stylish, and matched my bride-to-be's outfit.

What to Wear on Engagement Indian Groom: Kurta

Kurta is another popular option for Indian grooms. It is a long shirt that is usually worn with pajama or churidar. Kurta comes in various designs, colors, and fabrics, giving you plenty of options to choose from. It is essential to choose a kurta that matches the theme of your engagement ceremony and your personality.

On my friend's engagement day, he wore a navy-blue kurta with intricate embroidery on the collar and sleeves. He paired it with a white churidar and a pair of brown mojari. The outfit was simple, elegant, and comfortable, making him stand out on his engagement day.

What to Wear on Engagement Indian Groom: Bandhgala

Bandhgala is a jacket-like garment that is usually worn with a kurta or a shirt. It is a modern outfit that has gained popularity among Indian grooms in recent years. Bandhgalas come in various designs, colors, and fabrics, giving you plenty of options to choose from. It is essential to choose a bandhgala that matches the theme of your engagement ceremony and your taste.

On my cousin's engagement day, he wore a black bandhgala with gold buttons. He paired it with a white shirt and black trousers. The outfit was simple, stylish, and comfortable, making him stand out on his engagement day.

Question and Answer

Q. Can I wear a suit on my engagement day?

A. While suits are a popular outfit choice for western weddings, they are not a common choice for Indian engagement ceremonies. It is best to stick to traditional Indian outfits like sherwani, kurta, or bandhgala.

Q. Can I wear a bright-colored outfit on my engagement day?

A. Yes, you can wear a bright-colored outfit on your engagement day. However, it is essential to choose a color that matches the theme of the ceremony and your bride-to-be's outfit.

Q. Do I need to wear a turban on my engagement day?

A. While turban is a traditional headwear for Indian grooms, it is not mandatory to wear one on your engagement day. You can wear a turban if you want to, or you can skip it.

Q. Can I wear a fusion outfit on my engagement day?

A. Yes, you can wear a fusion outfit on your engagement day. However, it is essential to choose an outfit that matches the theme of the ceremony and your personality.

Conclusion of What to Wear on Engagement Indian Groom

Choosing the right outfit for your engagement day is essential as it sets the tone for your wedding journey. When deciding on what to wear on engagement Indian groom, keep in mind the time of the day, the theme of the ceremony, and your bride-to-be's outfit. Choose an outfit that is comfortable, stylish, and matches your personality. With these factors in mind, you will surely make a statement on your engagement day.
