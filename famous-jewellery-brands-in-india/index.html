Famous Jewellery Brands in India: A Guide to the Best

India is known for its rich cultural heritage, which includes its stunning jewellery designs. However, with so many famous jewellery brands in India, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. Whether you're shopping for a wedding, a special occasion, or just because, finding the perfect jewellery piece can be a challenge.

If you're looking for high-quality, beautiful jewellery, look no further than the top jewellery brands in India. These brands offer a wide range of jewellery pieces, from traditional to modern, and are known for their quality craftsmanship and attention to detail.

In this article, we'll explore the best famous jewellery brands in India and what sets them apart from the rest. From Tanishq to Kalyan Jewellers, we'll cover everything you need to know to make an informed decision about your next jewellery purchase.

Tanishq

Tanishq is one of the most popular and well-known jewellery brands in India. Known for its intricate designs and high-quality craftsmanship, Tanishq offers a wide range of jewellery pieces, including necklaces, earrings, bracelets, and rings. What sets Tanishq apart is its commitment to ethical sourcing and sustainable practices. The brand sources its gold from certified mines and ensures fair labour practices at every stage of production.

My personal experience with Tanishq has been nothing short of amazing. When I was shopping for my wedding jewellery, I knew I wanted something unique and special. The team at Tanishq worked with me to create a custom bridal set that was absolutely perfect. The attention to detail and quality of the finished product exceeded my expectations.

Kalyan Jewellers

Kalyan Jewellers is another one of the top jewellery brands in India. With over 140 showrooms across India and the Middle East, Kalyan Jewellers is known for its exquisite designs and exceptional customer service. The brand offers a wide range of jewellery pieces, from traditional to modern, and is committed to using only the highest quality materials.

When I was shopping for a special occasion, Kalyan Jewellers was my first choice. The team at the showroom was incredibly helpful and knowledgeable, and they helped me find the perfect piece to match my outfit. The quality of the jewellery was impeccable, and I received so many compliments on it throughout the evening.

Tribhovandas Bhimji Zaveri

Tribhovandas Bhimji Zaveri, or TBZ, is one of the oldest and most respected jewellery brands in India. With a history dating back to 1864, TBZ is known for its exquisite craftsmanship and attention to detail. The brand offers a wide range of jewellery pieces, from traditional to modern, and is committed to using only the highest quality materials.

When I was shopping for a gift for my mother's birthday, I knew that TBZ would be the perfect choice. The team at the showroom was incredibly helpful, and they helped me find a beautiful pendant that my mother absolutely loved. The quality of the jewellery was exceptional, and I knew that it would last for years to come.

PC Jeweller

PC Jeweller is one of the fastest-growing jewellery brands in India. With over 100 showrooms across the country, PC Jeweller offers a wide range of jewellery pieces, from traditional to modern. The brand is known for its exceptional customer service and commitment to quality.

When I was shopping for my sister's wedding, PC Jeweller was my go-to choice. The team at the showroom was incredibly helpful, and they helped me find the perfect bridal set that complemented my sister's outfit perfectly. The quality of the jewellery was exceptional, and my sister received so many compliments on her wedding day.
