How to Care for Your Boxing Gloves

Caring for your boxing gloves is essential if you want them to last longer. Here are some tips to help you care for your gloves:

  • After each training session, wipe down your gloves with a damp cloth to remove sweat and dirt.
  • Avoid leaving your gloves in a humid place as this can cause mold and mildew to grow.
  • Avoid exposing your gloves to direct sunlight as this can cause the material to fade and weaken.
  • Use deodorizing sprays or bags to keep your gloves smelling fresh.
