Q: How do I know if a vintage suit is authentic?

A: Look for labels or tags that indicate the decade the suit was made. Additionally, inspect the suit for signs of wear and tear that are consistent with the age of the suit.

Q: Can I alter a vintage suit to fit me better?

A: It's possible to alter a vintage suit to fit you better, but it's important to work with a tailor who has experience with vintage clothing. Additionally, be aware that altering a vintage suit may affect its value.
