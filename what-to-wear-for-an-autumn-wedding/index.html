What To Wear For An Autumn Wedding A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Sep 21, 2023 Â· 5 min read

As the leaves start to turn and the air gets crisper, autumn weddings become more popular. However, with the change in season comes a change in wardrobe. It can be difficult to determine what to wear for an autumn wedding, especially with unpredictable weather and varying dress codes. Don't worry, we've got you covered with this complete guide to autumn wedding attire.

The target of what to wear for an autumn wedding is to help you make the right wardrobe choices for a wedding during this season. You want to look stylish and appropriate while also being comfortable and weather-appropriate. Whether you're attending a casual outdoor wedding or a formal indoor affair, there are certain guidelines to follow when it comes to autumn wedding attire.

In summary, when deciding what to wear for an autumn wedding, you want to consider the venue, dress code, and weather. Opt for rich, warm colors and fabrics, and choose layers that can easily be added or removed depending on the temperature. Don't forget to accessorize appropriately, and dress for the occasion while still staying true to your personal style.

Colors and Fabrics for Autumn Weddings

When it comes to colors, think warm and rich. Deep reds, burnt oranges, and rich browns are all great options for an autumn wedding. As for fabrics, you want to choose materials that will keep you warm while still looking stylish. Wool, tweed, and velvet are all excellent choices for autumn wedding attire. Don't be afraid to mix and match textures for a unique and visually interesting look.

Personally, I attended an outdoor autumn wedding in a burgundy velvet dress with black tights and ankle boots. I added a faux fur stole for warmth and accessorized with statement earrings and a clutch. The velvet was both warm and luxurious, and the deep burgundy color was perfect for the season.

Casual Autumn Wedding Attire

For a more casual autumn wedding, you can have a little more fun with your outfit. A flowy midi dress in a warm color or a cozy sweater paired with a skirt and boots are both stylish and comfortable options. Don't be afraid to play with patterns and textures, and add a statement accessory like a scarf or hat for a touch of personality.

Personally, I attended a casual outdoor autumn wedding in a floral midi dress with ankle boots and a denim jacket. The dress was flowy and comfortable, and the denim jacket added a casual touch while also keeping me warm.

Formal Autumn Wedding Attire

For a formal autumn wedding, you'll want to opt for more classic and elegant attire. A long gown in a rich color or a sleek suit paired with a tie or bowtie are both appropriate choices. Don't forget to accessorize with statement jewelry and a clutch or small handbag.

Personally, I attended a formal indoor autumn wedding in a navy blue floor-length gown with silver jewelry and a small clutch. The gown was simple yet elegant, and the navy blue color was perfect for the season.

Footwear for Autumn Weddings

When it comes to footwear for autumn weddings, you want to choose shoes that are both stylish and practical. Ankle boots are a great option for both casual and formal weddings, and they can easily be dressed up or down. Closed-toe heels or flats are also appropriate, especially in rich colors or interesting textures.

Personally, I attended an outdoor autumn wedding in ankle boots with a low heel. The boots were comfortable and stylish, and the low heel was practical for walking on uneven terrain.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear white to an autumn wedding?

A: While it used to be taboo to wear white to a wedding, many modern brides are embracing non-traditional colors and styles. However, it's still best to avoid wearing white to a wedding unless the bride specifically requests it.

Q: Can I wear black to an autumn wedding?

A: Yes, you can definitely wear black to an autumn wedding. However, you'll want to make sure the rest of your outfit is appropriately festive and not too somber. Add a pop of color with your accessories or choose a dress with a fun pattern or texture.

Q: What should I wear to an outdoor autumn wedding?

A: For an outdoor autumn wedding, you'll want to dress in layers and choose footwear that can handle uneven terrain. A flowy dress or skirt paired with ankle boots and a denim or leather jacket is a stylish and practical option.

Q: What should I wear to a formal indoor autumn wedding?

A: For a formal indoor autumn wedding, you'll want to opt for a classic and elegant gown or suit. Choose rich colors and interesting textures, and don't forget to accessorize with statement jewelry and a clutch.

Conclusion of What to Wear for an Autumn Wedding

When it comes to what to wear for an autumn wedding, the most important thing is to choose an outfit that makes you feel comfortable and confident while also being appropriate for the occasion. Consider the venue, dress code, and weather, and choose colors and fabrics that are warm and rich. Don't be afraid to add layers and accessories to complete your look, and have fun with your outfit while still staying true to your personal style.
