2. Baldwin - Baldwin is a Kansas City-based clothing brand that offers a mix of casual and formal wear. Their style is modern and trendy, with an emphasis on denim. The prices are moderate, making it a great option for those who want quality clothing without breaking the bank.

3. ULAH - ULAH is a men's lifestyle store that offers clothing, accessories, and grooming products. Their style is contemporary and laid-back, with an emphasis on comfort. The prices are reasonable, and the staff is friendly and helpful.

Tips for Shopping for Men's Clothing in Kansas City

1. Know your style - Before you start shopping, have an idea of the types of clothing and styles you're looking for. This will help you narrow down your options and save time.

2. Set a budget - Men's clothing can be expensive, so it's important to set a budget before you start shopping. This will help you avoid overspending and regretting it later.

3. Try everything on - Don't assume that something won't fit or look good on you. Always try everything on before you buy it, and don't be afraid to ask for help or opinions.

4. Shop sales - Many stores offer sales and discounts throughout the year, so keep an eye out for these opportunities to save money.
