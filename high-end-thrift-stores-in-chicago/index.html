The Ultimate Guide To High End Thrift Stores In Chicago

Written by William Jun 01, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune on designer clothing and accessories? Do you want to find unique, one-of-a-kind pieces without breaking the bank? If so, high end thrift stores in Chicago might be just what you need. These stores offer a curated selection of gently used designer items at a fraction of the original price. Not only will you save money, but you'll also be doing your part for the environment by reducing waste and supporting sustainable fashion.

But where do you start? With so many high end thrift stores in Chicago, it can be overwhelming to know which ones to visit. You might also be wondering how to find the best deals and what to look for when shopping at these stores. In this article, we'll answer all your questions and provide you with the ultimate guide to high end thrift stores in Chicago.

Firstly, let's summarize the main points of this article. We'll cover the benefits of shopping at high end thrift stores, how to find the best deals, what to look for when shopping, and our top picks for the best high end thrift stores in Chicago.

Benefits of Shopping at High End Thrift Stores in Chicago

Shopping at high end thrift stores in Chicago has many benefits. Firstly, you'll be able to find unique, one-of-a-kind pieces that you won't find anywhere else. These stores often have a curated selection of designer items, so you can be sure that you're getting high quality pieces at a fraction of the original price.

Secondly, shopping at high end thrift stores is environmentally friendly. By buying secondhand clothing, you're reducing waste and supporting sustainable fashion. It's estimated that the fashion industry is responsible for 10% of global carbon emissions, so shopping secondhand is a great way to reduce your carbon footprint.

Finally, shopping at high end thrift stores is also a great way to save money. Designer clothing and accessories can be incredibly expensive, but by shopping secondhand, you can get these items for a fraction of the original price.

How to Find the Best Deals at High End Thrift Stores in Chicago

If you want to find the best deals at high end thrift stores in Chicago, there are a few tips to keep in mind. Firstly, try to shop during sales or clearance events. Many thrift stores have regular sales, and these can be a great opportunity to snag a bargain.

Secondly, don't be afraid to negotiate. Many thrift stores are open to negotiation, especially if an item has been on the rack for a while. It's always worth asking if you can get a discount.

Finally, try to visit thrift stores in upscale neighborhoods. These stores are more likely to have a selection of designer items, and you might be able to find some real gems.

What to Look for When Shopping at High End Thrift Stores in Chicago

When shopping at high end thrift stores in Chicago, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, check the condition of the item. Make sure there are no stains, holes, or other damage.

Secondly, check the label. Look for designer brands and high quality materials. Some thrift stores also have a separate section for designer items, so make sure to check there as well.

Finally, don't be afraid to try things on. Thrift store sizes can be unpredictable, so it's always best to try things on before you buy.

Top Picks for High End Thrift Stores in Chicago

Now that we've covered the basics, let's take a look at some of the best high end thrift stores in Chicago:

1. The Brown Elephant

The Brown Elephant is a Chicago institution, with multiple locations throughout the city. They offer a wide selection of clothing, accessories, and home goods, and all proceeds support the Howard Brown Health Center.

2. Luxury Garage Sale

With a name like Luxury Garage Sale, you know you're in for a treat. This boutique offers a curated selection of high end designer items, with a focus on current and vintage pieces.

3. The Salvation Army Family Store and Donation Center

The Salvation Army is a well-known thrift store chain, but their Chicago location is particularly noteworthy. They have a large selection of clothing, accessories, and home goods, and all proceeds support the organization's charitable efforts.

4. Crossroads Trading Co.

While not strictly a thrift store, Crossroads Trading Co. offers a similar experience. They buy and sell gently used clothing and accessories, with a focus on trendy, fashionable pieces.

Question and Answer

Q: Are high end thrift stores in Chicago expensive?

A: While high end thrift stores may be more expensive than traditional thrift stores, they are still significantly cheaper than buying new designer items. Plus, you can often find designer pieces for a fraction of the original price.

Q: Can I sell my clothing at high end thrift stores in Chicago?

A: Some high end thrift stores, such as Crossroads Trading Co., allow you to sell your gently used clothing and accessories for cash or store credit.

Q: Are high end thrift stores in Chicago sustainable?

A: Yes! Shopping secondhand is a great way to reduce waste and support sustainable fashion.

Q: Can I find vintage items at high end thrift stores in Chicago?

A: Yes! Many high end thrift stores have a selection of vintage items, including clothing, accessories, and home goods.

Conclusion of High End Thrift Stores in Chicago

High end thrift stores in Chicago offer a unique shopping experience with many benefits. You can find unique, one-of-a-kind pieces at a fraction of the original price, while also supporting sustainable fashion and reducing waste. By following our tips and visiting our top picks for the best high end thrift stores in Chicago, you'll be sure to find some amazing deals and treasures. Happy shopping!
