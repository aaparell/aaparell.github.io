The Fight of the Century: Muhammad Ali vs. Joe Frazier

The fight of the century between Muhammad Ali and Joe Frazier, held on March 8, 1971, at Madison Square Garden, is widely considered one of the most significant events in boxing history. It was the first time that two undefeated heavyweight champions had faced each other in the ring. The fight was dubbed "The Fight of the Century," and it lived up to its billing. The fight was intense, with both fighters displaying incredible skill and determination. In the end, Joe Frazier won the fight in a unanimous decision, ending Muhammad Ali's undefeated record.

The Thrilla in Manila: Muhammad Ali vs. Joe Frazier III

The Thrilla in Manila, held on October 1, 1975, was the third and final fight between Muhammad Ali and Joe Frazier. The fight was held in the Philippines and is widely considered one of the greatest fights in boxing history. The two boxers went back and forth for 14 rounds, and the fight was so intense that both fighters were on the verge of collapse by the end. Muhammad Ali won the fight in the 14th round when Joe Frazier's corner threw in the towel.

The Fight of the Millennium: Floyd Mayweather Jr. vs. Manny Pacquiao

The Fight of the Millennium, held on May 2, 2015, between Floyd Mayweather Jr. and Manny Pacquiao, was a highly anticipated event. The fight had been in the works for years, and it finally came to fruition in 2015. The fight was significant because it featured two of the best boxers in the world, and it was the most expensive pay-per-view event in history. Floyd Mayweather Jr. won the fight in a unanimous decision.

The Money Fight: Floyd Mayweather Jr. vs. Conor McGregor

The Money Fight, held on August 26, 2017, between Floyd Mayweather Jr. and Conor McGregor, was a unique event that brought together two of the biggest names in combat sports. The fight was primarily a boxing match, but it also featured elements of mixed martial arts. The fight was highly anticipated and generated a lot of buzz in the weeks leading up to the event. Floyd Mayweather Jr. won the fight in the 10th round via TKO.
