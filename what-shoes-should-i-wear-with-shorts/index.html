What Shoes Should I Wear With Shorts

Written by Oliver May 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Summer is here and it's time to rock your favorite shorts. But, what shoes should you wear with them? Choosing the right shoes can make or break your outfit. In this tutorial, we'll help you figure out what shoes to wear with shorts based on your style and comfort level.

Are you tired of feeling unsure about what shoes to pair with your shorts? Do you often find yourself changing your shoes multiple times before finding the perfect match? Are you looking for guidance on what shoes would look best with your shorts? If so, you're not alone. Many people struggle with this fashion dilemma.

When it comes to what shoes to wear with shorts, there's no one-size-fits-all answer. It all depends on your style, the occasion, and your personal comfort level. However, there are some general guidelines you can follow to help you make the right choice.

Overall, the key to pairing shoes with shorts is to find a balance between style and comfort. You want your shoes to complement your shorts, but you also want to be able to walk around in them all day without any discomfort. Here are some tips to help you choose the right shoes for your shorts:

The Classic Sneakers

When in doubt, you can never go wrong with classic sneakers. They are comfortable, versatile, and go well with any type of shorts. Whether you're wearing denim shorts or chino shorts, a pair of sneakers will give you a casual and stylish look. You can even wear them with your favorite t-shirt or polo shirt for a relaxed summer outfit.

Sandals and Flip Flops

If you're looking for something more casual and comfortable, sandals and flip flops are a great choice. They are perfect for the beach, pool, or any laid-back summer activity. Just make sure to choose a pair that fits well and provides enough support for your feet. Avoid wearing them if you plan on doing a lot of walking or if you're attending a formal event.

Loafers and Boat Shoes

If you're going for a preppy or nautical look, loafers and boat shoes are a great choice. They look best with chino shorts and a button-down shirt. They are also a good option for more formal events, such as outdoor weddings or garden parties. Just make sure to choose a pair that fits well and is comfortable to walk in.

Dress Shoes

If you're attending a formal event and want to wear shorts, dress shoes are your best bet. They look best with tailored shorts and a dress shirt. Avoid wearing them with denim or cargo shorts, as they are too casual for this type of shoe. Choose a pair of dress shoes that are comfortable to walk in, as you'll likely be on your feet for a while.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear high heels with shorts?

A: While it's technically possible, we don't recommend it. High heels and shorts can look awkward together and it's hard to find a comfortable pair that won't hurt your feet.

Q: Can I wear ankle boots with shorts?

A: Ankle boots can look great with shorts if you're going for a boho or edgy look. However, they may be too hot to wear during the summer months.

Q: Can I wear running shoes with shorts?

A: Running shoes are designed for exercise and not for fashion. While they may be comfortable, they don't look great with shorts. Stick to classic sneakers instead.

Q: Can I wear socks with sandals?

A: It's generally not recommended to wear socks with sandals, as it can look tacky. If you must wear socks, choose a pair that matches your shorts or shoes and keep them hidden.

Conclusion of What Shoes Should I Wear with Shorts

Choosing the right shoes to wear with shorts can be a challenge, but it doesn't have to be. By following these general guidelines, you can find the perfect pair of shoes to complement your shorts and your personal style. Remember to prioritize comfort and choose shoes that you can walk in for hours. With the right pair of shoes, you can rock any pair of shorts with confidence.
