Where to Find Indian Food in Dubai?

One of the best places to find Indian food in Dubai is the famous "Little India" in the Bur Dubai area. It is a small street filled with Indian restaurants and shops. You can find authentic Indian street food, like chaat and samosas, as well as traditional Indian curries and biryanis.

Another popular area to find Indian food is the Jumeirah Lakes Towers (JLT) area. It has a variety of Indian restaurants, from casual to fine dining.
