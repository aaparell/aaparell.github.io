Expensive Clothing Brands In Pakistan A Guide

Written by Christine May 15, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you someone who loves to dress up in the latest fashion trends, but finds it hard to keep up with the constantly changing styles and brands? Do you often wonder which are the most expensive clothing brands in Pakistan and whether they are worth the investment? If yes, then you've landed on the right page! In this guide, we'll take you through the most popular and expensive clothing brands in Pakistan and help you make an informed decision when it comes to your wardrobe.

Pakistani women are known for their love of fashion and style. However, finding the right clothing brand that ticks all the boxes can be quite a task. From affordability to quality, there are several factors that one needs to consider before making a purchase. Moreover, with the emergence of several new brands in the market, the competition has only heightened, making it even harder to choose the right one.

When it comes to expensive clothing brands in Pakistan, the target audience is usually the elite class or those who can afford to splurge on luxury clothing. These brands are known for their high-quality fabric, intricate embroidery, and unique designs that set them apart from the rest. However, it's important to note that not all expensive brands guarantee quality, and it's always best to do your research before making a purchase.

In this guide, we've rounded up some of the most expensive clothing brands in Pakistan that are worth investing in. From designer wear to luxury pret, we've covered it all. So, let's dive in!

1. Sana Safinaz

When it comes to luxury pret wear, Sana Safinaz is a name that needs no introduction. The brand was launched in 1989 by two friends, Sana Hashwani and Safinaz Muneer, and has since become a household name. The brand is known for its exquisite designs, premium quality fabric, and attention to detail. The price range for their pret wear starts from PKR 10,000 and goes up to PKR 100,000.

2. Elan

Elan is another popular name in the Pakistani fashion industry that is known for its luxurious bridal wear and formal wear. The brand was launched in 2006 by Khadijah Shah and has since become a go-to choice for women who want to make a statement with their clothing. Their bridal wear can cost anywhere from PKR 300,000 to PKR 1,000,000, while their formal wear starts from PKR 30,000.

3. Faraz Manan

Faraz Manan is a Lahore-based designer who has made a name for himself in the fashion industry with his luxurious designs and intricate embroidery. His clothing line is a favorite among celebrities and socialites and is known for its elegant and timeless designs. The price range for his clothing line starts from PKR 50,000 and goes up to PKR 300,000.

4. Maria B

Maria B is one of the leading fashion designers in Pakistan who is known for her versatile designs and use of vibrant colors. Her clothing line includes everything from casual wear to bridal wear and is a favorite among women of all ages. The price range for her clothing line starts from PKR 5,000 and can go up to PKR 200,000.

Question and Answer

Q1. Are expensive clothing brands in Pakistan worth the investment?

A. It depends on the brand and the quality of the clothing. While some expensive brands may offer premium quality fabric and unique designs, others may not be worth the investment. It's always best to do your research before making a purchase.

Q2. Are there any affordable clothing brands in Pakistan?

A. Yes, there are several affordable clothing brands in Pakistan that offer stylish and trendy clothing at reasonable prices. Some examples include Khaadi, Sapphire, and Gul Ahmed.

Q3. How can I determine the quality of expensive clothing brands in Pakistan?

A. The quality of clothing depends on several factors, including the type of fabric used, the stitching, and the finishing. It's always best to visit the store and check the clothing in person before making a purchase. You can also read reviews online and ask for recommendations from friends and family.

Q4. What are some tips for maintaining expensive clothing brands in Pakistan?

A. It's important to follow the care instructions provided by the brand to ensure that the clothing lasts longer. Avoid using harsh detergents or bleach and store the clothing in a cool and dry place. It's also recommended to get the clothing dry cleaned instead of washing it at home.

Conclusion of Expensive Clothing Brands in Pakistan

Investing in expensive clothing brands in Pakistan can be a daunting task, but with the right research and guidance, it can be a worthwhile investment. From Sana Safinaz to Maria B, there are several brands that offer premium quality fabric and unique designs that are worth the investment. However, it's important to keep in mind that not all expensive brands guarantee quality, and it's always best to do your research before making a purchase. We hope that this guide has been helpful in providing you with the information you need to make an informed decision when it comes to your wardrobe.
