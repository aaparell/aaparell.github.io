Best Collar Stays For Dress Shirts Keep Your Collars Looking Sharp

Written by Robby Nov 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your dress shirt collars looking sloppy and unkempt? Do you find yourself constantly adjusting and readjusting your collar throughout the day? You're not alone. Collar stays are a simple and effective solution to keep your collar looking sharp all day long.

Without collar stays, dress shirt collars can easily become misshapen and floppy. This can be especially frustrating for those who regularly wear dress shirts for work or formal occasions. Not only does it look unprofessional, but it can also be uncomfortable to wear. Collar stays are the perfect solution to this common problem.

So what are collar stays exactly? Collar stays are small, flat, and rigid strips that are inserted into the underside of a dress shirt collar. They provide structure and support to the collar, helping it maintain its shape throughout the day.

In this article, we'll explore the best collar stays for dress shirts and how they can help keep your collars looking sharp.

Best Collar Stays for Dress Shirts: Plastic, Metal, or Magnetic?

When it comes to collar stays, there are three main materials to choose from: plastic, metal, or magnetic. Each has its own advantages and disadvantages, so it's important to consider your individual needs and preferences when selecting the best collar stays for your dress shirts.

Personally, I prefer metal collar stays as they are durable, long-lasting, and provide the most support for my collars. However, plastic collar stays are a more affordable option and are great for those who frequently lose or misplace their collar stays. Magnetic collar stays are also a popular choice as they provide a strong hold and are easy to use.

How to Choose the Right Size Collar Stays for Your Dress Shirts

Choosing the right size collar stays is crucial to ensure they fit properly and provide enough support to your collar. The size of your collar stays should correspond to the size of your dress shirt collar. Most standard dress shirt collars require collar stays that are 2.5 to 3 inches in length. However, if you have a particularly large or small collar, you may need to adjust the size accordingly.

Additional Considerations When Choosing Collar Stays

Aside from material and size, there are a few other factors to consider when choosing the best collar stays for your dress shirts. Some collar stays come with rounded edges, which can help prevent damage to your shirt collar. Others may have a removable tip that can be replaced if it becomes damaged or lost.

How to Insert Collar Stays Into Your Dress Shirt

Inserting collar stays into your dress shirt is a simple process. Begin by flipping up your collar and locating the small slots on the underside of your collar. Insert the collar stays into the slots, making sure they are positioned evenly and parallel to each other. Once the collar stays are in place, you can adjust the collar to your desired position.

The Benefits of Using Collar Stays

Using collar stays can provide a number of benefits for those who wear dress shirts regularly. Not only do they help keep your collar looking neat and professional, but they can also improve your overall comfort. A well-supported collar will stay in place and prevent rubbing or irritation against your skin.

Conclusion of Best Collar Stays for Dress Shirts

Overall, collar stays are a simple and effective solution to keep your dress shirt collars looking sharp and professional. By choosing the right size and material, you can find the perfect collar stays to meet your individual needs and preferences. Say goodbye to sloppy, misshapen collars and hello to a polished and put-together look.

Question and Answer

Q: How many collar stays do I need for one dress shirt?

A: Typically, dress shirts have two slots for collar stays, so you will need two collar stays per shirt.

Q: Can I reuse collar stays?

A: Yes, collar stays can be reused multiple times as long as they are not damaged or bent out of shape.

Q: Can I wash my dress shirt with the collar stays still inserted?

A: It's not recommended to wash your dress shirt with the collar stays still inserted as this can damage the stays or the shirt. Remove the collar stays before washing and reinsert them once the shirt is dry.

Q: Can I use collar stays with a button-down collar?

A: While button-down collars do not typically have slots for collar stays, some collar stays can be attached to the buttons themselves to provide additional support.
