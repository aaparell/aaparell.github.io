Question and Answer

Q. Is it safe to buy gold jewellery online in India?

Q. What is the best way to check the authenticity of the gold?

A. The best way to check the authenticity of the gold is to look for the hallmark on the jewellery. The hallmark is a symbol that certifies the purity of the gold.
