Question and Answer

Q: What is the best genuine leather wallet brand?

Q: How can I tell if a leather wallet is genuine?

A: To tell if a leather wallet is genuine, look for markings or labels that indicate it is made from real leather. Genuine leather will also have a distinct smell and texture.
