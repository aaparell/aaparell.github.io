What Color Shoes To Wear With A Wedding Dress

Written by Robby Oct 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Weddings are one of the most important events in someone's life. Whether you're the bride, a bridesmaid, or a guest, it's essential to look your best. One of the most critical questions is what color shoes to wear with a wedding dress. The right shoes can make or break your entire outfit, so it's crucial to get it right.

Choosing the right color shoes to wear with a wedding dress can be a daunting task. Many factors come into play, such as the color of the dress, the theme of the wedding, and the time of day. You want to choose the right color shoes that will complement your dress and make you feel confident and comfortable.

The answer to what color shoes to wear with a wedding dress depends on the color of the dress. If the dress is white or ivory, you can wear any color shoes, including metallics like silver or gold. If the dress is a different color, you want to choose shoes that either match or complement the dress. For example, if the dress is blush, you can wear nude or champagne-colored shoes. If the dress is red, you can wear black or nude shoes.

In summary, when choosing what color shoes to wear with a wedding dress, consider the color of the dress, the theme of the wedding, and the time of day. If the dress is white or ivory, any color shoes are suitable. If the dress is a different color, choose shoes that either match or complement the dress.

Matching Shoes with a Wedding Dress

When I was a bridesmaid for my best friend's wedding, I had trouble choosing what color shoes to wear with my emerald green dress. After some research, I found that metallic shoes are a great option when you're unsure about what color shoes to wear. I ended up wearing silver shoes, and they looked great with my dress. Metallic shoes are versatile and can match any color dress.

Complementing Shoes with a Wedding Dress

When I attended my cousin's wedding, I wore a navy blue dress. I paired it with nude-colored shoes, and it complemented my dress perfectly. Nude-colored shoes are an excellent choice for dresses with bold colors because they don't clash with the dress and make your outfit look well put together.

Choosing Shoes Based on the Time of Day

The time of day can also play a significant role in what color shoes to wear with a wedding dress. For daytime weddings, choose shoes that are lighter in color, such as nude or pastel colors. For evening weddings, choose shoes that are darker in color, such as black or navy blue.

How to Choose the Right Shoe Style

The style of the shoe is also important when choosing what color shoes to wear with a wedding dress. If the wedding is outdoors, consider wearing wedges or flats. If the wedding is indoors, you can wear heels or flats. The type of shoe you choose should also reflect your personal style and comfort level.

Personal Experience with Choosing Shoes for a Wedding

When I was the maid of honor at my sister's wedding, I wore a light pink dress. I was torn between wearing black or nude shoes. In the end, I ended up wearing nude-colored shoes, and they looked great with my dress. It was a daytime wedding, so the lighter color shoes were the perfect choice.

Question and Answer

Q. Can you wear colorful shoes with a wedding dress?

A. Yes, you can wear colorful shoes with a wedding dress, but make sure the color complements or matches the dress.

Q. Can you wear open-toed shoes with a wedding dress?

A. Yes, you can wear open-toed shoes with a wedding dress, but make sure they're appropriate for the venue and the time of day.

Q. Can you wear flats with a wedding dress?

A. Yes, you can wear flats with a wedding dress, especially if the wedding is outdoors or if you're not comfortable wearing heels.

Q. Can you wear boots with a wedding dress?

A. It depends on the style of the dress and the theme of the wedding. If the wedding is outdoors or has a rustic theme, boots can be a great addition to your outfit.

Conclusion of What Color Shoes to Wear with a Wedding Dress

Choosing the right color shoes to wear with a wedding dress can seem overwhelming, but it doesn't have to be. Consider the color of the dress, the theme of the wedding, and the time of day when choosing shoes. Remember to choose shoes that either match or complement the dress, and reflect your personal style and comfort level. With these tips, you'll be able to choose the perfect shoes for any wedding event.
