Where To Buy Men s Dress Shoes A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Aug 04, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you in the market for a new pair of dress shoes? Whether you need them for a special event, a job interview, or just to elevate your style, finding the perfect pair can be a daunting task. With so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. But fear not, we've got you covered. In this guide, we'll walk you through some of the best places to buy men's dress shoes, so you can find the perfect pair with ease.

When it comes to buying dress shoes, there are a few pain points that many men experience. For one, it can be difficult to find a pair that is both stylish and comfortable. Additionally, dress shoes can often be quite expensive, making it tough to find a pair that fits within your budget. And of course, there's the issue of finding a store that carries shoes in your size and preferred style.

So where should you start your search for the perfect pair of dress shoes? The answer depends on your personal preferences, budget, and location. In this guide, we'll cover a variety of stores and retailers, both online and in-person, that offer a range of men's dress shoes to suit every need.

Department Stores

When it comes to finding a wide selection of men's dress shoes, department stores are a great place to start. Stores like Macy's, Nordstrom, and Bloomingdale's carry a range of brands and styles, from classic leather oxfords to modern suede loafers. These stores often run sales and promotions, making it easier to find a pair of shoes that fits within your budget.

Personally, I've had great luck finding dress shoes at Nordstrom. Their sales associates are knowledgeable and helpful, and they offer a wide range of sizes and styles.

Specialty Stores

If you're looking for a more curated selection of dress shoes, specialty stores might be the way to go. Stores like Allen Edmonds and Johnston & Murphy offer high-quality, handcrafted dress shoes that are built to last. While these shoes can be pricier than what you'd find at a department store, they are often worth the investment in terms of quality and longevity.

My personal favorite specialty store is Allen Edmonds. Their shoes are made in the USA and are known for their durability and classic style. Plus, they offer a range of widths and sizes to ensure a perfect fit.

Online Retailers

Shopping for dress shoes online might seem like a risky move, but there are plenty of reputable retailers out there that offer a great selection and easy returns. Zappos and Amazon are two popular options, and both offer a wide range of brands and styles at competitive prices.

One thing to keep in mind when shopping online is to read reviews carefully and pay attention to sizing charts. It's also a good idea to order multiple sizes or styles if you're unsure, as many retailers offer free returns.

Discount Stores

Finally, if you're on a tight budget, don't count out discount stores like T.J. Maxx and Marshalls. While their selection might be more limited than what you'd find at a department store or specialty store, they often offer great deals on high-quality dress shoes from well-known brands.

My personal experience at T.J. Maxx has been hit or miss, but I've found some great deals on dress shoes there in the past.

Question and Answer

Q: Are dress shoes only meant to be worn with suits?

A: While dress shoes are often paired with suits, they can also be worn with other types of dress pants, such as khakis or chinos. They can even be dressed down with jeans for a more casual look.

Q: What should I look for when trying on dress shoes?

A: It's important to pay attention to the fit of the shoe, as well as the quality of the materials and construction. Look for shoes that feel comfortable and supportive, with a sturdy sole and a snug (but not tight) fit.

Q: How much should I expect to spend on a pair of dress shoes?

A: The cost of dress shoes can vary widely depending on the brand, materials, and construction. While it's possible to find a decent pair for under $100, higher-quality shoes can cost upwards of $500 or more.

Q: How do I care for my dress shoes?

A: Proper care and maintenance is essential for keeping your dress shoes looking their best. Use a shoe horn to help preserve the shape of the shoe, and invest in a good quality shoe polish to keep the leather looking shiny and new. It's also a good idea to rotate your shoes and let them air out between wears.

Conclusion of Where to Buy Men's Dress Shoes

Whether you're in the market for a classic pair of leather oxfords or something a bit more modern, there are plenty of options out there when it comes to buying men's dress shoes. By considering your personal preferences, budget, and location, you can find the perfect pair to elevate your style and take your wardrobe to the next level.
