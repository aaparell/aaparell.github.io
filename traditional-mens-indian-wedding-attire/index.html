Traditional Men s Indian Wedding Attire A Guide

Written by Aprilia Nov 05, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Indian weddings are known for their grandeur and extravagance. From the decor to the food and clothing, everything is larger than life. One of the most important aspects of an Indian wedding is the attire, especially for the groom and his male family members. Traditional men's Indian wedding attire is not only beautiful but also rich in culture and symbolism. In this guide, we will take a closer look at the different elements of traditional men's Indian wedding attire and their significance.

While Indian weddings are a celebration of love and togetherness, choosing the right outfit can be a daunting task. Many factors come into play, such as the region, community, and personal preferences. Finding the perfect outfit that fits all these criteria can be stressful for the groom and his family.

So, what is the target of traditional men's Indian wedding attire? The aim is to create a look that is not only stylish but also culturally significant. The outfit should reflect the groom's personality and heritage while also adhering to the traditions of the wedding ceremony.

In this guide, we will discuss the main components of traditional men's Indian wedding attire, including the sherwani, dhoti, safa, and shoes. We will also look at the different styles and colors available, as well as some tips on how to choose the perfect outfit for your big day.

The Sherwani

The sherwani is a long coat-like garment that is typically worn over a kurta and churidar, which are loose-fitting pants. It is a popular choice for grooms and male family members at Indian weddings. The sherwani is usually made of silk, cotton, or linen, and is adorned with intricate embroidery, beadwork, and other embellishments.

My personal experience with the sherwani was amazing. I felt like royalty wearing it on my wedding day. The intricate embroidery and beadwork made me feel like a prince, and the comfortable fabric allowed me to move around freely during the ceremony and reception. It was the perfect mix of traditional and modern, and I received many compliments on my outfit.

The Dhoti

The dhoti is a rectangular piece of cloth that is typically worn as a lower garment. It is usually made of cotton or silk and is draped around the waist and legs. The dhoti is often paired with a kurta, which is a long shirt-like garment, and a dupatta, which is a long scarf worn around the neck or head.

My grandfather wore a dhoti on my wedding day, and it was a sight to behold. He looked regal in the simple yet elegant garment. The dhoti is a classic choice for Indian weddings and adds a touch of tradition to any outfit.

The Safa

The safa is a traditional turban that is worn by men at Indian weddings. It is usually made of cotton or silk and is wrapped around the head in a specific style. The safa is an important part of the groom's attire and is often matched to the color of the bride's outfit.

My father wore a safa at my wedding, and it looked great. The safa added a touch of elegance to his outfit and made him look even more distinguished. It was the perfect accessory to complete his traditional Indian wedding look.

The Shoes

The shoes are an often-overlooked part of traditional men's Indian wedding attire. However, they are just as important as the rest of the outfit. The shoes should be comfortable yet stylish and should match the color and style of the rest of the outfit.

My brother wore a pair of mojris, which are traditional Indian shoes, at his wedding. They were intricately embroidered and matched the color of his sherwani perfectly. He received many compliments on his shoes, and they added a touch of sophistication to his overall look.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the significance of traditional men's Indian wedding attire?

A: Traditional men's Indian wedding attire is significant because it reflects the groom's personality and heritage while also adhering to the traditions of the wedding ceremony. It is a celebration of culture and tradition.

Q: What are some popular colors for men's Indian wedding attire?

A: Red, gold, and cream are popular colors for men's Indian wedding attire. However, other colors such as blue, green, and black are also becoming more popular.

Q: Can men's Indian wedding attire be rented?

A: Yes, many stores offer rental services for men's Indian wedding attire. This is a great option for those who do not want to invest in a new outfit or who are attending multiple weddings.

Q: Can men's Indian wedding attire be customized?

A: Yes, many designers offer customized men's Indian wedding attire. This is a great option for those who want a unique outfit that reflects their personal style and preferences.

Conclusion of Traditional Men's Indian Wedding Attire

Traditional men's Indian wedding attire is not only beautiful but also rich in culture and tradition. It is a celebration of love, culture, and togetherness. Whether you choose a sherwani or a dhoti, a safa or a pair of mojris, make sure that your outfit reflects your personality and heritage. With so many styles, colors, and options available, finding the perfect outfit for your big day has never been easier.
