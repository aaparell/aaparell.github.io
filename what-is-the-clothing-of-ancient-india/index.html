Types of Ancient Indian Clothing

Ancient Indian clothing includes a wide range of garments, each with its own unique style and significance. Some of the most popular types of ancient Indian clothing include:

Saree

The saree is a long piece of fabric draped around the body, worn by women in India. It is a symbol of cultural identity and is worn on special occasions and festivals.

How to wear a saree?

Wearing a saree can be a daunting task for many, but with a little practice, it can be done easily. Firstly, the saree is draped around the waist and tucked in securely. The remaining fabric is then draped over the shoulder and pinned in place. The end result is a beautiful, flowing garment that is both elegant and practical.

Dhoti

The dhoti is a rectangular piece of cloth worn by men in India. It is worn by wrapping it around the waist and legs and tucking it in securely. It is a symbol of cultural identity and is worn on special occasions and festivals.

How to wear a dhoti?
