Best Best Designer Shorts Trend In 2023

Written by Petter May 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Summer is just around the corner and it's time to update your wardrobe with the best designer shorts. Whether you're going to the beach or running errands, a good pair of shorts can make all the difference.

Pain Points

When it comes to finding the best designer shorts, there are a few pain points that many people face. Some of these include:

  • Difficulty finding shorts that fit well
  • Limited options in stores
  • High prices for designer brands

Target of Best Designer Shorts

The target of best designer shorts is to provide comfortable, stylish shorts that fit well and are made of high-quality materials. Designer brands often offer unique and trendy designs that you won't find in regular stores, and they are made to last for several seasons.

In this article, we'll be discussing the best designer shorts for men and women, the benefits of investing in designer shorts, and how to style them for different occasions.

Benefits of Investing in Best Designer Shorts

Investing in the best designer shorts comes with several benefits:

  • High-quality materials that last longer
  • Unique and trendy designs
  • Better fit and comfort

While designer shorts may be pricier than regular shorts, they are often worth the investment in the long run.

Best Designer Shorts for Men and Women

Here are some of the best designer shorts for men and women:

Bermuda Shorts

Bermuda shorts are a classic style that are perfect for both men and women. They are typically knee-length and made of lightweight materials such as cotton or linen. Designer brands such as Ralph Lauren and Tommy Hilfiger offer a variety of Bermuda shorts in different colors and patterns.

Chino Shorts

Chino shorts are another popular style that are perfect for both casual and dressier occasions. They are typically made of cotton twill and have a more structured fit than Bermuda shorts. Designer brands such as J.Crew and Brooks Brothers offer a variety of chino shorts in different colors and fits.

Cargo Shorts

Cargo shorts are a more casual style that are perfect for outdoor activities such as hiking or camping. They typically have a looser fit and several pockets for storing essentials. Designer brands such as Patagonia and The North Face offer a variety of cargo shorts in different colors and materials.

Denim Shorts

Denim shorts are a classic style that never goes out of fashion. They are perfect for both men and women and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. Designer brands such as Levi's and Calvin Klein offer a variety of denim shorts in different washes and fits.

How to Style Best Designer Shorts

Here are some tips for styling the best designer shorts:

  • Pair Bermuda shorts with a polo shirt and boat shoes for a classic preppy look
  • Dress up chino shorts with a button-down shirt and loafers for a dressier occasion
  • Pair cargo shorts with a t-shirt and hiking boots for an outdoor adventure
  • Dress down denim shorts with a graphic t-shirt and sneakers for a casual look

Question and Answer

Q: How do I know if the shorts will fit me?

A: Many designer brands have a sizing chart on their website that you can use as a guide. It's also a good idea to read reviews from other customers to see if the shorts run true to size or if you should size up or down.

Q: Are designer shorts worth the investment?

A: Yes, investing in designer shorts is often worth it in the long run. They are made of high-quality materials and often have a better fit and comfort than regular shorts. Plus, they are often unique and trendy designs that you won't find in regular stores.

Q: How should I care for my designer shorts?

A: Always follow the care instructions on the label of your shorts. Most designer shorts should be washed in cold water and hung to dry to prevent shrinking or damage to the fabric.

Q: Can I wear designer shorts to work?

A: It depends on your workplace dress code. Bermuda or chino shorts may be appropriate for a casual workplace, but cargo or denim shorts are typically too casual for most workplaces.

Conclusion of Best Designer Shorts

In conclusion, investing in the best designer shorts is worth it for the high-quality materials, unique designs, and better fit and comfort. Whether you prefer Bermuda, chino, cargo, or denim shorts, there are plenty of options from designer brands such as Ralph Lauren, J.Crew, Patagonia, and Levi's. Just remember to follow the care instructions on the label to keep your designer shorts looking great for several seasons.
