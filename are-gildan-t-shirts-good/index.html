How Gildan T-Shirts are Made

Gildan t-shirts are made from a blend of cotton and polyester. This blend makes the t-shirts both soft and durable. The cotton provides a comfortable feel, while the polyester adds strength and durability to the fabric.

Gildan also uses a process called "ring-spun" when making their t-shirts. This process involves spinning the cotton fibers into a fine yarn, which is then woven into the fabric. The result is a softer and smoother fabric that feels great against the skin.

How to Care for Gildan T-Shirts

To ensure that your Gildan t-shirts last as long as possible, it's important to take care of them properly. Here are a few tips for caring for your Gildan t-shirts:

- Wash your t-shirts in cold water to prevent shrinking

- Avoid using bleach or fabric softener

- Dry your t-shirts on low heat or hang them to dry

- Store your t-shirts folded or hanging to prevent wrinkles
