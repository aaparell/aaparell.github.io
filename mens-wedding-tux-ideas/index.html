Mens Wedding Tux Ideas A Guide To Finding The Perfect Look

Written by Eveline May 13, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you getting ready for your big day but struggling to find the perfect tuxedo? With so many styles and options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. But don't worry, we've got you covered with some great mens wedding tux ideas that will help you look and feel your best on your special day.

Planning a wedding is a lot of work, and finding the right tuxedo is just one of the many tasks on your to-do list. You want to look your best, but you also want to feel comfortable and confident. It's important to find a tuxedo that fits your personal style and makes you feel like a million bucks.

So, where do you start? Let's take a look at some mens wedding tux ideas that will help you find the perfect look for your big day.

Classic Black Tuxedo

The classic black tuxedo is a timeless option that never goes out of style. It's a sleek and sophisticated look that will make you feel like James Bond. Pair it with a crisp white shirt and black bow tie for a classic look that will never disappoint.

When it comes to accessories, consider adding a pocket square or cufflinks to add a bit of personality to your look. And don't forget about shoes – a classic black leather shoe will complete the look perfectly.

Colored Tuxedo

If you're looking to add a bit of personality to your wedding day look, consider a colored tuxedo. Colors like navy or burgundy can add a pop of color to your look without being too over-the-top. Pair it with a white shirt and a complementary colored tie for a cohesive look.

When it comes to accessories, keep it simple with a pocket square or a tie clip. And don't be afraid to mix and match – a brown leather shoe can add a bit of texture to your look.

Tuxedo with a Twist

If you're looking for something a bit more unique, consider a tuxedo with a twist. This could mean a patterned jacket, a velvet blazer, or even a sequin jacket. Just make sure to keep the rest of your look simple – pair it with a white shirt and black pants for a cohesive look.

When it comes to accessories, keep it simple with a pocket square or cufflinks. And don't forget about shoes – a simple black leather shoe will complement the look perfectly.

Tuxedo Rental or Purchase?

When it comes to tuxedos, you have the option to rent or purchase. If you're only planning on wearing a tuxedo once, renting may be the best option for you. However, if you have multiple events coming up that require a tuxedo, it may be worth it to invest in purchasing one.

When purchasing a tuxedo, make sure to get it tailored to your body for the best fit. And don't forget about accessories – investing in a good pair of shoes and a classic tie or bow tie will complete the look.

The Bottom Line

When it comes to mens wedding tux ideas, there are plenty of options to choose from. Whether you opt for a classic black tuxedo or a colored tuxedo with a twist, make sure to choose a look that fits your personal style and makes you feel confident on your special day. And don't forget about accessories – they can make all the difference when it comes to pulling the look together.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a suit instead of a tuxedo for my wedding?

A: While a tuxedo is the traditional option for weddings, a suit can work just as well. Make sure to choose a suit that fits the formality of your wedding and pair it with a dress shirt and tie for a cohesive look.

Q: What accessories should I wear with my tuxedo?

A: Accessories like a pocket square, cufflinks, and a tie clip can add a bit of personality to your look. And don't forget about shoes – a classic black leather shoe will complete the look perfectly.

Q: How do I know if a tuxedo fits me properly?

A: A properly fitting tuxedo should fit snugly in the shoulders and chest, with the sleeves hitting at the wrist bone. The pants should sit at the waist and break slightly at the top of the shoe.

Q: Can I mix and match my tuxedo?

A: Absolutely! Mixing and matching can add a bit of personality to your look. Just make sure to keep it cohesive – pair a patterned jacket with solid pants, or a colored jacket with black pants.

Conclusion of Mens Wedding Tux Ideas

Choosing the right tuxedo for your wedding day is important – it's a day you'll remember for the rest of your life. With these mens wedding tux ideas, you'll be sure to find a look that fits your personal style and makes you feel confident on your special day. Whether you opt for a classic black tuxedo or a colored tuxedo with a twist, make sure to choose a look that makes you feel like a million bucks.
