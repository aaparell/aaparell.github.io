Which Clothing Brands Are Indian

Written by Robby Sep 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you want to support local businesses and buy clothes made in India? Are you tired of the same old international brands dominating the market? If so, you may be wondering which clothing brands are Indian and where to find them. In this blog post, we will explore the different options available to you and highlight some of the best Indian clothing brands on the market.

Many people are frustrated by the lack of variety and originality in the fashion industry. International brands tend to dominate the market, leaving little room for smaller, local businesses. This can be especially frustrating for those who want to support local communities and promote sustainable fashion. Additionally, some people may feel disconnected from international brands and prefer to wear clothes that reflect their cultural identity.

There are many Indian clothing brands that offer unique and stylish options. These brands often incorporate traditional Indian designs and materials into their clothing, creating a one-of-a-kind look. Additionally, by supporting these local businesses, you can help promote sustainable and ethical fashion practices.

In this article, we will highlight some of the best Indian clothing brands on the market. We will discuss their unique styles, materials, and price points. We will also explore the benefits of buying local and supporting sustainable fashion.

Indian Clothing Brands to Check Out

One of the best Indian clothing brands on the market is Fabindia. Fabindia offers a wide range of clothing options, from traditional kurtas to modern dresses. They use traditional Indian materials such as cotton, silk, and linen to create unique and stylish pieces. Their prices are reasonable, making them accessible to a wide range of customers.

Another great Indian clothing brand is Global Desi. Global Desi offers a more modern and contemporary take on traditional Indian clothing. They use bright colors, bold patterns, and unique designs to create a fun and eclectic look. Their clothes are perfect for those who want to add a little bit of Indian flair to their wardrobe.

The Benefits of Buying Local

There are many benefits to buying local and supporting small businesses. By buying from local brands, you can help promote sustainable and ethical fashion practices. Local businesses tend to have a smaller carbon footprint and are more likely to use sustainable materials and manufacturing processes.

Additionally, buying local helps support your community and promote economic growth. When you buy from small businesses, you are helping to create jobs and support local families. This can have a ripple effect throughout the community, leading to a stronger and more vibrant local economy.

How to Find Indian Clothing Brands

If you are interested in finding Indian clothing brands, there are several ways to do so. One option is to visit local markets and boutiques that specialize in Indian clothing. These shops often carry a wide range of traditional and modern clothing options.

You can also search online for Indian clothing brands. Many of these brands have websites and online stores where you can browse their collections and make purchases. Some popular online stores for Indian clothing include Utsav Fashion and Indian Cloth Store.

Conclusion of Which Clothing Brands are Indian

Overall, there are many Indian clothing brands on the market that offer unique and stylish options. By supporting these local businesses, you can help promote sustainable and ethical fashion practices while also promoting economic growth in your community. Whether you prefer traditional or modern designs, there is an Indian clothing brand out there for you.
