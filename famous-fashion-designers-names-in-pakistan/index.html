One of the most famous fashion designers in Pakistan is HSY, also known as Hassan Sheheryar Yasin. He is best known for his unique and extravagant bridal collections. HSY has also been featured in several fashion shows and has dressed many celebrities, including Angelina Jolie and Naomi Campbell.
