Brand value is perhaps the most important factor in determining the ranking of a luxury fashion brand. Popularity is another important factor in determining the ranking of a luxury fashion brand. Brands that are popular are those that are well-known and have a large following. Popularity can be measured by factors such as social media engagement, website traffic, and sales figures.

The quality of materials used in luxury fashion items is also an important factor in determining a brand's ranking. Luxury brands are typically known for using high-quality materials such as leather, silk, and cashmere. Brands that use inferior quality materials are unlikely to rank highly.
