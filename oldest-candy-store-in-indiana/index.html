How to Make Your Own Vintage Candies at Home

If you're feeling inspired by the oldest candy store in Indiana, why not try making some vintage candies at home? Here's a simple recipe for homemade peanut brittle:

Ingredients:

- 1 cup granulated sugar

- 1/2 cup light corn syrup

- 1/4 cup water

- 1 cup unsalted peanuts

- 2 tablespoons butter

- 1 teaspoon baking soda

- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:

1. Grease a baking sheet and set aside.

2. In a large saucepan, combine sugar, corn syrup, and water. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the sugar dissolves.

3. Add peanuts and stir until the mixture reaches 300 degrees F on a candy thermometer.

4. Remove from heat and stir in butter, baking soda, and vanilla extract.

5. Pour the mixture onto the prepared baking sheet and let cool.

6. Once cooled, break into pieces and enjoy!
