Discover The Best Fine Jewelry Brands In India

Written by Christine Aug 23, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you looking for high-quality fine jewelry that reflects the beauty and culture of India? With so many options available, it can be difficult to know where to start your search. However, choosing the right brand is essential to ensure that you get the best value for your money and a piece of jewelry that you will treasure for years to come.

In this article, we will explore the best fine jewelry brands in India and what makes them stand out from the crowd. Whether you're looking for traditional designs or modern styles, you're sure to find a brand that suits your needs and preferences.

Let's begin by taking a look at some of the most popular fine jewelry brands in India.

The Top Fine Jewelry Brands in India

As one of the world's largest producers of gold and precious stones, India has a rich history and culture of fine jewelry-making. From traditional Kundan and Polki designs to contemporary styles that combine traditional and modern elements, there is something for everyone.

Here are some of the top fine jewelry brands in India:

Tanishq

Tanishq is one of the most well-known fine jewelry brands in India, offering a wide range of gold and diamond jewelry. The brand is known for its high-quality craftsmanship and attention to detail, with each piece carefully designed to showcase the beauty of Indian culture.

One of the brand's most popular collections is the Rivaah range, which features traditional bridal jewelry with a modern twist. The collection includes intricate necklaces, earrings, and bangles that are perfect for any bride-to-be.

Kalyan Jewellers

Kalyan Jewellers is another popular fine jewelry brand in India, with a range of gold and diamond jewelry that is both elegant and timeless. The brand's designs are inspired by Indian culture and heritage, with each piece crafted to perfection.

One of the brand's most iconic collections is the Muhurat collection, which includes stunning bridal jewelry sets that are perfect for any wedding ceremony. The collection features intricate designs and exquisite craftsmanship, making it a must-have for any bride.

PC Jewellers

PC Jewellers is a well-known brand in India that offers a range of gold, diamond, and silver jewelry. The brand's designs are a blend of traditional and contemporary styles, with each piece crafted to perfection.

One of the brand's standout collections is the Amaze collection, which features modern designs that are perfect for everyday wear. The collection includes a range of earrings, rings, and pendants that are both stylish and affordable.

Tribhovandas Bhimji Zaveri (TBZ)

Tribhovandas Bhimji Zaveri (TBZ) is a brand that has been synonymous with fine jewelry in India for over 150 years. The brand is known for its exquisite craftsmanship and attention to detail, with each piece crafted to perfection.

One of the brand's most iconic collections is the Heritage collection, which features traditional Kundan and Polki designs that are perfect for any occasion. The collection includes a range of necklaces, earrings, and bangles that are sure to make a statement.
