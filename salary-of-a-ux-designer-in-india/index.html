Salary Of A Ux Designer In India A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Aug 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a UX designer in India wondering about your salary? Are you looking to enter the field and want to know what to expect? The salary of a UX designer in India can vary greatly depending on various factors such as experience, location, and industry. In this comprehensive guide, we will explore the average salary of a UX designer in India, factors that affect salary, and tips for negotiating your salary.

As a UX designer in India, you may face pain points related to salary. This could include not knowing what the industry standard is, feeling underpaid, or not knowing how to negotiate your salary. These pain points can leave you feeling frustrated and undervalued.

The target salary of a UX designer in India can vary depending on the factors mentioned earlier. According to Payscale, the average salary of a UX designer in India is around ₹600,000 per year. However, this can range from ₹300,000 to ₹1,500,000 per year depending on experience, location, and industry.

In summary, the salary of a UX designer in India can vary greatly depending on various factors such as experience, location, and industry. It is important to research the industry standard for your specific situation and negotiate your salary accordingly.

Factors That Affect Salary

One of the biggest factors that affect the salary of a UX designer in India is experience. The more experience you have, the more you can expect to earn. Location also plays a big role, with cities like Bangalore and Hyderabad offering higher salaries compared to smaller cities. Industry also affects salary, with finance and healthcare industries offering higher salaries compared to startups and non-profits.

For example, I have been a UX designer for 5 years and work for a healthcare company in Bangalore. My salary is around ₹1,200,000 per year. However, a colleague of mine with the same amount of experience working for a startup in Delhi makes around ₹600,000 per year.

Negotiating Your Salary

Negotiating your salary can be intimidating, but it is important to know your worth and advocate for yourself. Research the industry standard for your specific situation, and come prepared with data to support your salary request. Be confident and articulate when discussing your salary, and be willing to compromise if necessary.

When I was negotiating my salary for my current position, I researched the industry standard for a UX designer with my level of experience working in the healthcare industry in Bangalore. I came prepared with data to support my salary request, and was able to negotiate a salary that was higher than the initial offer.

Industry Standard Salaries

Here are some industry standard salaries for UX designers in India based on experience:

  • Entry-level (0-2 years of experience): ₹300,000-₹700,000 per year
  • Mid-level (2-5 years of experience): ₹700,000-₹1,200,000 per year
  • Senior-level (5+ years of experience): ₹1,200,000-₹1,500,000+ per year

Tips for Getting a Higher Salary

Here are some tips for getting a higher salary as a UX designer in India:

  • Gain more experience through internships or freelance work
  • Network with other UX designers in your industry
  • Continuously learn and improve your skills to make yourself more valuable

Q&A

Q: How much should I expect to earn as an entry-level UX designer in India?

A: Entry-level UX designers in India can expect to earn around ₹300,000-₹700,000 per year.

Q: What industries offer the highest salaries for UX designers in India?

A: Finance and healthcare industries offer the highest salaries for UX designers in India.

Q: How can I negotiate my salary as a UX designer in India?

A: Research the industry standard for your specific situation, come prepared with data to support your salary request, and be confident and articulate when discussing your salary.

Q: How can I make myself more valuable as a UX designer in India?

A: Gain more experience through internships or freelance work, network with other UX designers in your industry, and continuously learn and improve your skills.

Conclusion of Salary of a UX Designer in India

The salary of a UX designer in India can vary greatly depending on various factors such as experience, location, and industry. It is important to research the industry standard for your specific situation and negotiate your salary accordingly. By gaining more experience, networking, and continuously improving your skills, you can make yourself more valuable and increase your chances of earning a higher salary.
