1. Ike's Food & Cocktails

If you are looking for a fun and lively atmosphere, Ike's Food & Cocktails is the perfect place to go. This trendy and upscale restaurant offers a wide selection of delicious food and handcrafted cocktails. With its retro decor and lively music, Ike's Food & Cocktails is a great place to hang out with friends or celebrate a special occasion.

When it comes to food, Ike's Food & Cocktails does not disappoint. Their menu features classic American dishes with a modern twist. Some of the most popular items on the menu include the Ike's Burger, the Chicken and Waffles, and the Wild Mushroom Flatbread. Additionally, their drink menu is extensive, featuring a wide selection of beers, wines, and cocktails.

2. The Bloomington ChopHouse

If you are a steak lover, The Bloomington ChopHouse is the perfect place to go. This upscale restaurant offers some of the best steaks in town, along with a wide selection of other delicious dishes. With its elegant decor and attentive service, The Bloomington ChopHouse is the perfect place for a romantic dinner or a special occasion.

When it comes to food, The Bloomington ChopHouse is known for its premium steaks. Their menu features a variety of cuts, including filet mignon, New York strip, and ribeye. Additionally, they offer a selection of seafood dishes, such as lobster tail and seared scallops. To top it all off, they have an extensive wine list, featuring some of the best wines from around the world.

3. Masu Sushi & Robata

If you are a sushi lover, Masu Sushi & Robata is a must-visit. This trendy and modern restaurant offers some of the best sushi in town, along with a variety of other Japanese dishes. With its sleek decor and lively atmosphere, Masu Sushi & Robata is a great place to go with friends or for a fun night out.

When it comes to food, Masu Sushi & Robata is known for its high-quality sushi and sashimi. They offer a wide selection of rolls, including classic favorites and creative new combinations. Additionally, they have a variety of other Japanese dishes, such as ramen, udon, and robata-grilled meats and vegetables.

4. Fat Lorenzos

If you are looking for a casual and laid-back atmosphere, Fat Lorenzos is the perfect place to go. This cozy pizza joint offers some of the best pizza in town, along with a variety of other comfort foods. With its friendly service and retro decor, Fat Lorenzos is a great place to go for a quick bite or a casual dinner with friends.

When it comes to food, Fat Lorenzos is known for its delicious pizza. They offer a variety of classic and creative toppings, such as pepperoni, sausage, mushrooms, and even mac and cheese. Additionally, they have a variety of other comfort foods, such as burgers, sandwiches, and wings.
