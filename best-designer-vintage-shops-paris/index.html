Discover The Best Designer Vintage Shops In Paris

Written by Eveline Oct 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a fashion lover looking for unique and timeless pieces? Do you want to explore vintage shops in Paris but don't know where to start? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you through the best designer vintage shops Paris has to offer.

Paris is known as the fashion capital of the world, and it's no surprise that it has some of the best designer vintage shops. However, finding the right shop can be overwhelming and time-consuming, especially if you are not familiar with the city's layout or language.

If you are looking for the best designer vintage shops Paris has to offer, you've come to the right place. We have carefully curated a list of the most renowned and unique vintage shops that are worth visiting.

Chanel

If you are a fan of Chanel, make sure to visit Didier Ludot. This vintage shop has a vast collection of vintage Chanel pieces, including bags, shoes, and clothes. You can find anything from classic tweed jackets to signature chain bags. The shop is located in Palais Royal and has been frequented by celebrities and fashionistas.

"I visited Didier Ludot last summer and was blown away by the collection of vintage Chanel pieces. I ended up buying a unique tweed jacket that I get compliments on every time I wear it. The staff was friendly and helpful, and the shop had a great atmosphere."

Hermès

If you are looking for vintage Hermès pieces, head to Hermès Vintage. This shop has a wide range of vintage Hermès bags, scarves, and accessories. The shop is located in the trendy Marais district and has a chic interior that adds to the shopping experience.

"I stumbled upon Hermès Vintage during a walk in Marais and was amazed by the collection of vintage Hermès bags. The staff was knowledgeable and helped me pick the perfect bag that suited my style and budget."

Yves Saint Laurent

If you are a fan of Yves Saint Laurent, make sure to visit La Demeure des Couturiers. This shop has a vast collection of vintage Yves Saint Laurent pieces, including iconic dresses and suits. The shop is located in the affluent 16th arrondissement and has a cozy atmosphere that makes shopping a pleasure.

"I visited La Demeure des Couturiers during my last trip to Paris and was impressed by the collection of vintage Yves Saint Laurent pieces. The staff was friendly and helped me find a unique dress that I wore to a special occasion. The shop had a great ambiance, and I enjoyed exploring the different pieces."

How to Shop for Designer Vintage in Paris

Shopping for designer vintage in Paris can be a daunting task, but with the right approach, it can be an enjoyable and rewarding experience. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Research: Before your trip, research the best vintage shops in Paris and make a list of the ones you want to visit.
  • Location: Make sure to plan your route and group shops that are in the same area to save time.
  • Budget: Set a budget for your shopping trip and stick to it.
  • Familiarize yourself with the brand: If you are looking for a specific brand, do some research on the brand's history and signature pieces to help you make an informed purchase.
  • Try on the clothes: Vintage pieces can have different sizing, so make sure to try on the clothes before purchasing.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I bargain for the price of vintage items in Paris?

A: It depends on the shop and the item. Some shops have a fixed price policy, while others are open to negotiation. It's best to ask the staff if bargaining is possible.

Q: Are the vintage items in Paris authentic?

A: Most vintage shops in Paris have a strict policy on authenticity and quality. However, it's always best to do your research and check the item's authenticity before purchasing.

Q: Do vintage shops in Paris have a return policy?

A: It depends on the shop. Some shops have a strict no-return policy, while others offer exchanges or store credit. It's best to ask the staff about the shop's return policy before purchasing.

Q: Can I find vintage items from different brands in one shop?

A: Yes, many vintage shops in Paris offer a variety of brands and designers. It's best to research the shop's collection before visiting to make sure they have the brands you are interested in.

Conclusion of Best Designer Vintage Shops Paris

Paris has some of the best designer vintage shops in the world, and exploring them can be a fun and exciting experience. By following our guide and tips, you can discover unique and timeless pieces that will elevate your style game. Happy shopping!
