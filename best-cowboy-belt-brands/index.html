Best Cowboy Belt Brands You Need To Know About

Written by Oliver Jun 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a cowboy who loves to dress up in western style? If yes, then you know how important a cowboy belt is for your attire. A cowboy belt not only holds up your pants but also adds a touch of style to your overall look. But with so many brands out there, it can be overwhelming to choose the best one. That’s why we’ve put together this guide to help you find the best cowboy belt brands.

When it comes to cowboy belts, there are a few pain points to consider. Firstly, the quality of the leather used in the belt is crucial. You want a belt that is made of high-quality leather so that it lasts longer and doesn't wear out easily. Secondly, the buckle of the belt should be sturdy and durable to hold up to everyday wear and tear. Lastly, you want a belt that suits your style and personality.

The target of this guide is to provide you with a list of the best cowboy belt brands that meet all the pain points mentioned above. We've researched and tested various brands to bring you the most reliable and stylish cowboy belts in the market.

In summary, this guide will help you choose the best cowboy belt brand that meets your needs in terms of quality, durability, and style.

Ariat Cowboy Belts

One of the best cowboy belt brands in the market is Ariat. I have been using Ariat cowboy belts for years, and I can vouch for their quality and durability. The brand uses high-quality leather to make their belts, which means they last longer than other brands. The buckle of the belt is also made of sturdy material that can withstand everyday wear and tear. The best part is that Ariat offers a wide range of styles to choose from, so you're sure to find one that suits your personality.

Justin Cowboy Belts

Another great brand for cowboy belts is Justin. Justin cowboy belts are known for their high-quality leather and stylish designs. They offer a range of styles, from classic to modern, so you're sure to find one that fits your style. The buckle of the belt is also made of durable material that can hold up to everyday use. I've used Justin belts in the past, and I can say they are one of the most reliable brands in the market.

Wrangler Cowboy Belts

If you're looking for a more affordable option, then Wrangler cowboy belts are an excellent choice. The brand offers a range of styles, from classic to modern, and uses high-quality leather to make their belts. The buckle of the belt is also made of sturdy material that can hold up to everyday use. Wrangler cowboy belts are a great option for those who want a reliable belt without breaking the bank.

Tony Lama Cowboy Belts

Tony Lama is another great brand for cowboy belts. The brand is known for its high-quality leather and stylish designs. They offer a range of styles, from classic to modern, and their belts are durable enough to last for years. The buckle of the belt is also made of sturdy material that can hold up to everyday use. Tony Lama cowboy belts are a bit pricier than other brands, but they are worth the investment if you want a reliable and stylish belt.

Cowboy Belt Brands Q&A

Q: How do I know if a cowboy belt is made of high-quality leather?
A: Check the texture and smell of the leather. High-quality leather should have a smooth texture and a natural smell. Q: Can I wear a cowboy belt with any outfit?
A: Cowboy belts are best suited for western-style outfits. They may not look good with formal or casual wear. Q: How do I know what size cowboy belt to buy?
A: Measure your waist and add two inches to get the correct size. For example, if your waist measures 34 inches, then you should buy a size 36 cowboy belt. Q: How do I clean my cowboy belt?
A: Use a soft brush to remove dirt and dust from the belt. You can also use a leather cleaner to remove stains and polish the belt.

Conclusion of Best Cowboy Belt Brands

Choosing the right cowboy belt brand is crucial to complete your western-style attire. We hope this guide has helped you narrow down your choices and find the best cowboy belt brand for your needs. Remember to consider the quality of the leather, the durability of the buckle, and the style of the belt when making your decision. Happy shopping!
