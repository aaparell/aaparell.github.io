What Shoes To Wear With Dresses Besides Heels A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Oct 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of wearing heels with your dresses but don't know what other shoes to wear? Do you feel uncomfortable or awkward in heels but still want to look stylish in your outfits? If you answered yes to either of these questions, then this guide is for you.

There are many reasons why someone might not want to wear heels with dresses. They could be uncomfortable, difficult to walk in, or simply not their personal style. Whatever the reason, there are plenty of other shoe options that can elevate your outfit without sacrificing comfort or style.

So, what shoes should you wear with dresses besides heels? Let's explore some options.

Loafers and Oxfords

Loafers and oxfords are a great alternative to heels for a more polished look. They are comfortable, stylish, and versatile enough to pair with a variety of dress styles. For a more casual look, opt for a pair of loafers in a fun color or print. For a more formal occasion, choose a classic black or brown oxford.

Flats and Ballet Pumps

Flats and ballet pumps are another great option for those who want to avoid heels. They are comfortable and easy to wear, yet still look chic and put-together. Pair them with a summer dress or a midi skirt for a feminine look.

Sneakers

If you want to add a touch of edge to your dresses, try pairing them with sneakers. This unexpected combination is fun and playful, and perfect for a casual day out. Choose a pair of white sneakers for a classic look, or go for something bold and colorful to make a statement.

Ankle Boots

Ankle boots are a great option for those who want something a little more substantial than flats or sneakers. They are versatile enough to wear with a variety of dress styles, from maxi dresses to mini dresses. Choose a pair with a chunky heel for added height, or go for a flat style for maximum comfort.

Mules and Sandals

Mules and sandals are perfect for warmer weather, and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. Choose a pair of heeled mules for a more formal look, or opt for a flat sandal for a more casual vibe. These shoe styles pair perfectly with maxi dresses or midi skirts.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear boots with a dress?

A: Yes, ankle boots are a great option for pairing with dresses of all lengths.

Q: Can I wear sneakers with a maxi dress?

A: Yes, pairing sneakers with a maxi dress is a great way to add a touch of edge to your outfit.

Q: What kind of flats should I wear with a dress?

A: Ballet pumps or loafers are a great option for pairing with dresses.

Q: Can I wear sandals with a formal dress?

A: Yes, heeled sandals can be a great option for dressing up a formal dress.

Conclusion of What Shoes to Wear with Dresses Besides Heels

As you can see, there are plenty of shoe options to wear with dresses besides heels. From loafers to sandals, there is a shoe style for every occasion and personal style. Don't be afraid to experiment with different shoe styles and dress lengths to find your perfect outfit combination.
