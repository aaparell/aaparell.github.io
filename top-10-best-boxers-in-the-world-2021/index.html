Top 10 Best Boxers In The World 2021

Written by Eveline Jun 16, 2023 · 5 min read

Boxing is one of the most exciting and engaging sports in the world. The thrill of seeing two highly trained athletes battle it out in the ring is unmatched. However, with so many talented boxers out there, it can be difficult to determine who the best of the best are. In this post, we will be discussing the top 10 best boxers in the world 2021 and why they deserve to be on this list.

Boxing fans all over the world are always looking for the best fighters to follow, support and cheer on. Knowing who the top 10 best boxers in the world 2021 are is important for fans, as well as for those who are looking to place bets on matches or simply want to stay informed about the sport.

So, who are the top 10 best boxers in the world 2021? Let's take a closer look at each of them and what makes them so great.

Floyd Mayweather Jr.

As one of the greatest boxers of all time, Floyd Mayweather Jr. is a name that almost everyone recognizes. With a record of 50-0, he has never lost a professional fight. Mayweather is known for his exceptional defense and counterpunching skills, as well as his ability to adapt to any opponent's style. He is also a master of financial management, having earned over $1 billion in his career.

Vasyl Lomachenko

Vasyl Lomachenko is considered by many to be one of the greatest amateur boxers of all time. He won two Olympic gold medals before turning professional in 2013. Since then, he has become a three-weight world champion and has a record of 14-2. Lomachenko is known for his incredible footwork, hand speed, and ring IQ.

Canelo Alvarez

Saul 'Canelo' Alvarez is a Mexican boxer who has won world titles in four different weight classes. With a record of 55-1-2, he is considered one of the best pound-for-pound fighters in the world. Alvarez is known for his powerful punches, excellent defense, and his ability to take on tough opponents.

Terence Crawford

Terence Crawford is a former undisputed light-welterweight champion who has now moved up to welterweight. With a record of 37-0, he is known for his impressive knockout power and his ability to switch between orthodox and southpaw stances seamlessly. Crawford is also considered one of the best switch-hitters in boxing history.

Anthony Joshua

Anthony Joshua is a British boxer who is currently the unified world heavyweight champion. With a record of 24-1, he is known for his incredible punching power and his ability to knock out opponents with a single blow. Joshua is also a skilled boxer who can fight effectively both on the inside and outside.

Errol Spence Jr.

Errol Spence Jr. is an American boxer who is the current IBF and WBC welterweight champion. With a record of 27-0, he is known for his excellent body punching and his ability to wear down opponents. Spence Jr. is also a southpaw fighter, which gives him an advantage over most orthodox fighters.

Tyson Fury

Tyson Fury is a British boxer who is the current WBC heavyweight champion. With a record of 30-0-1, he is known for his incredible size and reach, as well as his excellent boxing skills. Fury is also known for his unorthodox fighting style and his ability to improvise in the ring.

Naoya Inoue

Naoya Inoue is a Japanese boxer who is the current WBA and IBF bantamweight champion. With a record of 20-0, he is known for his exceptional punching power and his ability to knock out opponents with either hand. Inoue is also a skilled counterpuncher and a master of distance control.

Deontay Wilder

Deontay Wilder is an American boxer who is a former WBC heavyweight champion. With a record of 42-1-1, he is known for his incredible punching power, having knocked out 41 of his opponents. Wilder is also a tall and rangy fighter, which gives him an advantage over most opponents.

Manny Pacquiao

Manny Pacquiao is a Filipino boxer who is considered one of the greatest boxers of all time. With a record of 62-7-2, he has won world titles in eight different weight classes. Pacquiao is known for his incredible speed and footwork, as well as his ability to change angles in the ring.

Conclusion of Top 10 Best Boxers in the World 2021

Boxing has produced some of the most exciting and talented athletes in the world, and the top 10 best boxers in the world 2021 are no exception. These fighters have proven themselves time and time again, and they continue to inspire new generations of boxing fans. Whether you are a lifelong fan of the sport or are just starting to get into it, these boxers are definitely worth following.
