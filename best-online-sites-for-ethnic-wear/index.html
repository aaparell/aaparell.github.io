1. Myntra

As one of the leading e-commerce sites in India, Myntra provides an extensive range of ethnic wear for men, women, and children. They offer a wide range of products, from sarees and kurtas to lehengas and salwar suits. Myntra also features numerous brands, including top designers, to cater to all budgets and styles.

Personally, I have shopped from Myntra multiple times, and their customer service is exceptional. They offer free shipping on orders above a certain amount and have easy returns and exchange policies. However, some products can be overpriced, and the quality may not be up to the mark.
