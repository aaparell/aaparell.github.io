Which Brand Shirts Are Best In India

Written by Luffy Jul 21, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of buying shirts that don't fit well or don't last long? Do you struggle to find the perfect brand for your style and budget? Look no further, as we have researched and compiled a list of the best brand shirts in India.

When it comes to buying shirts, there are a few pain points to consider. Quality, fit, style, and price are all factors that come into play. You want a shirt that looks good, feels comfortable, and lasts long without breaking the bank. With so many brands available in India, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one.

The answer to the question of which brand shirts are best in India is subjective and depends on personal preferences. However, through our research and analysis, we have found that the top brands for shirts in India are Van Heusen, Arrow, Louis Philippe, Peter England, and Raymond.

These brands offer a wide range of styles and fits, from formal to casual wear, and cater to different price ranges. They are known for their high-quality fabrics, durability, and attention to detail. Moreover, they have a strong presence in the Indian market and are easily accessible both in-store and online.

Van Heusen

I have personally tried Van Heusen shirts and can attest to their quality and comfort. The brand offers a variety of fits, including slim, regular, and comfort, and has a wide range of colors and patterns to choose from. Their shirts are perfect for formal occasions and business meetings, and the brand also offers a collection of casual wear.

Arrow

Arrow is another brand that offers high-quality shirts for men. They have a reputation for their attention to detail, and their shirts are made with premium fabrics that are comfortable and durable. Arrow shirts are perfect for formal wear and office settings, and the brand also has a collection of casual wear for everyday use.

Louis Philippe

Louis Philippe is a premium brand that caters to men's formal wear. Their shirts are made with high-quality fabrics and have a classic, elegant style. The brand offers a variety of fits, including slim, regular, and comfort, and has a range of colors and patterns to choose from. Their shirts are perfect for special occasions and business meetings.

Peter England

Peter England is a popular brand that offers affordable shirts for men. They have a wide range of styles and fits, from formal to casual wear, and cater to different price ranges. Their shirts are made with good quality fabrics and are perfect for everyday use.

Raymond

Raymond is a well-known brand that offers premium shirts for men. Their shirts are made with high-quality fabrics and have a classic, timeless style. The brand offers a variety of fits, including slim, regular, and comfort, and has a range of colors and patterns to choose from. Their shirts are perfect for special occasions and business meetings.

Question and Answer

Q. Which brand is best for formal wear?

A. Louis Phillipe and Raymond are the best brands for formal wear, as they offer premium quality shirts with classic, elegant styles.

Q. Which brand is best for casual wear?

A. Van Heusen and Arrow are great brands for casual wear, as they offer a variety of styles and fits that are comfortable and stylish.

Q. Which brand is best for budget-friendly shirts?

A. Peter England is the best brand for budget-friendly shirts, as they offer good quality shirts at affordable prices.

Q. Which brand has the best range of colors and patterns?

A. Van Heusen and Louis Phillipe have the best range of colors and patterns to choose from, catering to different styles and preferences.

Conclusion of Which Brand Shirts are Best in India

In conclusion, when it comes to buying shirts in India, it's important to choose a brand that offers quality, comfort, style, and affordability. Van Heusen, Arrow, Louis Philippe, Peter England, and Raymond are the top brands for shirts in India, catering to different styles and budgets. By choosing one of these brands, you can ensure that you get a shirt that fits well, feels comfortable, and lasts long.
