Lacoste Classic Fit Vs Regular Fit Understanding The Difference

Written by Daniel Aug 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you in the market for a Lacoste polo shirt but unsure of which fit to choose? The Classic Fit and Regular Fit may seem similar, but there are distinct differences that could impact your comfort and style. In this article, we’ll break down the differences between these two fits, their target audiences, and provide tips for choosing the right one for you.

When it comes to buying a Lacoste polo shirt, some pain points may arise. You might be worried about the fit feeling too tight or too loose, or you might be unsure if the shirt will look flattering on your body type. These are valid concerns, as finding the right fit is crucial for both your comfort and style.

The Lacoste Classic Fit is designed for those who prefer a more relaxed and comfortable fit. It features a slightly looser cut that allows for greater movement and breathability. On the other hand, the Regular Fit is more form-fitting and tailored, offering a sleeker and more polished appearance.

In summary, the Classic Fit is best suited for those who prioritize comfort and mobility, while the Regular Fit is ideal for those who want a more tailored and sophisticated look.

The Classic Fit

When I first tried on a Lacoste Classic Fit polo shirt, I was immediately struck by how comfortable it felt. The looser cut allowed for greater movement and breathability, making it perfect for casual wear or outdoor activities. The Classic Fit is also a great option for those with a larger build, as it can help to conceal any problem areas.

The Regular Fit

When I opted for a Lacoste Regular Fit polo shirt, I was impressed by how polished and tailored it looked. The closer cut hugged my body in all the right places, giving me a more put-together appearance. The Regular Fit is a great option for those who want to dress up their polo shirt for more formal occasions or simply prefer a sleeker silhouette.

Choosing the Right Fit

When choosing between the Classic Fit and Regular Fit, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, consider your body type and personal style. If you prefer a more relaxed and comfortable fit, the Classic Fit is likely the best choice for you. If you want a more tailored and sophisticated look, the Regular Fit is the way to go.

Secondly, consider the occasion. If you’re wearing the polo shirt for a casual outing, the Classic Fit will be more appropriate. If you’re dressing up for a wedding or formal event, the Regular Fit will be a better fit.

Tips for Sizing

Regardless of which fit you choose, it’s important to ensure that you’re selecting the right size. Take your measurements and refer to the Lacoste size chart to find your perfect fit. Keep in mind that Lacoste polo shirts tend to run slightly small, so you may need to size up for optimal comfort.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can the Classic Fit be dressed up for formal occasions?

A: While the Classic Fit is more casual in appearance, it can still be dressed up with the right accessories and styling. Consider pairing it with a blazer or dress pants for a more elevated look.

Q: Are there any differences in fabric between the Classic Fit and Regular Fit?

A: No, the fabric used in both fits is the same. The difference lies in the cut and silhouette.

Q: What body type is best suited for the Regular Fit?

A: The Regular Fit is best suited for those with a slimmer build, as it is more form-fitting and tailored.

Q: Can the Classic Fit be tucked in?

A: Yes, the Classic Fit can be tucked in for a more polished appearance. However, keep in mind that the looser cut may result in a less streamlined look.

Conclusion of Lacoste Classic Fit vs Regular Fit

Choosing between the Lacoste Classic Fit and Regular Fit ultimately comes down to personal preference and occasion. Consider your body type, style, and event before making your decision. Regardless of which fit you choose, make sure to select the right size for optimal comfort and style.
