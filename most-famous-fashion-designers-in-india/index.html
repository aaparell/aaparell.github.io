Who are the most famous fashion designers in India? The most famous fashion designers in India are Manish Malhotra, Sabyasachi Mukherjee, Ritu Kumar, Tarun Tahiliani, and Rohit Bal. Manish Malhotra is one of the most famous fashion designers in India. He is known for his glamorous and sophisticated designs, which are loved by Bollywood celebrities and fashion enthusiasts alike. Manish Malhotra's designs are a fusion of traditional and modern styles, making them perfect for any occasion. His collections include everything from sarees and lehengas to dresses and gowns.

Ritu Kumar is a pioneer of Indian fashion design. She is known for her use of traditional techniques and materials, which she combines with contemporary styles to create unique and beautiful designs. Ritu Kumar's collections include sarees, dresses, and bridal wear.

Tarun Tahiliani
