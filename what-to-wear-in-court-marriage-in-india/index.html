What To Wear In Court Marriage In India A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Jun 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Getting married is one of the most important events in a person's life. And if you are planning to get married in court, you might be wondering what to wear. Court marriages in India are becoming increasingly popular, and it's important to make sure you dress appropriately for the occasion. In this post, we will provide a complete guide on what to wear in court marriage in India.

The Pain Points of What to Wear in Court Marriage in India

Choosing the right attire for a court marriage can be stressful. You want to look your best, but you also want to be comfortable and appropriate for the occasion. Moreover, many people are not sure what the dress code is for court marriages in India. There is no one-size-fits-all answer to this question, as there are different types of court marriages and the dress code may vary depending on the court's location and the couple's preferences.

What to Wear in Court Marriage in India: A Complete Guide

When it comes to what to wear in court marriage in India, the key is to dress modestly and elegantly. Here are some tips:

  • For Men: It is best to wear a formal suit or a traditional Indian outfit such as a sherwani or a kurta-pyjama. Make sure your clothes are clean and well-ironed. Don't forget to wear formal shoes and a tie if you are wearing a suit.
  • For Women: You can wear a saree, salwar kameez, or a formal dress. It is important to avoid any outfit that is too revealing or provocative. Make sure you choose comfortable footwear as you might have to stand for a long time.

It is important to keep the dress code in mind when choosing your attire. If you are unsure about what to wear, you can always ask the couple or the court officials for guidance.

Things to Avoid

When it comes to what to wear in court marriage in India, there are some things you should avoid:

  • Avoid wearing anything that is too revealing or provocative.
  • Avoid wearing anything that is too casual, such as jeans or shorts.
  • Avoid wearing anything that is too flashy, such as sequins or loud prints.

Remember, court marriages are formal occasions, and it's important to dress accordingly.

What If You Are Attending a Court Marriage as a Guest?

If you are attending a court marriage as a guest, the dress code may be less formal. However, it's still important to dress appropriately. You can wear a formal or semi-formal outfit, such as a saree, salwar kameez, or a suit.

How to Accessorize

Accessories can add the perfect finishing touch to your outfit. Here are some tips on how to accessorize for a court marriage:

  • For Men: Wear a watch and a belt that matches your shoes. You can also wear a tie or a pocket square.
  • For Women: Wear simple jewelry such as earrings and a bracelet. Avoid wearing anything too flashy or distracting.

Personal Experience

I recently attended a court marriage and was unsure about what to wear. I decided to wear a saree with a matching blouse and accessorized with a simple necklace and earrings. My husband wore a formal suit with a tie. We received many compliments on our outfits, and we felt comfortable and appropriate for the occasion.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. Can I wear jeans to a court marriage in India?

A1. It's best to avoid wearing jeans to a court marriage in India as it's considered too casual.

Q2. Can I wear a sleeveless dress to a court marriage in India?

A2. It's best to avoid wearing a sleeveless dress to a court marriage in India as it's considered too revealing.

Q3. Can I wear a black outfit to a court marriage in India?

A3. Yes, you can wear a black outfit to a court marriage in India. However, it's best to avoid wearing anything too flashy or distracting.

Q4. Can I wear sandals to a court marriage in India?

A4. You can wear sandals to a court marriage in India, but make sure they are formal and match your outfit.

Conclusion of What to Wear in Court Marriage in India

Choosing the right attire for a court marriage in India can be stressful, but it doesn't have to be. By following the tips in this guide, you can ensure that you dress appropriately and elegantly for the occasion. Remember to keep the dress code in mind, avoid anything too casual or revealing, and accessorize appropriately. With the right outfit, you can feel comfortable and confident on your special day.
