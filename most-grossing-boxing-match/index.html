In this tutorial, we will explore the most grossing boxing match of all time and discuss its significance in the world of sports.

The target of the most grossing boxing match is to generate the highest revenue possible. This can be achieved through various means, including pay-per-view sales, ticket sales, sponsorships, and merchandise sales. The more people that tune in to watch the match, the more revenue it will generate.
