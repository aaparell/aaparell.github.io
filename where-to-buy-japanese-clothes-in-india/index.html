Where To Buy Japanese Clothes In India A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Aug 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Japan is known for its unique fashion sense, with a mix of traditional and modern styles. However, finding Japanese clothes in India can be quite challenging. You may have searched high and low, but still, you cannot find the right Japanese clothes that fit your style. Fortunately, we have compiled a comprehensive guide on where to buy Japanese clothes in India.

If you are looking for Japanese clothes in India, you have come to the right place. There are several online stores and physical shops that sell Japanese clothes in India. You can find traditional Japanese clothes such as Kimonos, Yukatas, and Haoris, as well as modern Japanese fashion styles such as Harajuku, Lolita, and Gyaru.

In this article, we will explore the different options available for buying Japanese clothes in India. We will also provide tips on how to choose the right Japanese clothes for your style and budget.

Online Stores

One of the easiest ways to buy Japanese clothes in India is through online stores. There are several online stores that sell Japanese clothes in India, with a wide range of styles and prices. These online stores offer convenience, as you can shop from the comfort of your home and have your items delivered to your doorstep.

Some popular online stores for Japanese clothes in India include:

  • Japan Centre
  • Kimono Kitsune
  • Uniqlo
  • YesStyle

Physical Shops

If you prefer to try on clothes before purchasing them, you can visit physical shops that sell Japanese clothes in India. These shops offer a unique shopping experience, as you can see and feel the clothes before buying them. However, physical shops may have limited options compared to online stores.

Some physical shops that sell Japanese clothes in India include:

  • Kimono Kitsune (Mumbai)
  • Kimono Raja (Delhi)
  • Pass the Baton (Mumbai)
  • Kyoto (Delhi)

Tips for Choosing Japanese Clothes

When choosing Japanese clothes, it is important to consider your personal style and budget. Here are some tips to help you choose the right Japanese clothes:

  • Choose clothes that fit your body shape and size.
  • Consider the occasion or event where you will wear the clothes.
  • Choose clothes that match your personal style.
  • Check the fabric and quality of the clothes.
  • Compare prices from different stores to get the best deal.

How to Style Japanese Clothes

Japanese clothes can be styled in different ways, depending on the occasion and your personal style. Here are some tips on how to style Japanese clothes:

  • Pair a Kimono or Yukata with sandals or boots for a casual look.
  • Wear a Haori over a dress or skirt for a formal event.
  • Pair a Harajuku outfit with bold accessories and colorful makeup.
  • Wear a Lolita dress with lace stockings and Mary Jane shoes.
  • Pair a Gyaru outfit with platform shoes and a cute handbag.

Question and Answer

Q: Where can I find traditional Japanese clothes in India?

A: You can find traditional Japanese clothes such as Kimonos, Yukatas, and Haoris at online stores and physical shops that sell Japanese clothes in India.

Q: How much do Japanese clothes cost in India?

A: The price of Japanese clothes in India varies depending on the store and the type of clothes. Traditional Japanese clothes such as Kimonos and Yukatas may cost more than modern Japanese fashion styles such as Harajuku and Gyaru.

Q: Can I wear Japanese clothes for everyday wear?

A: Yes, you can wear Japanese clothes for everyday wear. Japanese fashion styles such as Harajuku and Gyaru are popular for everyday wear.

Q: What accessories should I wear with Japanese clothes?

A: You can wear accessories such as sandals, boots, hats, headbands, earrings, necklaces, and bracelets with Japanese clothes. Choose accessories that match your personal style and the occasion.

Conclusion

Buying Japanese clothes in India may seem challenging, but with the right information and resources, you can find the perfect Japanese clothes for your style and budget. Whether you prefer online stores or physical shops, there are several options available to choose from. Just remember to consider your personal style, body shape, and occasion when choosing Japanese clothes, and have fun experimenting with different styles and accessories.
