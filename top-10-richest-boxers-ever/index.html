Are you curious to know about the wealthiest boxers in history? If so, you're in the right place. In this article, we'll be discussing the top 10 richest boxers ever. These boxers have not only made a significant name for themselves in the ring, but they've also made a fortune from their boxing careers.

Boxing is one of the most rewarding sports in terms of prize money. Every year, millions of dollars are awarded to boxers who win major fights, and some of these boxers have accumulated a fortune over time.

In this article, we'll be discussing the top 10 richest boxers ever and how they made their money.

Floyd Mayweather Jr.

Floyd Mayweather Jr. is considered one of the greatest boxers of all time, with an undefeated record of 50-0. He has made a fortune from his boxing career, estimated to be around $1.1 billion. Mayweather has won several titles and has been involved in some of the highest-grossing fights of all time.

Mayweather's wealth doesn't only come from boxing, but he is also a shrewd businessman. He owns several businesses, including Mayweather Promotions, The Money Team, and a strip club.

Manny Pacquiao

Manny Pacquiao is a Filipino boxer who has won world titles in eight different weight divisions. He has an estimated net worth of $220 million. Pacquiao has been involved in some of the most-watched fights of all time, including his showdown with Mayweather.

Pacquiao is also involved in politics in the Philippines, where he serves as a senator.

Oscar De La Hoya

Oscar De La Hoya is a retired American boxer who won multiple world titles in six different weight classes. He has an estimated net worth of $200 million. De La Hoya is also a successful promoter and founded Golden Boy Promotions.

George Foreman

George Foreman is a retired American boxer who won two world championships in his career. He has an estimated net worth of $300 million. Foreman is also a successful entrepreneur and has his own line of grills called the George Foreman Grill.

Lennox Lewis

Lennox Lewis is a retired British-Canadian boxer who won multiple world titles in his career. He has an estimated net worth of $140 million. Lewis was involved in some of the biggest fights of the 1990s, including his bouts with Mike Tyson and Evander Holyfield.

Sugar Ray Leonard

Sugar Ray Leonard is a retired American boxer who won world titles in five different weight classes. He has an estimated net worth of $120 million. Leonard was one of the biggest stars of the 1980s and was involved in some of the most-watched fights of the decade.

Leonard is also a successful entrepreneur and has his own line of energy drinks called Sugar Ray's Power Punch.

Vitali Klitschko

Vitali Klitschko is a retired Ukrainian boxer who won multiple world titles in his career. He has an estimated net worth of $80 million. Klitschko was a dominant heavyweight in the 2000s and was involved in some of the biggest fights of the decade.

Wladimir Klitschko

Wladimir Klitschko is a retired Ukrainian boxer who won multiple world titles in his career. He has an estimated net worth of $70 million. Klitschko was a dominant heavyweight in the 2000s and was involved in some of the biggest fights of the decade.

Saul "Canelo" Alvarez

Saul "Canelo" Alvarez is a Mexican boxer who has won world titles in four different weight classes. He has an estimated net worth of $140 million. Alvarez is one of the most popular boxers in the world today and has been involved in some of the biggest fights in recent years.

Mike Tyson

Mike Tyson is a retired American boxer who won multiple world titles in his career. He has an estimated net worth of $3 million. Tyson was one of the biggest stars of the 1980s and 1990s and was involved in some of the most-watched fights of the era.

Despite his wealth, Tyson has had some financial difficulties in recent years and has filed for bankruptcy twice.

Conclusion of Top 10 Richest Boxers Ever

These are the top 10 richest boxers ever, and they've made their money through their dedication and hard work in the ring. While the sport can be brutal, it can also be rewarding for those who are successful.

Whether you're a fan of boxing or just interested in the sport's wealthiest fighters, these boxers are worth knowing about.
