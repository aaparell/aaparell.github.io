Should You Wear Black To A Spring Wedding

Written by Daniel Jul 10, 2023 · 4 min read

Wedding season is here, and you may be wondering what to wear to that spring wedding you've been invited to. But if you're considering wearing black, you may be hesitant because you've heard conflicting opinions. Some say that black is always appropriate, while others say it's a faux pas. So, should you wear black to a spring wedding?

Many people believe that wearing black to a wedding is taboo because it's traditionally associated with funerals. However, times are changing, and black is now considered a chic and sophisticated color choice for any occasion, including weddings. But it's important to consider the specific circumstances of the wedding before making your decision.

The answer to whether or not you should wear black to a spring wedding is a bit complicated. It depends on the time of day, the formality of the event, and the wedding's overall style. If it's an evening or black-tie wedding, then black is definitely appropriate. But if it's a daytime or more casual wedding, then you may want to opt for a lighter color.

In conclusion, wearing black to a spring wedding is acceptable as long as you consider the specific circumstances of the event. If you're still unsure, here are some things to keep in mind:

Time of Day Matters

The time of day can greatly affect the appropriateness of wearing black to a spring wedding. If the wedding is in the evening, then black is a classic and elegant choice. However, if the wedding is during the day, then you may want to choose a lighter color such as pastels or bright hues.

Formality is Key

The formality of the wedding is also an important factor to consider. If the wedding is a black-tie affair, then black is definitely appropriate. However, if it's a more casual event, then you may want to dress down a bit and choose a lighter color.

Consider the Wedding's Style

The overall style of the wedding can also influence your decision to wear black. If the wedding is modern and trendy, then black can be a great choice. But if it's a more traditional or rustic wedding, then you may want to choose a lighter color that better suits the theme.

How to Accessorize

If you do decide to wear black to a spring wedding, then it's important to accessorize properly. You can add pops of color with your shoes, jewelry, or even a colorful clutch. This will help to balance out the darkness of the black and add a bit of fun to your outfit.

Personal Experience

As a wedding guest, I've worn black to both daytime and evening weddings. For daytime weddings, I opted for a black dress with colorful accessories, such as a bright pink scarf and matching clutch. For evening weddings, I chose a more formal black gown with silver jewelry and black heels. In both cases, I felt appropriate and comfortable.

Q&A

Q: Is it ever inappropriate to wear black to a spring wedding?
A: It depends on the specific circumstances of the wedding. If it's a daytime or more casual wedding, then you may want to choose a lighter color. But if it's an evening or black-tie wedding, then black is definitely appropriate.

Q: Can I wear a black dress to a spring wedding?
A: Yes, you can wear a black dress to a spring wedding as long as it's appropriate for the time of day, formality of the event, and overall style of the wedding.

Q: What should I pair with a black dress for a spring wedding?
A: You can pair a black dress with colorful accessories, such as bright shoes, jewelry, or a clutch. This will help to balance out the darkness of the black and add a bit of fun to your outfit.

Q: Can men wear black to a spring wedding?
A: Yes, men can wear black to a spring wedding as long as it's appropriate for the time of day, formality of the event, and overall style of the wedding.

Conclusion

In conclusion, wearing black to a spring wedding is acceptable as long as you consider the specific circumstances of the event. Time of day, formality, and style are all important factors to consider when deciding what to wear. As long as you accessorize properly and feel comfortable in your outfit, you'll be sure to make a great impression as a wedding guest.
