Question and Answer:

Q: Are all sunglasses created equal when it comes to UV protection?

A: No, not all sunglasses offer the same level of UV protection. Look for sunglasses that offer 100% UV protection to ensure your eyes are protected.

Q: Can I wear sunglasses even when it's overcast?

A: Yes, it's still important to wear sunglasses even when it's overcast. UV rays can still penetrate through clouds and cause damage to your eyes.

Q: How do I know if sunglasses are comfortable?

A: When trying on sunglasses, make sure they sit comfortably on your nose and ears without pinching or rubbing. You should be able to wear them for an extended period of time without any discomfort.

Q: Can I wear sunglasses indoors?

A: While it's not necessary to wear sunglasses indoors, some people may find it helpful if they have light sensitivity. If you do wear sunglasses indoors, make sure they are not too dark and don't impair your vision.
