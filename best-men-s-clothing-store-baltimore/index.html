Question and Answer

Q: What should I wear to a job interview?

A: For a job interview, it's important to dress professionally. Opt for a suit or dress pants and a dress shirt. Avoid wearing anything too casual or flashy.

Q: How do I know if a shirt fits me properly?

A: A shirt should fit comfortably around the chest and shoulders. The sleeves should end at your wrist bone, and the collar should fit snugly but not be too tight.

Q: What's the best way to care for my clothes?

A: Always read the care label on your clothes before washing or drying them. Use cold water and gentle detergent, and avoid using fabric softener or bleach. Hang dry or use a low heat setting in the dryer.

Q: What should I wear to a wedding?

A: The dress code for a wedding will depend on the invitation. For a formal wedding, opt for a suit or tuxedo. For a semi-formal or casual wedding, dress pants and a dress shirt will suffice.
