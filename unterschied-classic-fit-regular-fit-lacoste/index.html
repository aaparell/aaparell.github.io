Understanding The Difference Between Classic Fit And Regular Fit Lacoste

Written by Christine Oct 31, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of buying clothes that don't fit you well? Have you ever wondered what the difference is between classic fit and regular fit Lacoste? If so, you're in the right place. In this post, we'll explore the different fit types, their target, and how to choose the right one for you.

When it comes to clothing, one size doesn't fit all. Different brands and styles have different fits, which can make it challenging to find the perfect fit for your body type. This is where understanding the difference between classic fit and regular fit Lacoste comes in handy.

Classic fit Lacoste is designed for men who prefer a looser fit. It has a relaxed fit through the chest, waist, and hip, which makes it ideal for men with a larger build or those who prefer a more comfortable fit. On the other hand, regular fit Lacoste is designed for men who prefer a more tailored fit. It has a slimmer fit through the chest, waist, and hip, which makes it ideal for men with a slimmer build.

In summary, the main difference between classic fit and regular fit Lacoste is the fit through the chest, waist, and hip. Classic fit is more relaxed, while regular fit is more tailored.
