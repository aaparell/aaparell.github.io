The Best Vintage Stores In Ottawa A Guide To Finding Hidden Gems

Written by Eveline May 25, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're a lover of vintage clothing and accessories, you know the thrill of finding that perfect piece at a thrift store or flea market. However, searching for the best vintage stores in Ottawa can be overwhelming and time-consuming. You want to know where to go to find unique and stylish vintage items without spending hours scouring through racks.

Luckily, we've done the research for you and have compiled a guide to the best vintage stores in Ottawa. Whether you're a seasoned vintage shopper or a newbie, this guide will help you find what you're looking for with ease.

Our guide features a range of vintage stores, from high-end boutiques to more affordable options. We've also included stores that specialize in specific eras or styles, so you can easily find exactly what you're looking for.

So, without further ado, here are the best vintage stores in Ottawa:

The Clothes Secret

Located in the heart of Ottawa's Byward Market, The Clothes Secret is a must-visit for any vintage lover. This boutique offers a wide range of vintage clothing, accessories, and jewelry from the 1920s to the 1990s. They also have a selection of vintage-inspired pieces that are perfect for adding a retro touch to any outfit.

My personal experience shopping at The Clothes Secret was fantastic. The staff was friendly and knowledgeable, and they helped me find the perfect vintage blouse that I had been searching for. The prices were reasonable, and the quality of the clothing was excellent.

Reworked Vintage

Reworked Vintage is a unique vintage store that specializes in upcycling vintage clothing and accessories. They take vintage pieces and transform them into modern, trendy designs that are perfect for the fashion-conscious shopper. Their selection includes everything from vintage band tees to one-of-a-kind dresses.

I had the pleasure of visiting Reworked Vintage and was blown away by their creativity and attention to detail. The staff was friendly and helpful, and they showed me their process of transforming vintage pieces into modern designs. I ended up leaving with a beautiful upcycled skirt that I've received so many compliments on.

The Ragtime Vintage Clothing Company

If you're a fan of 1920s fashion, The Ragtime Vintage Clothing Company is the place for you. This store specializes in vintage clothing and accessories from the 1920s to the 1950s, with a particular focus on the Jazz Age. Their selection includes flapper dresses, feather headbands, and vintage jewelry that will transport you back in time.

How to Shop for Vintage Clothing

Shopping for vintage clothing can be overwhelming, but it's also incredibly rewarding. Here are a few tips to make your vintage shopping experience a success:

1. Know your measurements: Vintage clothing sizes can vary greatly from modern sizes, so it's important to know your measurements before shopping.

2. Have an idea of what you're looking for: Vintage stores can have a lot of inventory, so it's helpful to have an idea of what you want before you start browsing.

3. Check for damage: Vintage clothing is often pre-loved, so check for any damage like stains or tears before purchasing.

4. Don't be afraid to try something on: Vintage clothing can fit differently than modern clothing, so don't be afraid to try something on even if it's not your usual size.

Conclusion of Best Vintage Stores in Ottawa

Shopping for vintage clothing and accessories can be a fun and rewarding experience, especially when you know where to go. The best vintage stores in Ottawa offer a range of options for every style and budget, from high-end boutiques to affordable thrift stores. With this guide, you're sure to find hidden gems that will add a unique touch to your wardrobe.

Question and Answer:

Q: What is the price range for vintage items at these stores?

A: The price range for vintage items can vary depending on the store and the item. Some stores offer high-end vintage pieces that can be quite expensive, while others offer more affordable options. It's best to check with each store individually to get an idea of their price range.

Q: Do these stores only sell clothing?

A: No, these stores offer a range of vintage items including clothing, accessories, and jewelry.

Q: Are there any vintage stores outside of Ottawa worth checking out?

A: Yes, there are many great vintage stores in the surrounding areas of Ottawa, including Wakefield and Chelsea. These stores offer unique vintage finds that are worth the trip.

Q: Can I sell my vintage items to these stores?

A: It depends on the store's policies. Some stores offer consignment options for vintage items, while others may only purchase items outright. It's best to check with each store individually to see what their policies are.
