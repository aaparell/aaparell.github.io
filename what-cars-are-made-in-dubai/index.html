Jannarelly Automotive: Retro Sports Cars

Another brand that is gaining popularity in Dubai is Jannarelly Automotive. The company was founded in 2016 and produces retro-inspired sports cars like the Design-1. The car's design is reminiscent of classic sports cars from the 1960s and 70s, but it's equipped with modern technology like a lightweight carbon fiber chassis and a mid-mounted V6 engine. Electric Cars in Dubai
