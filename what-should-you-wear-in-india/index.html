What Should You Wear In India

Written by Aprilia Oct 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

India is a country with a diverse culture and traditions that can be overwhelming for first-time visitors. One of the most important aspects to consider when traveling to India is what to wear. The clothing you choose can have a significant impact on your experience, as well as your safety and comfort. In this blog post, we will guide you through what you should wear in India to make your trip a pleasant one.

Many travelers struggle with what to wear in India, as the country has a unique culture and climate. The weather can be hot and humid, and cultural norms dictate that clothing should be modest and appropriate. Additionally, the types of clothing you wear can impact how you are perceived by locals, and may even lead to unwanted attention or harassment.

When traveling to India, it is important to dress modestly and respectfully. This means avoiding revealing clothing such as shorts, tank tops, or low-cut tops. Instead, opt for loose-fitting clothing that covers your shoulders, chest, and legs. Cotton and linen fabrics are ideal for the hot weather, and can help keep you cool and comfortable.

Overall, the key to dressing appropriately in India is to be respectful of the local culture and customs. This means avoiding clothing that is too revealing or provocative, and opting for modest and comfortable attire instead. By doing so, you can ensure that your trip to India is a positive and memorable experience.

Personal Experience with What to Wear in India

As someone who has traveled to India multiple times, I can attest to the importance of dressing appropriately. On my first trip, I made the mistake of wearing shorts and a tank top, which led to unwanted attention and harassment from locals. Since then, I have made a conscious effort to dress more modestly, and have found that it has made a significant difference in how I am perceived and treated.

Clothing Tips for Women in India

Women traveling to India should be especially mindful of their clothing choices. It is recommended that women wear long skirts or pants with a loose-fitting top that covers their shoulders and chest. Avoid wearing tight or revealing clothing, as this can attract unwanted attention. Additionally, it is important to carry a scarf or shawl to cover your head and shoulders when entering religious sites or temples.

Clothing Tips for Men in India

Men traveling to India should also dress modestly and respectfully. It is recommended that men wear pants or long shorts with a shirt that covers their shoulders and chest. Avoid wearing tank tops or sleeveless shirts, as this can be seen as disrespectful in Indian culture.

How to Dress for Different Occasions in India

When traveling to India, it is important to dress appropriately for different occasions. For example, if you are attending a wedding or formal event, it is recommended that you wear traditional Indian attire such as a saree or kurta. Similarly, if you are visiting a religious site or temple, it is important to dress modestly and cover your head and shoulders.

Packing List for What to Wear in India

When packing for a trip to India, it is important to bring clothing that is appropriate for the climate and culture. Here is a packing list of essentials that you should consider bringing:

  • Loose-fitting pants or skirts
  • Long-sleeved shirts
  • Cotton or linen fabrics
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • A scarf or shawl
  • Traditional Indian attire (if attending a wedding or formal event)

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear jeans in India?

A: Yes, you can wear jeans in India, but it is recommended that you pair them with a loose-fitting top that covers your shoulders and chest.

Q: Can I wear shorts in India?

A: It is not recommended to wear shorts in India, as they are considered inappropriate and can attract unwanted attention.

Q: Do I need to cover my head in India?

A: It is not necessary to cover your head in India, but it is recommended to carry a scarf or shawl to cover your head and shoulders when entering religious sites or temples.

Q: Can I wear Western clothing in India?

A: Yes, you can wear Western clothing in India, but it is important to be respectful of the local culture and customs by avoiding revealing or provocative clothing.

Conclusion of What to Wear in India

Overall, when traveling to India, it is important to dress modestly and respectfully to ensure a positive and memorable experience. By following these tips and guidelines, you can ensure that your clothing choices are appropriate for the climate and culture, and avoid unwanted attention or harassment from locals.
