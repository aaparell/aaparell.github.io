Best Men s Summer Styles Look Cool And Stay Comfortable

Written by William May 31, 2023 · 4 min read

Summer is a time when men want to look stylish while also staying cool and comfortable. However, finding the perfect summer outfit can be a challenge. With so many options available, it can be difficult to know what will look good and keep you comfortable in the heat.

If you're struggling to find the right summer style, don't worry. In this article, we will cover the best men's summer styles, including the latest trends and classic looks that never go out of fashion.

To help you look your best this summer, we've put together a list of the top men's summer styles. Whether you're heading to the beach or the office, these styles will have you looking and feeling great.

1. The Classic Polo Shirt

There's a reason why the polo shirt has been a staple in men's summer fashion for decades. This classic shirt is both stylish and comfortable, and it can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

Personally, I love to pair my polo shirt with a pair of chino shorts and boat shoes for a casual look. However, you can also wear a polo with tailored trousers and loafers for a more formal outfit.

2. The Lightweight Linen Shirt

For a dressier summer look, a lightweight linen shirt is the way to go. Not only is linen breathable and comfortable in the heat, but it also has a relaxed, beachy vibe that's perfect for summer.

I like to wear my linen shirt with a pair of white jeans and sandals for a stylish yet comfortable outfit. You can also dress up your linen shirt with a blazer and dress shoes for a more formal occasion.

3. The Short-Sleeve Button-Down Shirt

If you're looking for a versatile summer shirt that you can wear to both casual and formal events, a short-sleeve button-down shirt is a great option. This shirt can be worn with shorts, chinos, or even tailored trousers.

For a casual look, pair your short-sleeve button-down with a pair of shorts and sneakers. If you're going to a more formal event, wear it with a pair of chinos and loafers.

4. The Lightweight Shorts

No summer outfit is complete without a pair of lightweight shorts. Whether you prefer chino shorts, denim shorts, or cargo shorts, make sure they're comfortable and breathable.

I like to wear my chino shorts with a polo shirt and boat shoes for a classic summer look. If you're going for a more casual vibe, you can wear your shorts with a t-shirt and sneakers.

5. The Slip-On Sneakers

When it comes to summer footwear, slip-on sneakers are a must-have. These shoes are comfortable and easy to wear, and they can be paired with almost any summer outfit.

I like to wear my slip-on sneakers with a pair of denim shorts and a t-shirt for a casual look. However, you can also wear them with chinos and a polo for a dressier outfit.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear sandals with socks?

A: No, it's generally not a good idea to wear sandals with socks. If you're wearing sandals, it's best to go sockless or wear no-show socks.

Q: Can I wear a t-shirt to the office?

A: It depends on your office dress code. If your office is more casual, you can probably get away with wearing a t-shirt. However, if your office is more formal, you should stick to a collared shirt.

Q: What shoes should I wear with shorts?

A: You can wear a variety of shoes with shorts, including boat shoes, slip-on sneakers, and sandals. Just make sure they're comfortable and appropriate for the occasion.

Q: Can I wear white after Labor Day?

A: Yes, you can definitely wear white after Labor Day. The old fashion rule about not wearing white after Labor Day is outdated.

Conclusion of Best Men's Summer Styles

Summer is a time to relax, have fun, and enjoy the warm weather. By following these best men's summer styles, you can look stylish and stay comfortable all season long. Whether you're heading to the beach, the office, or a night out with friends, these summer styles will have you looking your best.
