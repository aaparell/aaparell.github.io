Suit Price In Bangladesh A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Jun 06, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you in search of the perfect suit that fits your budget? Well, the suit price in Bangladesh varies based on several factors. As a result, it can be a daunting task to find the right suit that meets both your requirements and budget. In this article, we will provide you with a complete guide on suit price in Bangladesh and help you make an informed decision.

When it comes to purchasing a suit, there are several pain points that one can face. One of the most significant factors is the budget. Everyone wants to look good and wear the best quality suits, but the budget can often hold them back. Another pain point is the availability of quality suits in the market. It can be challenging to find the perfect fit and quality that meets your expectations.

The suit price in Bangladesh ranges from BDT 3,000 to BDT 50,000 depending on the quality, material, and brand. The price may also vary based on the type of suit, such as wedding suits, formal suits, or casual suits. Therefore, it is crucial to have a clear understanding of your requirements and budget before purchasing a suit.

In summary, suit price in Bangladesh varies based on several factors such as quality, material, brand, and type of suit. It is essential to have a clear understanding of your requirements and budget before making a purchase. In the following sections, we will discuss the different aspects of suit price in Bangladesh in more detail.

Factors That Affect Suit Price in Bangladesh

When it comes to purchasing a suit, several factors can affect the price. Here are some of the most significant factors:

Quality of Material

The quality of material used in the suit can significantly impact the price. Higher quality materials such as wool, cashmere, or silk are more expensive than synthetic materials such as polyester or rayon.

Brand

The brand of the suit can also affect the price. Famous brands such as Armani, Hugo Boss, or Tom Ford are known for their quality and style, and as a result, are more expensive than less popular brands.

Type of Suit

The type of suit you are looking for can also affect the price. Wedding suits and formal suits are generally more expensive than casual suits due to their intricate designs and high-quality materials.

Tailoring

The cost of tailoring can also add to the overall price of the suit. A well-tailored suit can make all the difference, but it can come at a cost. It is essential to factor in the cost of tailoring before making a purchase.

My Personal Experience with Suit Price in Bangladesh

As someone who has purchased several suits in Bangladesh, I can say that the suit price can vary significantly based on the factors mentioned above. For instance, I once purchased a wedding suit for BDT 30,000, but I also purchased a casual suit for BDT 5,000. The quality of the material, brand, and type of suit all contributed to the difference in price.

Where to Buy Suits in Bangladesh

There are several places to buy suits in Bangladesh, including shopping malls, standalone stores, and online marketplaces. Some of the popular places to buy suits in Bangladesh include:

Bashundhara City Shopping Mall

Bashundhara City Shopping Mall is home to several high-end stores that offer a wide range of suits. You can find both local and international brands here.

Aarong

Aarong is a popular retail chain in Bangladesh that offers a variety of suits. They are known for their quality and unique designs.

Daraz

Daraz is an online marketplace that offers a wide range of suits. You can find both local and international brands here at competitive prices.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I find a quality suit in Bangladesh on a tight budget?

A: Yes, you can find quality suits in Bangladesh on a tight budget. However, you may need to compromise on the brand or type of suit.

Q: Is it better to buy a suit from a standalone store or a shopping mall?

A: It depends on your requirements and budget. Shopping malls offer a wide range of options, while standalone stores may offer more personalized services.

Q: How much should I spend on a wedding suit in Bangladesh?

A: The price of a wedding suit in Bangladesh varies based on several factors. However, you can expect to spend anywhere from BDT 20,000 to BDT 50,000 for a high-quality wedding suit.

Q: Can I get my suit tailored in Bangladesh?

A: Yes, you can get your suit tailored in Bangladesh. There are several tailoring shops available in most shopping malls and standalone stores.

Conclusion of Suit Price in Bangladesh

When it comes to purchasing a suit in Bangladesh, the price can vary significantly based on several factors. It is essential to have a clear understanding of your requirements and budget before making a purchase. With the right research and knowledge, you can find the perfect suit that meets your expectations without breaking the bank.
