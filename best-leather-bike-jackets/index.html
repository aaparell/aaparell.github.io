The Best Leather Bike Jackets A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Nov 10, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Whether you're a seasoned biker or just starting out, finding the right gear is crucial to your safety and comfort on the road. One of the most important pieces of gear for any rider is a quality leather bike jacket. Not only does it provide protection from the elements, but it also serves as a second skin in the unfortunate event of an accident. But with so many options on the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the best leather bike jacket for your needs.

When it comes to purchasing the best leather bike jacket, there are a few things you should keep in mind. First, consider the level of protection you need based on the type of riding you'll be doing. Are you a casual rider or do you plan on hitting the open road for long distances? Next, think about your budget and what features are most important to you. Do you want a jacket with multiple pockets or one that's more streamlined?

In this comprehensive guide, we'll go over everything you need to know about the best leather bike jackets on the market. From the top brands to the most important features to look for, we'll cover it all.

The Importance of Choosing the Right Leather Bike Jacket

As mentioned earlier, a leather bike jacket is an essential piece of gear for any rider. It not only protects you from the elements but also serves as a protective layer in case of an accident. But not all jackets are created equal. Choosing the right one can mean the difference between a comfortable ride and a painful one.

Personally, I learned this lesson the hard way. In my early days of riding, I opted for a cheap leather jacket that didn't fit properly. It was bulky and uncomfortable, but I figured it was better than nothing. That is, until I took a spill on the highway. Thankfully, I wasn't seriously injured, but the jacket didn't hold up as well as it should have. After that experience, I knew I needed to invest in a better quality, properly fitting leather bike jacket.

Top Brands for Leather Bike Jackets

When it comes to quality leather bike jackets, there are a few brands that stand above the rest. One of the most well-known is Schott NYC. Founded in 1913, Schott has been producing high-quality leather jackets for over a century. Their Perfecto jacket is a classic and has been worn by everyone from Marlon Brando to Bruce Springsteen.

Another top brand is Harley-Davidson. While they're most commonly associated with motorcycles, Harley-Davidson also produces a line of leather jackets that are both stylish and functional. Their Men's Command Leather Jacket, for example, features a removable hoodie and body armor pockets for added protection.

Important Features to Look for in a Leather Bike Jacket

When shopping for the best leather bike jacket, there are a few key features to keep in mind. First and foremost, look for a jacket that fits you properly. It should be snug but not constricting, and the sleeves should be long enough to cover your wrists when you're in a riding position.

Next, consider the type of leather used in the jacket. Cowhide is the most common and durable option, but there are also jackets made from buffalo, goat, and other types of leather. Make sure to choose a jacket with a thickness of at least 1.2mm for optimal protection.

Other important features to look for include ventilation, padding, and pockets. Ventilation is crucial for staying comfortable on hot days, while padding can help protect you in case of an accident. Pockets are also important for storing your phone, wallet, and other essentials while you're on the road.

Caring for Your Leather Bike Jacket

Once you've found the perfect leather bike jacket, it's important to take care of it properly. This can include using a leather conditioner to keep the leather supple and prevent cracking, as well as storing the jacket in a cool, dry place when not in use.

It's also important to clean your jacket regularly to remove dirt and grime. This can be done using a damp cloth and a mild soap. Avoid using harsh chemicals or soaking the jacket in water, as this can damage the leather.

FAQs

Q: How much should I expect to spend on a quality leather bike jacket?

A: While there are cheaper options on the market, a high-quality leather bike jacket typically costs between $200 and $500.

Q: Can I wear a leather bike jacket off the bike?

A: Absolutely! Many leather bike jackets are stylish enough to be worn as a fashion statement off the bike.

Q: How do I know if a leather bike jacket fits properly?

A: The jacket should be snug but not constricting, and the sleeves should be long enough to cover your wrists when you're in a riding position.

Q: How should I store my leather bike jacket?

A: Store your jacket in a cool, dry place when not in use, and avoid folding or creasing the leather.

Conclusion

Investing in the best leather bike jacket is an essential step for any rider. By choosing a high-quality jacket with the right features, you can protect yourself from the elements and stay safe in case of an accident. Keep in mind the tips and recommendations outlined in this guide to help you find the perfect leather bike jacket for your needs.
