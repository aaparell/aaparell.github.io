Male Sustainable Clothing Brands A Guide To Ethical Fashion

Written by Christine May 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As the world becomes increasingly aware of the impact of fast fashion on the environment and the people who make our clothes, many men are looking for more sustainable and ethical options when it comes to their wardrobe. However, finding high-quality, stylish, and affordable sustainable clothing for men can be a challenge.

If you're looking for male sustainable clothing brands, you're not alone. Many men are seeking out clothing that is made with organic or recycled materials, produced in ethical and fair trade factories, and designed to last. But with so many options out there, it can be tough to know where to start.

This guide aims to help you navigate the world of male sustainable clothing brands and find the best options for you. We'll cover some of the top brands on the market, as well as tips for shopping sustainably and making your wardrobe more eco-friendly.

Patagonia

Patagonia is one of the most well-known and respected sustainable clothing brands for men (and women). The company has been at the forefront of the eco-friendly fashion movement for decades, and is committed to using sustainable materials and manufacturing processes.

I've personally been a fan of Patagonia for years, and their clothing has held up incredibly well over time. Their jackets, in particular, are some of the best on the market in terms of both style and function. Plus, they offer a range of sizes and styles to suit any man's taste.

Outerknown

Another great option for male sustainable clothing is Outerknown. Founded by pro surfer Kelly Slater, Outerknown is committed to using sustainable and recycled materials in their clothing, as well as ethical manufacturing practices.

I recently purchased a shirt from Outerknown and was impressed by the quality and style. It's definitely on the pricier side, but I feel good knowing that I'm supporting a brand that is doing its part for the planet.

Organic Basics

If you're looking for sustainable basics like t-shirts, underwear, and socks, Organic Basics is a great option. The brand uses organic cotton and other sustainable materials, and is committed to minimizing waste and reducing their carbon footprint.

I've been wearing Organic Basics underwear for a few months now and I'm really happy with the quality and comfort. Plus, it feels good to know that I'm supporting a brand that is doing good for the environment.

How to Shop Sustainably

When it comes to shopping sustainably, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, look for brands that are transparent about their materials and manufacturing processes. Ideally, you want a brand that uses sustainable materials like organic cotton, hemp, or recycled polyester, and is committed to ethical manufacturing practices.

Second, look for clothing that is designed to last. Avoid fast fashion trends and instead invest in high-quality pieces that will stand the test of time. This not only saves you money in the long run, but it also reduces the amount of clothing that ends up in landfills.

Finally, consider buying secondhand or vintage clothing. This is a great way to reduce your carbon footprint and support sustainable fashion without breaking the bank.

Question and Answer

Q: Are sustainable clothing brands more expensive?

A: In general, sustainable clothing brands tend to be more expensive than fast fashion brands. However, you're paying for higher-quality materials and manufacturing processes, as well as supporting brands that are doing good for the environment and their workers.

Q: How can I tell if a brand is truly sustainable?

A: Look for brands that are transparent about their materials and manufacturing processes. Check for certifications like GOTS (Global Organic Textile Standard) or Fair Trade, which indicate that a brand is committed to sustainable and ethical practices.

Q: Can I still be fashionable while shopping sustainably?

A: Absolutely! There are plenty of sustainable clothing brands that offer stylish and trendy options, from Patagonia to Outerknown to Reformation. Plus, investing in high-quality pieces that are designed to last is always in style.

Q: What are some other ways to make my wardrobe more sustainable?

A: Consider buying secondhand or vintage clothing, repairing clothing instead of throwing it away, and donating or recycling clothing that you no longer wear. You can also look for sustainable options for accessories like shoes, belts, and bags.

Conclusion of Male Sustainable Clothing Brands

Shopping sustainably doesn't have to be difficult or expensive. By investing in high-quality, eco-friendly clothing from brands like Patagonia, Outerknown, and Organic Basics, you can look great while doing your part for the planet. Remember to look for transparency and quality when choosing sustainable clothing brands, and consider buying secondhand or vintage to reduce your carbon footprint even further.
