How To Measure Neck Size On Shirt

Written by Petter Jul 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Have you ever bought a shirt that seemed like the perfect size, only to find that the neck was too tight or too loose? It can be frustrating to try on multiple shirts and still not find the right fit. However, measuring your neck size before purchasing a shirt can help you avoid this issue.

When it comes to purchasing a shirt, the neck size can make all the difference in how comfortable you feel. A shirt with a neck that is too tight can cause discomfort, while a shirt with a neck that is too loose can look sloppy. Therefore, it is important to know how to measure your neck size to ensure a proper fit.

The first step in measuring your neck size is to wrap a tape measure around the base of your neck. Make sure the tape measure is snug but not too tight, and is resting just below your Adam's apple. Record this measurement in inches, and round up to the nearest half-inch.

To ensure the most accurate measurement, it is recommended to have someone else measure your neck for you. This will help avoid any errors or miscalculations.

Measuring Neck Size: Personal Experience

As someone who has struggled with finding the right fit in shirts, I have learned the importance of measuring my neck size. Before measuring, I would often purchase shirts that looked great on the hanger but were uncomfortable to wear due to a tight or loose neck.

Once I learned how to measure my neck size, I was able to confidently purchase shirts that fit comfortably and looked great. It may seem like a small detail, but it can make a big difference in your overall comfort and appearance.

Factors to Consider

There are a few factors to consider when measuring your neck size. First, make sure you are standing up straight and looking straight ahead. This will ensure an accurate measurement.

Additionally, you should consider the type of shirt you will be wearing. If you plan to wear a tie with your shirt, you may want to add an extra half-inch to your measurement to ensure a comfortable fit. On the other hand, if you prefer a more relaxed fit, you may want to subtract a half-inch from your measurement.

Other Ways to Measure Neck Size

In addition to using a tape measure, there are other ways to measure your neck size. One method is to use a string or piece of ribbon to measure your neck, and then measure the string or ribbon with a ruler or tape measure.

Another method is to use a shirt that fits well and measure the neck of the shirt. Lay the shirt flat and measure from the center of the button to the center of the buttonhole. Double this measurement to get your neck size.

Tips for Measuring Neck Size

When measuring your neck size, it is important to keep a few tips in mind. First, make sure the tape measure is snug but not too tight. You should be able to fit a finger between the tape measure and your neck.

Additionally, it is important to measure your neck size while standing up straight and looking straight ahead. This will ensure an accurate measurement.

FAQs About Measuring Neck Size

Q: How do I know if my neck size is too small or too large?

A: If your neck size is too small, the shirt collar will be tight and uncomfortable. If your neck size is too large, the shirt collar will be loose and may look sloppy.

Q: How often should I measure my neck size?

A: Your neck size may change over time, so it is recommended to measure your neck size every few years or as needed.

Q: Can I adjust the neck size of a shirt?

A: It is possible to have a shirt collar adjusted by a tailor, but it may be more cost-effective to purchase a shirt with the correct neck size.

Q: What if my neck size falls between two sizes?

A: In this case, it is recommended to choose the larger size to ensure a comfortable fit.

Conclusion

Measuring your neck size is a simple but important step in finding the right fit for a shirt. By following these tips and tricks, you can ensure a comfortable and stylish look every time you wear a shirt.
