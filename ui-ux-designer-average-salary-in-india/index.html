It's important to know that salaries can vary depending on experience, location, and company size. The UI UX designer average salary in India ranges between INR 4,00,000 to INR 10,00,000 per annum, depending upon the company, experience and location. UI UX Designer Average Salary in India: Factors that Affect Salaries

Experience, location, and company size are some of the most important factors that can affect UI UX designer average salary in India. For example, a UI UX designer with 3-5 years of experience can expect a higher salary than someone just starting out. Similarly, a designer working in a metropolitan city like Mumbai or Bangalore can expect a higher salary than someone working in a smaller city.

Company size is another important factor to consider. Large companies like Amazon, Google, and Microsoft may offer higher salaries than smaller startups. However, smaller startups may offer more opportunities for growth and development.

UI UX Designer Average Salary in India: Negotiating Salaries

When negotiating a salary as a UI UX designer, it's important to have a strong understanding of your skills and experience. If you have a strong portfolio and a track record of success, you may be able to negotiate for a higher salary. It's also important to research salaries in your area and industry to ensure that you are being offered a fair wage.

UI UX Designer Average Salary in India: How to Build a Strong Portfolio

Building a strong portfolio is key to landing a job as a UI UX designer and negotiating a higher salary. Here are some tips for building a strong portfolio:

  • Include a variety of projects that showcase your skills and expertise
  • Showcase your process and problem-solving skills
  • Include case studies that demonstrate your ability to think critically and creatively
  • Highlight your experience with different design tools and software
