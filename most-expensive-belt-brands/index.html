Hermes: The Most Expensive Belt Brand

One of the most well-known and sought-after luxury belt brands is Hermes. This French brand is known for its iconic Birkin bags and silk scarves, but it also has a line of belts that are just as luxurious. The Hermes belts are made of the finest leathers, such as alligator, crocodile, and ostrich, and are handcrafted by skilled artisans. The most expensive Hermes belt is the "Collier de Chien," which features a gold-plated buckle and retails for over $5,000.

Gucci: The Iconic Luxury Belt Brand

Gucci is another iconic luxury brand that has a line of luxurious belts. The Gucci belts are known for their iconic "GG" logo and are made of high-quality leather. The most expensive Gucci belt is the "Double G" belt, which features a gold-tone buckle and retails for over $1,000.
