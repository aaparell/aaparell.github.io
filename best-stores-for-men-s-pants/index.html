Best Stores For Men s Pants Where To Shop For Comfortable And Stylish Bottoms

Written by Petter Jul 22, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of going from one store to another just to find the perfect pair of pants? Do you often settle for ill-fitting or uncomfortable bottoms just because you can't find anything better? Shopping for men's pants can be a daunting task, especially if you don't know where to look. This article will guide you through the best stores for men's pants where you can find comfortable and stylish bottoms that suit your taste and budget.

If you're looking for the best stores for men's pants, it's likely that you've experienced some pain points in the past. These could be related to the lack of variety, poor quality, or high prices of men's pants in certain stores. You might have also struggled to find pants that fit well or flatter your body type. Whatever your pain points may be, rest assured that there are stores out there that cater to your needs.

The target of this article is to provide you with a list of the best stores for men's pants, both online and offline. These stores have been carefully selected based on their range of styles, sizes, materials, and price points. Whether you're looking for dress pants, chinos, jeans, joggers, or any other type of pants, you'll find something that suits your preferences and requirements.

In summary, this article will help you discover the best stores for men's pants, provide you with tips on how to choose the right pants, and give you insights on the latest trends and styles in men's bottoms.

1. Nordstrom

I personally love shopping for pants at Nordstrom. They have a wide range of brands and styles to choose from, including dress pants, chinos, khakis, and jeans. You can find pants in different sizes, fits, and materials, such as cotton, wool, or denim. Nordstrom also offers free alterations on their pants, so you can get the perfect fit without any additional cost.

2. Bonobos

Bonobos is an online store that specializes in men's pants. They offer a variety of fits, including slim, athletic, and straight, as well as different lengths and waist sizes. One thing I love about Bonobos is that they have a feature called "Guideshop" where you can make an appointment to try on pants in person and get personalized recommendations from a stylist.

3. Uniqlo

If you're looking for affordable and comfortable pants, Uniqlo is the place to go. They have a range of styles, including chinos, joggers, and sweatpants, in different colors and sizes. Uniqlo is also known for their innovative materials, such as their "Ezy" pants that are made with a stretchy and breathable fabric.

4. Levi's

No list of best stores for men's pants would be complete without Levi's. This iconic brand has been around for over a century and is known for their high-quality denim jeans. They offer a range of fits, such as slim, straight, and relaxed, as well as different washes and sizes. Levi's also has a customization feature where you can add patches, embroidery, or distressing to your jeans.

5. J.Crew

J.Crew is another great option for men's pants. They have a range of dress pants, chinos, and jeans in different colors and sizes. J.Crew is also known for their collaborations with other brands and designers, such as Nike and New Balance, which results in unique and stylish pants.

Question and Answer

Q: What should I look for when shopping for men's pants?

A: When shopping for men's pants, you should consider the fit, length, material, and style. Make sure the pants fit you well and flatter your body type. Choose a length that works with your shoes and the occasion. Look for materials that are comfortable and durable, such as cotton, wool, or denim. Choose a style that suits your personal taste and the occasion, whether it's dressy, casual, or athletic.

Q: How do I know what size pants to buy?

A: To find your size in men's pants, measure your waist and inseam. The waist measurement should be taken at the narrowest part of your waist, while the inseam measurement should be taken from the crotch to the ankle. Compare your measurements to the size chart of the brand you're interested in, as sizing can vary between brands.

Q: What are some current trends in men's pants?

A: Some current trends in men's pants include relaxed fits, pleated pants, cargo pants, and tapered joggers. Neutral colors such as beige, khaki, and olive are also popular, as well as bold prints and patterns. Sustainable and eco-friendly materials, such as recycled polyester and organic cotton, are also gaining popularity.

Q: Can I wear the same pants for different occasions?

A: It depends on the style and material of the pants. Some pants, such as dress pants or khakis, are more versatile and can be worn for different occasions, from work to dinner parties. Other pants, such as joggers or sweatpants, are more casual and are better suited for lounging or exercising. It's always a good idea to have a variety of pants in your wardrobe to cater to different occasions and moods.

Conclusion of Best Stores for Men's Pants

Now that you know where to shop for the best men's pants, it's time to upgrade your wardrobe and feel confident and comfortable in your bottoms. Remember to consider the fit, length, material, and style when choosing your pants, and don't be afraid to try new trends and styles. Whether you prefer online shopping or in-person shopping, the stores mentioned in this article have got you covered. Happy shopping!
