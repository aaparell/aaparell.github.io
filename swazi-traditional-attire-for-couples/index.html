The Different Styles of Swazi Traditional Attire for Couples

Swazi traditional attire for couples comes in a variety of styles, each with its own unique look and feel. Some popular styles include:

Emahiya

Emahiya is a type of Swazi traditional attire that is worn by women. It consists of a wraparound skirt and a matching top that is tied at the front. The skirt is made of a colorful fabric, and the top is often adorned with intricate beadwork and embroidery.

Indlamu

Indlamu is a type of Swazi traditional attire that is worn by men. It consists of a cowhide skirt that is decorated with beads and cowrie shells. The upper body is usually bare or covered with a simple shirt or vest.

SiSwati

SiSwati is a type of Swazi traditional attire that is worn by both men and women. It consists of a colorful shawl or sash that is draped over the shoulders and tied at the waist. The shawl is often made of a lightweight fabric, such as cotton or silk, and is adorned with intricate embroidery or beadwork.
