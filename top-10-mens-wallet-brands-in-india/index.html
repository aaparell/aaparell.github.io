Wallets are an essential accessory for every man. It not only helps in keeping cards, cash and other important documents safe but also adds a style statement to your outfit. With so many options available it can be confusing to choose the right wallet for you. In this article, we will be discussing the top 10 men's wallet brands in India, to make your decision-making process easier.

When it comes to buying a wallet, there are certain pain points that one may face. Some of the common ones include not knowing the type of wallet that suits their needs, not being able to find the right size, and not being able to find a quality product within their budget.

The target of this article is to provide readers with a comprehensive list of the top 10 men's wallet brands in India, along with their unique features, and price range. This will help readers make an informed decision while purchasing a wallet that meets their needs.

1. Hidesign

Hidesign is a premium leather brand established in 1978 by Dilip Kapur. The brand is known for its high-quality leather products that are handcrafted by artisans. Hidesign wallets are a perfect blend of style and functionality. The price range varies from INR 1,295 to INR 6,995. I have been using Hidesign wallets for the past five years, and I must say they are worth every penny.

Tommy Hilfiger is a well-known American brand that offers a wide range of products including wallets. The brand is known for its classic designs and high-quality materials. Tommy Hilfiger wallets are priced between INR 2,999 to INR 5,999. I gifted a Tommy Hilfiger wallet to my brother on his birthday, and he was thrilled with it.
