What Do You Wear To A Spring Wedding In 2021

Written by Oliver Oct 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Spring weddings are one of the most exciting events of the year. The flowers are blooming, the weather is warming up, and love is in the air. But with so many fashion choices, it can be hard to decide what to wear to a spring wedding in 2021.

Attending a wedding can be stressful enough, especially if you're unsure of what to wear. You want to make sure you look your best, but you also don't want to take away from the bride and groom. Plus, there are so many factors to consider, such as the location, the dress code, and the time of day.

So, what do you wear to a spring wedding in 2021? The answer really depends on the specific wedding and its dress code. However, there are some general guidelines you can follow to ensure you look your best while still adhering to the dress code.

In this article, we will discuss some tips and ideas for what to wear to a spring wedding in 2021. We'll cover everything from dress codes to accessories and provide you with examples to help you make the right choice.

Dress Code: Formal

If the wedding invitation specifies a formal dress code, then you should wear a long gown or a dressy cocktail dress. Go for rich, luxurious fabrics like silk, satin, or chiffon. You can also wear a stylish jumpsuit or a dressy pantsuit if you prefer. Make sure to keep your accessories elegant and refined, such as a clutch, statement jewelry, and high heels.

Dress Code: Semi-Formal

For a semi-formal dress code, you can opt for a cocktail dress or a dressy skirt and top. You can also wear a dressy jumpsuit or a pantsuit. Choose light, flowy fabrics like cotton, linen, or lace. Keep your accessories simple and chic, such as a small clutch, delicate jewelry, and comfortable heels or flats.

Colors and Prints

Spring weddings are the perfect opportunity to play with colors and prints. You can wear pastel colors like pink, lavender, and mint green, or go for bold, vibrant hues like red, fuchsia, or royal blue. Florals are always in style for spring weddings, but you can also wear other prints like polka dots, stripes, or geometric patterns. Just make sure to balance your outfit with neutral accessories.

What Not to Wear

When it comes to spring weddings, there are a few things you should avoid wearing. First, don't wear white or ivory, as those colors are reserved for the bride. Also, avoid anything too revealing or too casual. You want to look put-together and stylish, without upstaging the bride and groom.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear black to a spring wedding?

A: Yes, you can wear black to a spring wedding, especially if it's a formal or semi-formal dress code. Just make sure to add some color with your accessories.

Q: Can I wear a short dress to a formal spring wedding?

A: It's generally better to wear a long gown to a formal spring wedding, but if you prefer a short dress, make sure it's still dressy and made of luxurious fabrics like silk or chiffon.

Q: Can I wear jeans to a spring wedding?

A: No, jeans are too casual for a spring wedding. Even if the dress code is casual, it's better to wear dress pants or a sundress.

Q: Can I wear sandals to a spring wedding?

A: Yes, you can wear sandals to a spring wedding, but make sure they're dressy and not too casual. Avoid flip-flops or beach sandals.

Conclusion of What Do You Wear to a Spring Wedding in 2021

Attending a spring wedding can be a lot of fun, but it can also be stressful when it comes to choosing the right outfit. By following these tips and guidelines, you can ensure that you look your best while still adhering to the dress code and respecting the bride and groom. Remember to choose light, flowy fabrics, play with colors and prints, and keep your accessories elegant and refined.
