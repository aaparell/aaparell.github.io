Cool Areas To Stay In Indianapolis A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Indianapolis, also known as Indy, is the capital city of Indiana. This beautiful city is home to several historic landmarks, museums, parks, and cultural sites. However, finding the perfect accommodation in Indy can be a bit overwhelming, especially if you're not familiar with the area. In this post, we'll explore some of the coolest areas to stay in Indianapolis, so you can make an informed decision when planning your trip.

Are you struggling to find the best place to stay in Indianapolis? Do you want to be close to the action but still have a peaceful place to stay? Are you looking for a budget-friendly option or a luxurious getaway? We understand that finding the perfect accommodation can be a daunting task, but don't worry, we've got you covered. In this post, we'll explore some of the best areas to stay in Indianapolis, so you can have a memorable trip.

1. Downtown Indianapolis

Downtown Indianapolis is the heart of the city and the perfect place to stay if you want to be close to the action. This area is home to several popular attractions, such as the Indianapolis Zoo, the Indiana State Museum, and the Lucas Oil Stadium. You'll also find several dining and shopping options in the area. If you're looking for a luxurious stay, the Conrad Indianapolis is a great option. For budget-friendly options, check out the Courtyard by Marriott Indianapolis Downtown or the Hampton Inn Indianapolis Downtown.

2. Broad Ripple

Broad Ripple is a trendy neighborhood located about 6 miles north of downtown Indianapolis. This area is known for its eclectic mix of boutiques, restaurants, and bars. If you're looking for a vibrant nightlife scene, this is the place to be. The Indianapolis Art Center and the Monon Trail are also located in this area. The Broad Ripple Airbnb is a great option for those looking for a unique stay, while the Ironworks Hotel Indy offers a luxurious experience.

3. Fountain Square

Fountain Square is a historic neighborhood located about 2 miles southeast of downtown Indianapolis. This area is known for its vibrant arts scene, with several art galleries and theaters in the area. You'll also find several dining and entertainment options in the area. The Fountain Square Theatre Building and the Fountain Square Cultural District are popular attractions in this area. The Fountainview Inn is a great option for those looking for a budget-friendly stay, while the Hotel Tango Artisan Distillery offers a unique experience.

4. Mass Ave

Mass Ave is a trendy neighborhood located about 2 miles northeast of downtown Indianapolis. This area is known for its artistic vibe, with several art galleries, theaters, and boutiques in the area. You'll also find several dining and entertainment options in the area. The Old Northside Bed and Breakfast is a great option for those looking for a cozy stay, while the Nestle Inn offers a luxurious experience.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the best area to stay in Indianapolis for families?

A: Downtown Indianapolis is a great option for families, as it is close to several popular attractions, such as the Indianapolis Zoo and the Indiana State Museum. The Courtyard by Marriott Indianapolis Downtown and the Hampton Inn Indianapolis Downtown are budget-friendly options that cater to families.

Q: What is the best area to stay in Indianapolis for a romantic getaway?

A: Broad Ripple and Fountain Square are both great options for a romantic getaway. Broad Ripple offers a vibrant nightlife scene, while Fountain Square has a more laid-back vibe with several art galleries and theaters in the area. The Ironworks Hotel Indy and the Hotel Tango Artisan Distillery are both luxurious options that cater to couples.

Q: What is the best area to stay in Indianapolis for budget travelers?

A: Fountain Square and Mass Ave are both great areas to stay in if you're on a budget. The Fountainview Inn and the Old Northside Bed and Breakfast offer budget-friendly accommodation options in these areas.

Q: What is the best area to stay in Indianapolis for business travelers?

A: Downtown Indianapolis is the best area to stay in for business travelers, as it is close to several corporate offices and convention centers. The Conrad Indianapolis and the Hyatt Regency Indianapolis are both luxurious options that cater to business travelers.

Conclusion of Cool Areas to Stay in Indianapolis

Indianapolis is a beautiful city with several exciting neighborhoods to choose from when it comes to finding the perfect accommodation. Whether you're looking for a luxurious stay or a budget-friendly option, there's something for everyone in Indy. We hope this guide has helped you narrow down your search and find the perfect area to stay in for your next trip to Indianapolis.
