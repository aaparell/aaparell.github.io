1. Van Heusen

Van Heusen is a popular brand for men's clothing and is known for its quality and style. The brand offers a wide range of shirts in different styles, including formal, semi-formal, and casual. The shirts are made from high-quality materials and are designed to fit perfectly. The price range for Van Heusen shirts varies from INR 899 to INR 4,000. You can buy Van Heusen shirts online or in-store.

I have personally tried Van Heusen shirts, and I must say, they are worth the investment. The quality is top-notch, and the fit is impeccable. The shirts are perfect for formal occasions and can also be dressed down for a casual look.

2. Arrow

Arrow is another popular brand for men's clothing and is known for its classic style. The brand offers a wide range of shirts in different styles, including formal, semi-formal, and casual. The shirts are made from high-quality materials and are designed to fit perfectly. The price range for Arrow shirts varies from INR 1,500 to INR 4,000. You can buy Arrow shirts online or in-store.

My experience with Arrow shirts has been fantastic. The shirts are comfortable and stylish, making them perfect for any occasion. The quality is also excellent, and the fit is spot on. I highly recommend Arrow shirts to anyone looking for a classic, timeless style.

3. Raymond

Raymond is a well-known brand for men's clothing and is known for its premium quality. The brand offers a wide range of shirts in different styles, including formal, semi-formal, and casual. The shirts are made from high-quality materials and are designed to fit perfectly. The price range for Raymond shirts varies from INR 1,500 to INR 5,000. You can buy Raymond shirts online or in-store.

Raymond shirts are a personal favorite of mine. The quality is exceptional, and the fit is perfect. The brand offers a variety of styles, making it easy to find a shirt for any occasion. Although the price range is on the higher side, the investment is worth it for the quality and style you get.

4. Peter England

Peter England is a popular brand for men's clothing and is known for its affordable prices. The brand offers a wide range of shirts in different styles, including formal, semi-formal, and casual. The shirts are made from high-quality materials and are designed to fit perfectly. The price range for Peter England shirts varies from INR 699 to INR 2,000. You can buy Peter England shirts online or in-store.

If you're on a budget but still want a quality shirt, Peter England is the brand for you. The shirts are comfortable, stylish, and affordable, making them perfect for everyday wear. Although the quality may not be as premium as some of the other brands on this list, Peter England shirts are still a great investment.
