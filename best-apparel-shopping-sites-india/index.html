Best Apparel Shopping Sites In India Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 06, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of spending hours in crowded malls, trying on clothes in stuffy changing rooms, and still not finding what you're looking for? Well, look no further! In this guide, we'll give you the lowdown on the best apparel shopping sites in India, so you can shop from the comfort of your own home and find exactly what you want.

When it comes to online shopping, there are a few pain points to consider. Firstly, you can't physically try on the clothes before you buy them, so there's always a risk that they won't fit or look how you imagined. Secondly, with so many options available, it can be overwhelming to know where to start.

The target of this guide is to help you navigate the world of online apparel shopping in India, providing you with a comprehensive list of the best sites to visit, along with some tips and tricks to make your shopping experience a breeze.

In summary, we'll be covering the best apparel shopping sites in India, including their pros and cons, how to navigate them, and what makes them stand out. We'll also give you some tips on how to shop smart, so you can get the best deals and avoid any potential pitfalls.

1. Myntra

When it comes to online shopping, Myntra is one of the biggest names in India. With a vast range of clothing options, from trendy western wear to traditional ethnic wear, there's something for everyone on this site. What makes Myntra stand out is its user-friendly interface and excellent customer service. The site also frequently runs sales and discounts, so you can score some great deals.

Personally, I've had a great experience shopping on Myntra. The site is easy to navigate, and the clothes I've ordered have always been of good quality and fit well. One tip I have is to always check the size guide before ordering, as sizing can vary between brands.

2. Jabong

Jabong is another popular option for apparel shopping in India. Like Myntra, it offers a wide range of clothing options, along with accessories and shoes. What sets Jabong apart is its focus on designer wear, with options from both Indian and international designers. The site also offers free shipping on all orders, which is a big plus.

I've personally used Jabong to buy some special occasion outfits, and I was impressed with the quality of the clothes and the speedy delivery. However, I did find the site a bit overwhelming at first, with so many options to choose from. My advice is to use the filters to narrow down your search, and take advantage of the site's recommendations based on your past purchases.

3. Koovs

Koovs is a great option for those looking for affordable, trendy clothing. The site offers a curated selection of clothes, shoes, and accessories, with a focus on street style and current fashion trends. Koovs also has its own in-house brand, which offers some unique and affordable options.

I've used Koovs to buy some statement pieces, like bold printed dresses and quirky accessories. The clothes are generally of good quality for the price, but I would recommend reading reviews before making a purchase, as some items can run small or not be true to the pictures.

4. Ajio

Ajio is a more recent addition to the world of online apparel shopping, but it's quickly gaining popularity. The site offers a mix of Indian and western wear, with a focus on sustainable and ethical fashion. Ajio also has its own in-house brand, which offers some unique and affordable options.

I haven't personally used Ajio yet, but I've heard great things about their sustainable fashion options and customer service. One tip I have is to check out their "Indie" section, which features clothes and accessories from independent Indian designers.

Question and Answer:

Q: Are online apparel shopping sites in India safe to use?

A: Yes, most reputable online shopping sites in India are safe to use. However, it's always a good idea to check reviews and do some research before making a purchase, especially if it's your first time using a particular site.

Q: Can I return clothes if they don't fit or I don't like them?

A: Yes, most online shopping sites in India have a return policy that allows you to return or exchange clothes if they don't fit or you're not satisfied with them. However, it's important to read the terms and conditions of each site's return policy before making a purchase.

Q: How can I get the best deals on online apparel shopping sites in India?

A: One way to get the best deals is to sign up for the site's newsletter, which often includes exclusive discounts and sales. You can also keep an eye out for seasonal sales, like Diwali or Christmas sales, which offer deep discounts on clothing and accessories.

Q: Can I trust the sizing on online apparel shopping sites in India?

A: Sizing can vary between different brands and sites, so it's important to always check the size guide before making a purchase. You can also read reviews from other customers to get an idea of how the clothes fit.

Conclusion of Best Apparel Shopping Sites in India:

Online apparel shopping in India has come a long way in recent years, and there are now plenty of options available for all styles and budgets. By following our guide and using our tips, you can shop smart and find exactly what you're looking for. Happy shopping!
