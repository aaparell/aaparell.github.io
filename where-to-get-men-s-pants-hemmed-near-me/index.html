How to Measure Your Pants for Hemming

Before taking your pants to be hemmed, it's important to measure them properly. To measure your pants, follow these steps:

  1. Put on the shoes you plan to wear with the pants.
  2. Stand up straight and have someone measure from the bottom of the pant leg to the floor.
  3. Subtract 1 inch from the measurement to account for the distance between the floor and the bottom of your shoe.
  4. Take the pants to your chosen tailor or alterations shop and have them hemmed to the measurement you took.
