What Is The Average Salary Of An Interior Designer

Written by Eveline Jun 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering a career in interior design but wondering about the salary? Or perhaps you're already working in the field and want to know if you're earning what you're worth. Either way, understanding the average salary of an interior designer can help you make informed decisions about your career.

The target of interior designer salary average is to inform individuals who are interested in pursuing a career in interior design about the typical earnings they can expect. It can also help current interior designers evaluate their salary and negotiate for better pay.

In summary, the average salary of an interior designer in the United States is $56,040 per year, according to the Bureau of Labor Statistics. However, this number can vary depending on factors such as location, experience, and employer.

Factors Affecting Interior Designer Salary Average

When it comes to determining an interior designer's salary, several factors come into play. One of the most significant is location. Interior designers in large cities tend to earn more than those in smaller towns or rural areas. This is because there is typically more demand for their services in urban areas, and the cost of living is higher.

Another factor that can affect an interior designer's salary is experience. As with most professions, the longer someone has been working as an interior designer, the more they can expect to earn. Designers who have been in the field for several years and have a portfolio of successful projects can command higher salaries.

My Experience with Interior Designer Salary Average

As an interior designer with five years of experience working in a large city, I can attest to the impact of location and experience on salary. When I first started out, I earned around $45,000 per year. However, as I gained more experience and built up my portfolio, I was able to negotiate for higher pay. Today, I earn over $70,000 per year, which is well above the national average.

Negotiating for a Higher Interior Designer Salary Average

If you're currently working as an interior designer and feel that you're not earning what you're worth, it may be time to negotiate for a higher salary. Start by doing research on the average salary for interior designers in your location and with your level of experience. Then, schedule a meeting with your employer and make a case for why you deserve to earn more. This could include citing successful projects you've worked on, positive feedback from clients, or even a comparison to salaries of others in similar positions.

The Future of Interior Designer Salary Average

As the world becomes more focused on sustainability and eco-friendliness, interior designers who specialize in these areas may see an increase in demand for their services. Additionally, as technology continues to advance, designers who are proficient in using design software may have an edge in the job market.

Question and Answer

Q: Can interior designers make a six-figure salary?

A: Yes, it's possible for experienced and successful interior designers to earn six figures per year. However, this is not the norm and can vary greatly based on location and employer.

Q: Is a degree necessary to become an interior designer?

A: While a degree in interior design can be beneficial, it is not always necessary. Some designers have learned through apprenticeships or on-the-job training. However, having a degree can give you an edge in the job market and help you command a higher salary.

Q: Can interior designers work from home?

A: Yes, many interior designers work from home or have their own home-based design studio. However, depending on the type of projects they work on and the equipment they need, some designers may need to work in a traditional office setting.

Q: How do I find out the average salary for interior designers in my area?

A: The Bureau of Labor Statistics is a great resource for finding out the average salary for interior designers in your location. You can also check job search websites like Indeed or Glassdoor for salary information.

Conclusion of Interior Designer Salary Average

Overall, the average salary of an interior designer can vary greatly depending on several factors, including location, experience, and employer. By doing research and negotiating for higher pay, interior designers can ensure they are earning what they're worth. Additionally, keeping up with industry trends and specializing in areas like sustainability or technology can help designers stay competitive in the job market and potentially earn higher salaries.
