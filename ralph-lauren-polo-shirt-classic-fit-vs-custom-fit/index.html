Are you in the market for a new polo shirt? Do you find yourself torn between the classic fit and the custom fit from Ralph Lauren? Choosing between the two can be a daunting task, especially if you aren't sure what the differences are. In this article, we'll break down the pros and cons of each so that you can make an informed decision.

The classic fit polo shirt from Ralph Lauren is the traditional cut that most people are familiar with. It has a relaxed fit that flatters most body types and is perfect for everyday wear. However, some people find that the classic fit can be too baggy, especially around the waist and arms. If you're looking for a more tailored look, the custom fit may be a better option.

The custom fit polo shirt from Ralph Lauren is designed to be more fitted than the classic fit. It has a slimmer cut that hugs the body and is perfect for those who prefer a more modern, streamlined look. However, the custom fit can be less forgiving than the classic fit, so it's important to make sure you get the right size.

In summary, the classic fit polo shirt from Ralph Lauren is great for a relaxed, traditional look, while the custom fit is perfect for those who want a more fitted, modern look. When choosing between the two, it's important to consider your body type and personal style preferences.

The Classic Fit Polo Shirt

When it comes to the classic fit polo shirt from Ralph Lauren, I have to say that it's my personal favorite. I love the relaxed fit and find that it's perfect for casual occasions. I also appreciate that it's available in a variety of colors and patterns, so I can easily find one that matches my style.

One of the things I like best about the classic fit is that it's not too tight around the waist or arms. I find that it's more comfortable than the custom fit, which can feel restrictive at times. Additionally, the classic fit is more forgiving, so I don't have to worry as much about getting the perfect size.

The Custom Fit Polo Shirt

While I prefer the classic fit, I can see why some people might prefer the custom fit. It's definitely more fitted, which can be flattering if you have a slim build. I also appreciate that it looks more modern and streamlined.

However, I find that the custom fit can be less comfortable than the classic fit. It can feel restrictive around the waist and arms, which is something to keep in mind if you plan on wearing it for an extended period of time. Additionally, it's important to make sure you get the right size, as the custom fit is less forgiving than the classic fit.

Which One Should You Choose?

Ultimately, the choice between the classic fit and custom fit polo shirt from Ralph Lauren comes down to personal preference. If you prefer a relaxed, traditional look, then the classic fit is the way to go. If you want a more fitted, modern look, then the custom fit is the better option.
