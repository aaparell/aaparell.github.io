Question and Answer

Q. What should I consider before buying a perfume?

A. You should consider the occasion, your personality, and the fragrance notes before buying a perfume.

Q. How long do perfumes last?

A. Perfumes can last up to 6-8 hours, depending on the fragrance concentration and your body chemistry.

Q. What are the fragrance notes?

A. Fragrance notes are the different layers of scents that make up a perfume. They can be classified into three categories: top notes, middle notes, and base notes.

Q. How can I make my perfume last longer?

A. You can make your perfume last longer by applying it on your pulse points, moisturizing your skin before applying, and layering it with a matching body lotion or oil.
