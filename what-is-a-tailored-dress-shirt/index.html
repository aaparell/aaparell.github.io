What Is A Tailored Dress Shirt A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Jun 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of wearing dress shirts that don't fit you properly? Do you want to look sharp and stylish in your formal attire? If yes, then a tailored dress shirt is what you need. In this article, we will explain everything you need to know about tailored dress shirts.

Pain Points

Most people struggle with finding dress shirts that fit them perfectly. Off-the-rack shirts are made to fit a general body type, which may not match yours. This often results in an ill-fitting shirt that is either too tight or too loose in some areas.

What is a Tailored Dress Shirt?

A tailored dress shirt is a shirt that is made specifically for you, taking into consideration your unique body shape and measurements. It is designed to fit you perfectly, ensuring maximum comfort and style. A tailored dress shirt is made from high-quality fabrics and is crafted to perfection.

Main Points

Tailored dress shirts are made to measure, which means that they are customized to fit your body perfectly. They are made from high-quality materials, such as Egyptian cotton or silk, which ensure maximum comfort and durability. Tailored dress shirts also come in a variety of styles, colors, and patterns, allowing you to express your personal style.

Why Choose a Tailored Dress Shirt?

If you want to look your best in formal attire, then a tailored dress shirt is a must-have. It offers several benefits over off-the-rack shirts:

1. Perfect Fit: A tailored dress shirt is made to fit you perfectly, ensuring maximum comfort and style.

2. High-Quality Materials: Tailored dress shirts are made from high-quality fabrics, such as Egyptian cotton, which ensure maximum comfort and durability.

3. Personal Style: Tailored dress shirts come in a variety of styles, colors, and patterns, allowing you to express your personal style.

How to Get a Tailored Dress Shirt?

You can get a tailored dress shirt by visiting a tailor or a made-to-measure shirt company. When you visit a tailor, they will take your measurements and create a shirt specifically for you. If you choose a made-to-measure company, you can either visit their store or order online.

How to Take Care of Your Tailored Dress Shirt?

To ensure the longevity of your tailored dress shirt, you should follow these care instructions:

1. Wash your shirt in cold water and hang dry it.

2. Iron your shirt on a low heat setting.

3. Store your shirt on a hanger, rather than folding it.

FAQs About Tailored Dress Shirts

Q: How much does a tailored dress shirt cost?

A: The cost of a tailored dress shirt varies depending on the materials used and the complexity of the design. It can range from $50 to $500 or more.

Q: How long does it take to get a tailored dress shirt?

A: The time it takes to get a tailored dress shirt depends on the tailor or made-to-measure company you choose. It can take anywhere from a few days to a few weeks.

Q: Can I customize the design of my tailored dress shirt?

A: Yes, you can customize the design of your tailored dress shirt. You can choose the fabric, color, pattern, collar style, cuff style, and more.

Q: Are tailored dress shirts only for men?

A: No, tailored dress shirts are available for both men and women.

Conclusion of What is a Tailored Dress Shirt

A tailored dress shirt is a must-have for anyone who wants to look their best in formal attire. It offers several benefits over off-the-rack shirts, such as a perfect fit, high-quality materials, and personal style. You can get a tailored dress shirt by visiting a tailor or a made-to-measure company. To ensure the longevity of your tailored dress shirt, follow the care instructions provided by the manufacturer.
