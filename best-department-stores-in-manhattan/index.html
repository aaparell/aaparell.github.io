Best Department Stores In Manhattan A Guide For Shoppers

Written by Oliver Jul 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you planning a shopping trip to Manhattan? The city has no shortage of department stores, but finding the best ones can be overwhelming. In this guide, we'll take a look at some of the top department stores in Manhattan and what makes them stand out.

Whether you're a seasoned shopper or a first-time visitor to the city, you're sure to find something that appeals to you in Manhattan's department stores. But with so many options, it can be hard to know where to start. That's where this guide comes in.

So, what are the best department stores in Manhattan? Let's take a look.

In summary, the best department stores in Manhattan offer a wide range of products, from high-end designer fashion to affordable basics. They also provide a unique shopping experience, with stunning architecture, excellent customer service, and a sense of history and tradition.

Macy's

Macy's is one of the most iconic department stores in Manhattan, and for good reason. Located in Herald Square, the store covers an entire city block and offers a huge variety of products, from clothing and shoes to home goods and beauty products.

One of the best things about Macy's is the range of price points. You can find high-end designer brands, but there are also plenty of affordable options. The store also has a rich history, having been around since 1858.

Personally, I love shopping at Macy's during the holiday season. The store is beautifully decorated, and the window displays are always a treat to see.

Bloomingdale's

If you're looking for a high-end shopping experience, Bloomingdale's is a must-visit. The store is located on 59th Street and Lexington Avenue and offers a wide range of designer brands, from Gucci to Chanel.

One thing that sets Bloomingdale's apart is its excellent customer service. The staff are knowledgeable and friendly, and they go out of their way to make your shopping experience enjoyable.

I recently visited Bloomingdale's to buy a wedding gift, and the staff were incredibly helpful in finding the perfect item. It made the whole experience stress-free and enjoyable.

Saks Fifth Avenue

Saks Fifth Avenue is another high-end department store that's worth a visit. Located on Fifth Avenue, the store is known for its luxury fashion and accessories, including brands like Prada and Saint Laurent.

But Saks isn't just about high-end fashion. The store also has a great selection of beauty products, home goods, and gifts. And like Bloomingdale's, the customer service is top-notch.

I've shopped at Saks several times, and I've always been impressed with the quality of the products and the level of customer service. It's definitely a store that's worth splurging on.

Century 21

If you're looking for a bargain, Century 21 is the place to go. The store offers discounted designer fashion, home goods, and accessories, and you can often find amazing deals on high-end brands.

I recently bought a designer dress at Century 21 for a fraction of the retail price. It was a great find, and I always make sure to stop by the store when I'm in Manhattan.

Question and Answer

Q: What should I look for when shopping at a department store in Manhattan?

A: When shopping at a department store in Manhattan, look for a wide range of products, excellent customer service, and a unique shopping experience. You should also consider the price points, as some stores cater to high-end shoppers while others offer more affordable options.

Q: Are there any department stores in Manhattan that offer discount prices?

A: Yes, Century 21 is a department store in Manhattan that offers discounted designer fashion, home goods, and accessories. It's a great place to find high-end brands at lower prices.

Q: What should I wear when shopping at a department store in Manhattan?

A: There's no dress code for shopping at a department store in Manhattan, but it's always a good idea to dress comfortably and wear shoes that are easy to walk in. You may also want to bring a tote bag or backpack to carry your purchases.

Q: Can I find unique or one-of-a-kind items at department stores in Manhattan?

A: Yes, many department stores in Manhattan offer unique or one-of-a-kind items, especially in their home goods and gift sections. It's worth taking the time to explore the store and see what treasures you can find.

Conclusion of Best Department Stores in Manhattan

Whether you're a local or a visitor, shopping in Manhattan's department stores is an experience like no other. From Macy's to Bloomingdale's to Saks Fifth Avenue and Century 21, there's something for everyone. So grab your shopping bags and get ready to explore some of the best department stores in Manhattan.
