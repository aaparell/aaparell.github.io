How to Measure Your Neck for 15 1 2 Shirt Size

If you're unsure of your neck size, you can easily measure it at home. All you need is a tape measure. Here's how to measure your neck for 15 1 2 shirt size:

Step 1: Stand Up Straight

Stand up straight with your shoulders relaxed and your head facing forward. Make sure that the tape measure is level around your neck, not too tight or too loose.

Step 2: Find the Base of Your Neck

Place the tape measure around the base of your neck, where your collar would sit. Make sure that the tape measure is straight and not twisted.

Step 3: Take the Measurement

Read the measurement on the tape measure in inches. This measurement is your neck size, which you can use to determine your 15 1 2 shirt size.

Tips for Finding the Right Fit

When shopping for a 15 1 2 shirt size, it's important to consider not only your neck size but also your body type. Here are some tips for finding the right fit:

  • Choose a shirt that fits snugly around your neck without feeling too tight.
  • Make sure that the shirt isn't too loose or too tight around the chest and waist.
  • Check the length of the shirt to ensure that it's not too long or too short on your body.
  • Consider the style of the shirt, as some styles may fit differently than others.

FAQs About 15 1 2 Shirt Size

Q: Is 15 1 2 shirt size the same as my collar size?
