Where To Find The Best Sites For Vintage Clothing A Guide

Written by Robby Jun 20, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you love vintage clothing but struggle to find the best sites to shop from? Are you tired of sifting through endless pages of outdated and overpriced options? Look no further! In this guide, we will provide you with a list of the top sites for vintage clothing, so you can easily find unique and affordable pieces to add to your wardrobe.

If you're a vintage clothing enthusiast, you know that finding the perfect piece can be a challenge. It can take hours of scrolling through pages and pages of clothing to find what you're looking for, and even then, the prices can be astronomical. It can be frustrating to have a passion for vintage clothing but not know where to find the best deals or the most unique pieces.

Our guide is here to help answer those questions and provide you with a list of the best sites for vintage clothing. Whether you're looking for a funky 70's jumpsuit or a romantic Victorian blouse, we've got you covered.

In this article, we will provide you with a list of the top sites for vintage clothing, along with a brief description of each site's target audience and style. We will also share personal experiences and tips on how to make the most out of your vintage shopping experience. So, let's get started!

The Best Sites for Vintage Clothing: Etsy

Etsy is one of the most popular sites for vintage clothing, and for a good reason. This platform offers a wide range of vintage clothing from various eras, styles, and price points. The sellers on Etsy are often small businesses or independent sellers, so you can be sure that you're supporting a great cause while adding a unique piece to your collection.

Personally, I have found some of my favorite vintage pieces on Etsy. Some tips for shopping on Etsy include using specific keywords to narrow down your search and checking the seller's reviews before making a purchase. You can find anything from a 1920's beaded flapper dress to a 90's grunge-inspired outfit on Etsy.

The Best Sites for Vintage Clothing: Depop

Depop is another popular platform for vintage clothing, especially for younger audiences. This app allows users to create a virtual store to sell their vintage clothing, making it easy to find unique pieces from all over the world. Depop is known for its streetwear and 90's-inspired clothing, making it a great option for those looking for a more casual vintage look.

I have personally found some great vintage t-shirts and denim jackets on Depop. Some tips for shopping on Depop include checking the seller's reviews and asking for additional photos or measurements before making a purchase.

The Best Sites for Vintage Clothing: The RealReal

The RealReal is a luxury consignment site that offers vintage designer clothing, including brands like Chanel, Gucci, and Saint Laurent. While this site is on the pricier side, it's a great option for those looking for high-quality vintage pieces that are still in excellent condition. The RealReal offers a rigorous authentication process to ensure that all items are authentic.

The Best Sites for Vintage Clothing: Beyond Retro

Beyond Retro is a UK-based vintage clothing store that offers a wide range of vintage pieces from the 60s, 70s, and 80s. This store is known for its unique pieces and vintage-inspired clothing, making it a great option for those looking for a more eclectic style. Beyond Retro also offers its own line of vintage-inspired clothing for those who want a modern take on vintage fashion.

Question and Answer

Q: Are there any other sites you would recommend for vintage clothing?

A: Yes! Other great options include Asos Marketplace, Poshmark, and Vestiaire Collective.

Q: How do I know if a vintage piece is worth the price?

A: It's essential to do your research and compare prices on other sites. Additionally, factors like the condition, rarity, and brand can all affect the value of a vintage piece.

Q: What are some tips for caring for vintage clothing?

A: It's important to read the care instructions on the label and avoid machine washing or drying vintage pieces. Additionally, storing vintage clothing in a cool, dry place can help prevent damage or yellowing.

Q: Can I sell my vintage clothing on these sites?

A: Yes! Many of these sites allow users to create a store and sell their vintage clothing to others.

Conclusion of Best Sites for Vintage Clothing

Overall, there are plenty of options for finding unique and affordable vintage clothing online. From Etsy to The RealReal, each site offers a different target audience and style, so it's essential to do your research and find the site that best fits your needs. With a little bit of effort and patience, you can find some incredible vintage pieces that will add a unique touch to your wardrobe.
