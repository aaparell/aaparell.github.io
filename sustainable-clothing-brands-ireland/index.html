The Top 10 Sustainable Clothing Brands in Ireland

1. ATRIUM

ATRIUM is a sustainable fashion brand that creates timeless and versatile clothing pieces. They use eco-friendly materials such as organic cotton, linen, and Tencel. Their clothes are made to last, and they focus on reducing waste by using deadstock fabrics and creating limited collections.

2. Re-dress

Re-dress is a sustainable fashion brand that focuses on educating consumers about sustainable fashion. They offer a range of sustainable fashion courses, workshops, and events. Their clothes are made from eco-friendly materials such as organic cotton, linen, and recycled polyester.

3. Six Items Challenge

Six Items Challenge is a social experiment that challenges participants to wear only six items of clothing for six weeks. The challenge aims to raise awareness about the impact of fast fashion on the environment and encourage people to buy less and choose sustainable fashion brands.

4. The Ethical Silk Company

The Ethical Silk Company is a sustainable fashion brand that creates luxurious silk clothing and accessories. They use ethically sourced silk and organic cotton to create their clothes, and they focus on providing fair wages and working conditions for their workers.

5. The Zip Yard

The Zip Yard is a sustainable fashion brand that provides alteration and repair services for clothes. They help customers to extend the life of their clothes and reduce waste by repairing and altering clothes instead of throwing them away.

Thought Clothing is a sustainable fashion brand that creates clothes from eco-friendly materials such as bamboo, hemp, and organic cotton. They focus on providing fair wages and working conditions for their workers and reducing their impact on the environment.

Grown Clothing is a sustainable fashion brand that creates clothes from eco-friendly materials such as organic cotton, recycled polyester, and Tencel. They focus on providing fair wages and working conditions for their workers and reducing their impact on the environment.
