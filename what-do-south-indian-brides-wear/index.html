What Do South Indian Brides Wear

Written by Frank Jun 14, 2023 · 5 min read

South Indian brides are known for their vibrant and traditional attire, but many people may not know what exactly they wear on their big day. For those who are unfamiliar with South Indian weddings, it can be overwhelming to navigate through the vast array of clothing options available.

Choosing the right outfit for a South Indian bride is crucial as it is an important part of their culture and tradition. There are several pain points that brides may face when deciding what to wear, such as finding the right color scheme, selecting the perfect jewelry, and ensuring the outfit is comfortable and practical.

When it comes to what South Indian brides wear, there are a few key elements that make up their traditional attire. The main outfit is a saree or a lehenga, which is a long skirt paired with a blouse and a dupatta. The saree is a long piece of cloth draped around the body, while the lehenga is a skirt with an attached blouse.

In addition to the saree or lehenga, South Indian brides also typically wear gold jewelry, including necklaces, earrings, bangles, and anklets. The jewelry is an essential part of the bride's attire and is often passed down from generation to generation.

In summary, South Indian brides wear sarees or lehengas paired with gold jewelry on their wedding day. The outfit is an important part of their culture and tradition, and brides spend a lot of time and effort selecting the perfect attire to wear on their big day.

What Makes Up South Indian Bridal Attire?

As a South Indian bride myself, I can vouch for the fact that selecting the perfect wedding outfit can be a daunting task. The process involves several steps, including choosing the right color, material, and style.

The saree is the most popular choice for South Indian brides. It is a long piece of cloth that is draped around the body in a specific way to create a beautiful and elegant look. The saree is usually made of silk, which is considered auspicious and is available in a variety of colors and designs.

Another popular choice for South Indian brides is the lehenga. It consists of a long skirt paired with a blouse and a dupatta. The lehenga is often heavily embroidered and embellished with stones and beads, making it a glamorous and eye-catching option.

South Indian brides also typically wear gold jewelry on their wedding day. The jewelry is an essential part of the bride's attire and is often passed down from generation to generation. It includes necklaces, earrings, bangles, and anklets, and is chosen to complement the outfit and enhance the overall look.

How to Choose the Perfect South Indian Bridal Attire?

Choosing the perfect South Indian bridal attire involves several factors, including the color, material, and style of the outfit. It is essential to choose an outfit that complements your skin tone and body type.

When selecting the color of your outfit, it is important to consider the time of day and the venue of your wedding. Bright and vibrant colors such as red, pink, and orange are popular choices for daytime weddings, while darker colors such as maroon and navy blue are preferred for evening weddings.

The material of the outfit is also crucial. Silk is the most popular material for sarees as it is considered auspicious and adds a touch of elegance to the attire. When it comes to lehengas, materials such as velvet and net are often used to create a more dramatic and glamorous look.

Finally, it is important to consider the style of the outfit. Traditional South Indian bridal attire is often heavily embroidered and embellished with stones and beads. However, modern brides often prefer a more subtle and minimalist look.

What Accessories Do South Indian Brides Wear?

In addition to the outfit and jewelry, South Indian brides also wear several accessories to complete their look. The most popular accessory is the maang tikka, which is a piece of jewelry worn on the forehead. It is often made of gold and embellished with stones and beads.

Another popular accessory is the nose ring, which is worn on the left nostril. It is usually made of gold and is often embellished with diamonds or other precious stones.

South Indian brides also wear a set of toe rings, which are worn on the second toe of each foot. The toe rings are usually made of silver and are considered a symbol of marriage.

Conclusion of What Do South Indian Brides Wear

Choosing the perfect attire for your wedding day is crucial, and South Indian brides have a vast array of options to choose from. From the brightly colored sarees to the heavily embroidered lehengas, it is essential to select an outfit that complements your skin tone and body type.

South Indian brides also wear gold jewelry and several accessories such as maang tikka, nose ring, and toe rings to complete their look. By considering the color, material, and style of the outfit, brides can create a beautiful and elegant look that reflects their culture and tradition.
