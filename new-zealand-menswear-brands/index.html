New Zealand Menswear Brands A Guide To Style And Quality

Written by Petter Oct 25, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old boring menswear options? Are you looking for something new and exciting to spice up your wardrobe? Look no further than the amazing selection of New Zealand menswear brands. These brands offer unique styles and high-quality materials that will make you stand out from the crowd.

Many men struggle to find clothing that fits well and looks good. They may find that traditional brands are too generic or don't offer enough variety. New Zealand menswear brands aim to solve this problem by offering a wide range of styles, fits, and materials to suit any taste or preference.

So, why should you consider New Zealand menswear brands? For starters, they offer a fresh perspective on men's fashion. Many of these brands are inspired by New Zealand's rugged landscapes and natural beauty, resulting in unique designs that incorporate earthy tones, organic shapes, and natural materials.

Overall, New Zealand menswear brands are known for their attention to detail, quality craftsmanship, and innovative designs. They offer a refreshing alternative to traditional men's fashion and are a great choice for anyone looking to add some personality and style to their wardrobe.

1. Rodd & Gunn

My personal favorite New Zealand menswear brand is Rodd & Gunn. This brand offers a wide range of clothing and accessories for men, including jackets, shirts, pants, and shoes. Their designs are inspired by the New Zealand landscape, and they use high-quality materials like merino wool and leather to create durable and stylish pieces.

One of my favorite pieces from Rodd & Gunn is their Quail Island jacket. This jacket is made from high-quality leather and features a unique design inspired by traditional New Zealand hunting jackets. It's the perfect mix of style and functionality and is sure to turn heads wherever you go.

2. Barkers

Barkers is another popular New Zealand menswear brand that offers a wide range of clothing and accessories for men. Their designs are classic and timeless, with a focus on quality materials and craftsmanship. They offer everything from suits and dress shirts to casual wear and accessories.

One of my favorite pieces from Barkers is their navy pinstripe suit. This suit is made from high-quality wool and features a classic design that is perfect for any formal occasion. It's a timeless piece that will never go out of style.

3. Working Style

Working Style is a New Zealand menswear brand that focuses on tailored suits and dress shirts. They offer a wide range of styles and fits to suit any body type or preference, and they use high-quality materials like Italian wool and Egyptian cotton to create their pieces.

If you're in the market for a new suit, I highly recommend checking out Working Style. Their suits are expertly tailored and feature unique details like contrast stitching and patterned linings. They also offer made-to-measure options for a truly custom fit.

4. I Love Ugly

I Love Ugly is a New Zealand menswear brand that offers a more casual, streetwear-inspired style. They offer a wide range of clothing and accessories for men, including graphic tees, hoodies, and sneakers.

If you're looking for something more casual and comfortable, I Love Ugly is a great choice. Their pieces are stylish and edgy, with a focus on bold graphics and unique designs.

Conclusion of New Zealand Menswear Brands

New Zealand menswear brands offer a unique perspective on men's fashion, with a focus on quality materials, innovative designs, and attention to detail. Whether you're looking for a tailored suit or a casual hoodie, there is a New Zealand menswear brand that is perfect for you. So, why not add some style and personality to your wardrobe with a piece from one of these amazing brands?

Question and Answer

Q: What makes New Zealand menswear brands unique?

A: New Zealand menswear brands are unique because they draw inspiration from the natural beauty and rugged landscapes of New Zealand. They often use organic shapes, earthy tones, and natural materials in their designs, resulting in a fresh and unique perspective on men's fashion.

Q: Are New Zealand menswear brands expensive?

A: It depends on the brand and the specific piece you are looking at. Some New Zealand menswear brands are more affordable than others, but in general, they tend to be slightly more expensive than traditional men's fashion brands. However, the higher price point is often justified by the quality of the materials and craftsmanship.

Q: Where can I buy New Zealand menswear brands?

A: Many New Zealand menswear brands have their own stores or stockists in New Zealand and Australia. However, you can also find many of these brands online, either through their own websites or through retailers that specialize in New Zealand fashion.

Q: Are New Zealand menswear brands sustainable?

A: Many New Zealand menswear brands prioritize sustainability and ethical production practices. They often use eco-friendly materials and production methods, and they prioritize fair labor practices and working conditions for their employees.
