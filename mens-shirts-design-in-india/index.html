Mens Shirts Design In India A Guide To The Latest Trends And Styles

Written by Christine Jul 09, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same old boring shirts every day? Do you want to spice up your wardrobe with the latest trends and styles in mens shirts design? Look no further than the diverse and vibrant fashion scene in India.

Indian fashion is known for its bold and colorful designs, intricate embroidery, and attention to detail. However, navigating the world of mens shirts design in India can be overwhelming, with so many options to choose from and varying levels of quality and craftsmanship.

So what should you look for when shopping for men's shirts in India?

First and foremost, consider the occasion and setting in which you will be wearing the shirt. Are you looking for a casual shirt to wear on the weekends, or a formal shirt for a wedding or business event? Different styles and fabrics are appropriate for different settings.

Secondly, pay attention to the fit of the shirt. A well-fitted shirt can make all the difference in your overall appearance and confidence. Make sure to try on different sizes and styles to find the one that suits your body type best.

In summary, when shopping for mens shirts design in India, consider the occasion and setting, as well as the fit of the shirt. Now, let's dive into some of the latest trends and styles in Indian mens shirt design.

The Classic Kurta

One of the most popular and timeless styles in Indian mens shirt design is the kurta. The kurta is a long, loose-fitting shirt that is typically made of cotton or silk and features intricate embroidery or embellishments. It can be worn casually or dressed up for formal occasions, making it a versatile addition to any wardrobe.

Personally, I love wearing my kurta with a pair of jeans and sandals for a comfortable and stylish look. It's also a great option for hot summer days, as the breathable fabric keeps you cool and comfortable.

The Modern Nehru Jacket

The Nehru jacket is another classic Indian menswear item that has been given a modern update in recent years. The jacket is named after Jawaharlal Nehru, the first Prime Minister of India, who was known for his signature style of wearing a jacket without a tie.

The modern Nehru jacket is a versatile piece that can be worn over a kurta or paired with a shirt and trousers for a more formal look. It typically features a Mandarin collar, button-down front, and sleek, fitted silhouette.

I recently wore a navy blue Nehru jacket with a white shirt and black trousers to a wedding, and received many compliments on my unique and stylish outfit.

The Bold Printed Shirt

For those who want to make a statement with their mens shirts design in India, look no further than the bold printed shirt trend. These shirts feature eye-catching prints and patterns, from floral to geometric to animal prints, and can be worn with solid-colored pants or shorts for a fun and playful look.

However, it's important to be careful not to go overboard with the prints. Stick to one statement piece and keep the rest of your outfit simple and understated.

How to Accessorize Your Indian Mens Shirt

Accessories can elevate your mens shirt design in India to the next level. Consider adding a statement watch, leather belt, or pocket square to your outfit for a touch of sophistication.

I personally love adding a colorful beaded bracelet or necklace to my kurta for a bohemian vibe, or a pair of aviator sunglasses for a classic and cool look.

The Future of Mens Shirts Design in India

The fashion industry in India is constantly evolving and pushing boundaries, and menswear is no exception. We can expect to see more experimentation with fabrics, prints, and styles in the coming years, as designers continue to blend traditional Indian techniques with modern aesthetics.

One trend to watch out for is sustainable and eco-friendly fashion, as consumers become more conscious of their impact on the environment. Many Indian designers are already incorporating sustainable practices into their mens shirts design, using organic fabrics and natural dyes.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the most popular fabric for mens shirts in India?

A: Cotton is the most popular fabric for mens shirts in India, as it is lightweight, breathable, and comfortable in hot and humid weather.

Q: Can I wear a kurta to a formal event?

A: Yes, a well-fitted and embellished kurta can be a great option for a formal event, especially if paired with a tailored jacket or Nehru jacket.

Q: Are there any colors or prints that are considered inappropriate for mens shirts in India?

A: It's generally best to avoid wearing white to weddings, as it is traditionally associated with mourning in Indian culture. Additionally, wearing religious or political symbols on your shirt may be considered inappropriate or offensive.

Q: How can I ensure the quality of my mens shirt in India?

A: Look for shirts that are made from high-quality fabrics and feature strong stitching and attention to detail. Additionally, consider purchasing from a reputable designer or brand with a track record of quality and craftsmanship.

Conclusion of Mens Shirts Design in India

Mens shirts design in India offers a wealth of options for those looking to add some color and style to their wardrobe. From classic kurtas to modern Nehru jackets to bold printed shirts, there is something for every occasion and style preference.

Remember to consider the occasion and fit of the shirt when shopping, and don't be afraid to experiment with accessories to take your outfit to the next level. With the constantly evolving fashion scene in India, the future of mens shirts design is bright and full of possibility.
