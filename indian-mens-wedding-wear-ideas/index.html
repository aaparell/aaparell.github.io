Indian Mens Wedding Wear Ideas Tips And Inspiration

Written by Christine Jul 12, 2023 · 5 min read

When it comes to weddings, there's no denying that the bride usually steals the show. But that doesn't mean the groom can't dress to impress! Indian weddings are known for their grandeur and opulence, with intricate decorations and lavish outfits. But for men, figuring out what to wear can be a daunting task. From choosing the right color to finding the perfect fit, there are plenty of factors to consider. In this post, we'll share some Indian mens wedding wear ideas to help you look your best on your big day.

As a groom, you want to look your best on your wedding day. But with so many options to choose from, it can be overwhelming. You may be wondering what style of outfit to wear, what colors to choose, or how to balance tradition with modern trends. These are all valid concerns, but with a little guidance, you can find the perfect outfit that reflects your personality and makes you feel confident.

So, what should you wear to an Indian wedding? The answer depends on a few factors, such as the time of day, the venue, and the theme of the wedding. But in general, Indian mens wedding wear usually consists of a sherwani or a kurta pajama with a dupatta (scarf) and a turban. The color and style of the outfit can vary depending on personal preference and cultural traditions.

Overall, there are a few key things to keep in mind when choosing Indian mens wedding wear. First, consider the formality of the event. A daytime wedding may call for a lighter color and fabric, while an evening wedding may require a more formal, darker outfit. Second, think about your own personal style and what makes you feel comfortable and confident. Finally, don't be afraid to incorporate modern trends into your outfit, such as bold prints or unique accessories.

Indian Mens Wedding Wear Idea 1: Sherwani

One of the most popular options for Indian mens wedding wear is the sherwani. A sherwani is a long, coat-like outfit that is usually worn with a churidar pajama (a fitted pant) and a dupatta. Sherwanis can be simple or heavily embellished with embroidery, beads, or sequins. They come in a variety of fabrics, including silk, velvet, and cotton.

For my own wedding, I chose to wear a navy blue sherwani with gold embroidery. It was the perfect balance of traditional and modern, and I felt comfortable and confident all day long. If you're considering a sherwani for your own wedding, I recommend trying on a few different styles to find the right fit and fabric for you.

Indian Mens Wedding Wear Idea 2: Kurta Pajama

If you're looking for something a little more comfortable and casual, a kurta pajama may be a good option. A kurta is a long tunic-style shirt that is usually paired with a pajama (a loose-fitting pant) or a dhoti (a traditional garment that is wrapped around the legs). Kurta pajamas come in a variety of colors and fabrics, from simple cotton to luxurious silk.

For a daytime wedding, a light-colored kurta pajama can be a great choice. You can dress it up with a colorful dupatta or a statement accessory, like a beaded necklace or a turban. For a more formal evening wedding, a darker-colored kurta pajama with gold or silver embroidery can be a showstopper.

Indian Mens Wedding Wear Idea 3: Accessorize

No outfit is complete without the right accessories! When it comes to Indian mens wedding wear, there are plenty of options to choose from. A turban is a traditional head covering that can add a touch of elegance to any outfit. You can choose a turban that matches your outfit or opt for a contrasting color for a bold look. Jewelry is also an important accessory for Indian weddings. A beaded necklace, bracelet, or earrings can add a pop of color and personality to your outfit.

Indian Mens Wedding Wear Idea 4: Shoes

Don't forget about your shoes! Indian mens wedding wear usually requires a formal pair of shoes, such as juttis or mojris. These are traditional Indian shoes that are made of leather or fabric and are often embellished with embroidery or beads. They come in a variety of colors and styles, so you can choose a pair that complements your outfit.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a suit to an Indian wedding?

A: While it's not traditional Indian mens wedding wear, you can certainly wear a suit if you prefer. Just make sure it's a formal suit that is appropriate for the occasion.

Q: What colors are appropriate for Indian mens wedding wear?

A: In general, brighter colors like red, gold, and green are popular for Indian mens wedding wear. However, you can also choose darker colors like navy, black, or maroon for a more formal look.

Q: How do I choose the right fit for my outfit?

A: It's important to try on different sizes and styles to find the right fit for you. Make sure the outfit is comfortable and allows for movement, but is still fitted enough to flatter your body.

Q: Can I mix and match different styles?

A: Absolutely! Indian mens wedding wear is all about personal style and creativity. You can mix and match different colors, fabrics, and styles to create a unique look that reflects your personality.

Conclusion of Indian Mens Wedding Wear Ideas

Choosing the perfect outfit for your wedding day can be a challenge, but with these Indian mens wedding wear ideas, you'll be on your way to looking and feeling your best. Remember to consider the formality of the event, your personal style, and any cultural traditions when choosing your outfit. And don't forget to accessorize with the right turban, jewelry, and shoes to complete your look. With a little guidance and a lot of confidence, you'll be the most stylish groom at the wedding!
