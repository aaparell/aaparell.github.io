Mens Formal Wear in Edmonton: Tips for Choosing the Right Fit

When it comes to choosing the right formal wear, fit is everything. A poorly fitting suit or dress shirt can ruin your look, no matter how stylish or expensive it is. Here are some tips for finding the right fit:

  • Make sure the shoulders of your jacket fit well and don't sag or bunch up.
  • The sleeves of your jacket should end at your wrist bone, and your shirt cuffs should show about half an inch.
  • Your pants should fit well around your waist and not be too tight or too loose.
  • The hem of your pants should just touch the top of your shoes.

Mens Formal Wear in Edmonton: How to Care for Your Formal Wear

  • Use a steamer or iron on a low setting to remove wrinkles.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the difference between a tuxedo and a suit?
A: A tuxedo is typically worn for formal occasions like weddings or prom, while a suit is more appropriate for business events or less formal occasions. Tuxedos often have satin lapels and a satin stripe down the side of the pants, while suits do not.

Q: How should I choose the color of my suit?
A: The color of your suit should match the occasion and your personal style. For business events, a navy or gray suit is appropriate, while for weddings or other formal occasions, a black or dark blue suit is more appropriate. You can also choose a suit color based on your skin tone and hair color.

Q: How should I choose the size of my dress shirt?
A: The size of your dress shirt should be based on your neck size and sleeve length. Measure your neck with a tape measure and add half an inch for comfort. Your sleeve length should be measured from the center of your back to your wrist bone.

Q: Can I wear a tuxedo to a business event?
A: While it is not appropriate to wear a tuxedo to a business event, you can wear a suit or a dress shirt and trousers. Make sure your outfit is clean, pressed, and fits well.
