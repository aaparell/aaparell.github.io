Jogger pants are a more casual option that is perfect for those lazy days at home or running errands. These pants are made from soft materials like cotton and have an elastic waistband and cuffs, making them comfortable and easy to wear.

Wide-leg trousers have become increasingly popular in recent years. These pants are perfect for those who want to make a fashion statement. They are comfortable and stylish, making them perfect for any occasion.
