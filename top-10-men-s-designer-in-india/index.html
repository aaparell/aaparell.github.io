Sabyasachi Mukherjee is a renowned fashion designer based in Kolkata, India. He is best known for his fusion of traditional and modern designs, and his use of bold colors and intricate embroidery. Abu Jani and Sandeep Khosla are a designer duo based in Mumbai, India. They are known for their use of traditional Indian techniques and modern designs.
