Do Sneakers Go With Dresses A Style Guide For The Fashion Forward

Written by Aprilia Oct 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of wearing heels or flats with your dresses? Do you want to switch up your style and add some edge to your outfit? Look no further than pairing sneakers with your dresses. However, the question remains: do sneakers go with dresses?

Many people are hesitant to pair sneakers with dresses due to the traditional idea that dresses should be worn with heels or flats. Others worry that sneakers will make their outfit look too casual or sporty. However, with the right styling, sneakers can be the perfect addition to any dress.

The answer is yes, sneakers can be worn with dresses. In fact, it's a trend that has been embraced by fashion influencers and celebrities alike. By following a few simple guidelines, you can rock this trend with confidence.

When pairing sneakers with dresses, the key is balance. You want to create a look that is both comfortable and stylish. Start by choosing a dress that is simple and not too busy. Neutral colors and solid prints work best. Then, choose a pair of sneakers that complement the color and style of the dress. White sneakers are a safe bet and go with almost any outfit.

My Personal Experience: Pairing Sneakers with a Dress

Recently, I attended a wedding where I decided to pair my black midi dress with a pair of white sneakers. At first, I was hesitant to break away from the traditional heels or flats, but I decided to take a chance. I received so many compliments on my outfit and felt comfortable dancing the night away without worrying about sore feet. It was a great decision and I plan on rocking this trend more often.

How to Style Sneakers with Dresses

Another way to style sneakers with dresses is to play with textures and patterns. Pair a floral dress with a pair of leather sneakers for an unexpected contrast. Or, try a denim dress with white canvas sneakers for a casual yet chic look. Don't be afraid to mix and match.

Choosing the Right Sneakers for Your Dress

When choosing sneakers to wear with your dress, consider the occasion. If you're going to a formal event, opt for a pair of sleek, minimalist sneakers. For a casual look, choose a pair of sneakers with a bold print or vibrant color. It's all about finding the right balance between comfort and style.

How to Accessorize Sneakers with Dresses

When accessorizing with sneakers and dresses, keep it simple. A statement necklace or earrings can add some sparkle to your outfit without taking away from the sneakers. Avoid adding too many accessories, as it can make the outfit look cluttered. A simple clutch or crossbody bag is all you need to complete the look.

My Personal Experience: Dressing Up Sneakers for a Night Out

I love to dress up my sneakers for a night out. Recently, I paired a little black dress with a pair of metallic silver sneakers for a fun and edgy look. I added a bold red lip and some statement earrings to complete the outfit. It was a great way to stand out while still being comfortable and practical.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear sneakers with a maxi dress?

A: Yes! Sneakers can be paired with any dress length, including maxi dresses. Just make sure to choose sneakers that complement the style and color of the dress.

Q: Are there any types of dresses that don't work with sneakers?

A: It's all about balance. Avoid pairing sneakers with overly formal or structured dresses, such as ballgowns or cocktail dresses.

Q: How can I make sure my sneakers look clean and polished?

A: Keep your sneakers clean and scuff-free by regularly wiping them down with a damp cloth. You can also invest in a shoe cleaner or protector to keep them looking fresh.

Q: Can I wear socks with my sneakers and dress?

A: Absolutely! Socks can add a fun pop of color or pattern to your outfit. Just make sure to choose socks that complement the overall look.

Conclusion of Do Sneakers Go with Dresses

In conclusion, sneakers can definitely be paired with dresses for a comfortable and stylish look. By following a few simple guidelines, you can rock this trend with confidence. Remember to choose a simple dress, balance the look with the right sneakers, and accessorize with minimal jewelry. With these tips in mind, you'll be turning heads in no time.
