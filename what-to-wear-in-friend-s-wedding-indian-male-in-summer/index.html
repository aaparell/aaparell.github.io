What To Wear In Friend s Wedding Indian Male In Summer A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Oct 21, 2023 · 5 min read

Attending a friend's wedding is always exciting, but deciding what to wear can be a daunting task, especially for Indian men in the scorching summer heat. From the choice of fabric to the color and style, there are many factors to consider when selecting an outfit. In this blog post, we will guide you on what to wear in friend's wedding Indian male in summer and help you make a lasting impression on the big day.

Summer weddings can be challenging, as the hot and humid weather can make it uncomfortable to wear heavy fabrics and formal attire. The last thing you want is to feel hot and stuffy during the wedding and spoil your friend's big day. However, you also want to look stylish and elegant, as weddings are a time to dress up and celebrate love.

The perfect outfit for a friend's wedding in summer should strike a balance between comfort and style. You want to wear something that keeps you cool and comfortable while also looking sharp and fashionable.

In summary, here are the main points to keep in mind when deciding what to wear in friend's wedding Indian male in summer:

What to Wear in Friend's Wedding Indian Male in Summer: Fabric

The fabric you choose for your outfit is crucial in summer. It can make or break your comfort level during the wedding. You want to opt for lightweight and breathable fabrics that can keep you cool and dry. Cotton, linen, and silk are excellent choices for summer weddings as they are natural fibers that allow air to circulate and prevent sweating. Avoid synthetic fabrics like polyester and nylon as they trap heat and moisture in the body.

What to Wear in Friend's Wedding Indian Male in Summer: Style

The style of your outfit can also affect your comfort level during the wedding. Opt for loose-fitting and relaxed styles that allow air to circulate and prevent sweat. Avoid tight-fitting and heavy styles that can trap heat and make you feel uncomfortable. For the daytime wedding, you can wear a simple kurta-pajama or a light-colored shirt with matching trousers. For the evening wedding, you can wear a Nehru jacket or a light-colored blazer with a shirt and trousers.

What to Wear in Friend's Wedding Indian Male in Summer: Color

The color of your outfit can also impact your comfort level in summer. Lighter colors reflect sunlight and heat, while darker colors absorb them. Therefore, you want to opt for light and pastel colors that can keep you cool and comfortable. Avoid dark and bold colors that can make you feel hot and stuffy. For the daytime wedding, you can wear light-colored outfits like white, cream, beige, or pastel shades of blue, pink, and green. For the evening wedding, you can wear darker shades of blue, gray, black, or navy.

What to Wear in Friend's Wedding Indian Male in Summer: Accessories

Accessories can add the finishing touch to your outfit and make it look more elegant and sophisticated. However, in summer, you want to keep your accessories minimal and lightweight. Opt for breathable and comfortable footwear like sandals, loafers, or jootis. Avoid heavy and closed-toe shoes that can make your feet sweat. You can also wear a simple watch, a pocket square, or a brooch to add some personality to your outfit.

Personal Experience

As an Indian male, attending a friend's wedding in summer can be challenging, especially when you want to look your best. Last year, I attended my friend's daytime wedding in a simple cotton kurta-pajama and matching jootis. The outfit was comfortable and breathable, and I felt cool and relaxed throughout the ceremony. I paired the outfit with a simple watch and a pocket square, which added some elegance to the look. Overall, I received many compliments on my outfit and felt confident and stylish.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to keep in mind when deciding what to wear in friend's wedding Indian male in summer:

  • Always choose natural and breathable fabrics like cotton, linen, and silk.
  • Opt for loose-fitting and relaxed styles that allow air to circulate.
  • Avoid synthetic fabrics like polyester and nylon, which trap heat and moisture.
  • Choose light and pastel colors that reflect sunlight and heat.
  • Avoid dark and bold colors that absorb heat and make you feel hot.
  • Keep your accessories minimal and lightweight.
  • Wear breathable and comfortable footwear like sandals, loafers, or jootis.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a suit to a friend's wedding in summer?

A: It is not recommended to wear a suit to a friend's wedding in summer as the fabric is often heavy and not breathable. Opt for a light-colored blazer or Nehru jacket instead.

Q: Can I wear jeans to a friend's wedding in summer?

A: It is not recommended to wear jeans to a friend's wedding in summer as the fabric is often heavy and not breathable. Opt for matching trousers or chinos instead.

Q: Can I wear a t-shirt to a friend's wedding in summer?

A: It is not recommended to wear a t-shirt to a friend's wedding in summer as it is too casual and not appropriate for the occasion. Opt for a light-colored shirt or kurta instead.

Q: Can I wear a hat to a friend's wedding in summer?

A: It is not recommended to wear a hat to a friend's wedding in summer as it can make you feel hot and uncomfortable. Opt for a pocket square or a brooch instead.

Conclusion of What to Wear in Friend's Wedding Indian Male in Summer

Attending a friend's wedding in summer can be challenging, but with the right outfit, you can look stylish and feel comfortable. Remember to choose natural and breathable fabrics, opt for loose-fitting and relaxed styles, and choose light and pastel colors. Keep your accessories minimal and lightweight, and wear breathable and comfortable footwear. By following these tips and tricks, you can make a lasting impression on your friend's big day.
