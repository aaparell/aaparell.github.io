In recent years, there has been a growing demand for sustainable fashion in India. Consumers are becoming more aware of the impact of fast fashion on the environment and are looking for brands that prioritize sustainability. Many top fashion brands in India have recognized this trend and are incorporating sustainable practices into their business models.
