Dealing with menstruation has always been a challenge for women worldwide, and India is no exception. Traditionally, women in India used homemade alternatives like old clothes or leaves to manage their periods. However, these methods were unhygienic and could cause health problems. This led to the introduction of sanitary pads in India, which revolutionized menstrual hygiene management for women.

The lack of proper menstrual hygiene management led to several issues for women, including infections, rashes, and other health problems. Women were also forced to skip school or work during their periods, which affected their education and careers. These pain points led to the development of sanitary pads in India.

Sanitary pads were first introduced in India in the late 1970s. The first brand to launch sanitary pads in India was Stayfree, which was later followed by other brands like Whisper and Stayfree. The introduction of sanitary pads in India was a significant step towards improving women's health and hygiene during their menstrual cycles.

The Evolution of Sanitary Pads in India

Since the introduction of sanitary pads in India, the product has evolved significantly. Today, there are several types of sanitary pads available in India, including ultra-thin pads, maxi pads, and overnight pads. Additionally, it's crucial to choose a pad that's made from high-quality materials to prevent infections and other health problems.
