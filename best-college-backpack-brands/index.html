Best College Backpack Brands Which One Is Right For You

Written by Aprilia Jul 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a college student in need of a new backpack? Look no further than these best college backpack brands. With so many options on the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one for you. But fear not, we've done the research and compiled a list of the top brands to consider.

As a college student, you likely have a lot to carry around with you every day. Heavy textbooks, laptops, and other supplies can quickly weigh down a backpack and cause discomfort. Additionally, you want a backpack that is durable and can withstand daily wear and tear. These are just a few pain points that college students may face when searching for the perfect backpack.

The target of this article is to provide you with a comprehensive list of the best college backpack brands currently on the market. We will highlight the pros and cons of each brand, as well as provide personal experiences and recommendations.

In summary, we will be discussing the top college backpack brands, their features, and which one may be best suited for your needs.

1. JanSport

JanSport is a classic brand that has been a popular choice among college students for decades. Their backpacks are known for their durability, with many students still using their JanSport backpacks years after graduation. The brand offers a variety of sizes and styles to choose from, with many featuring padded straps and compartments for laptops and other electronics.

Personally, I have owned a JanSport backpack for years and it has never let me down. It has survived countless semesters of heavy textbooks and has even traveled with me on international trips. I highly recommend this brand for its reliability and classic design.

2. North Face

North Face is another popular brand among college students, known for their high-quality outdoor gear. Their backpacks are no exception, with many featuring water-resistant materials and comfortable straps. The brand also offers a lifetime warranty on their products, ensuring that your investment is protected for years to come.

Personally, I have used a North Face backpack for hiking and camping trips and have been impressed with its durability and functionality. While it may be on the pricier side, it is definitely worth the investment for those who plan on using their backpack for outdoor activities as well as daily use.

3. Herschel

Herschel is a newer brand on the market but has quickly gained popularity among college students for their stylish and functional backpacks. Their backpacks often feature unique patterns and designs, making them stand out from the crowd. Additionally, many of their backpacks feature padded laptop sleeves and multiple compartments for organization.

Personally, I have not owned a Herschel backpack but have admired their designs from afar. While they may not be as durable as other brands, they are a great option for those who prioritize style and fashion.

4. Under Armour

Under Armour is a brand known for their athletic gear, but their backpacks are also a great option for college students. Their backpacks often feature water-resistant materials and breathable mesh back panels for added comfort. Additionally, many of their backpacks feature compartments specifically designed for sports equipment and gym clothes.

Personally, I have used an Under Armour backpack for gym and sports activities and have been impressed with its durability and functionality. It may not be the most stylish option, but it is definitely a great investment for those who prioritize athletic activities.

Question and Answer

Q: What should I look for in a college backpack?

A: When searching for a college backpack, it is important to consider factors such as durability, comfort, and functionality. Look for backpacks with padded straps and compartments for electronics, as well as water-resistant materials if you plan on using it for outdoor activities.

Q: How much should I expect to spend on a college backpack?

A: The price of college backpacks can vary greatly depending on the brand and features. On average, expect to spend anywhere from $50 to $150.

Q: How long can I expect my college backpack to last?

A: The lifespan of a college backpack can vary depending on the brand and usage. However, investing in a high-quality backpack from a reputable brand can ensure that it lasts for years to come.

Q: Are there any eco-friendly options for college backpacks?

A: Yes, there are several brands that offer eco-friendly options for college backpacks. Look for backpacks made from recycled materials or sustainable fabrics.

Conclusion of Best College Backpack Brands

When it comes to choosing the best college backpack brand, there is no one-size-fits-all solution. It is important to consider your individual needs and preferences when selecting a backpack. However, the brands listed above are a great place to start your search. With their durable materials, functional features, and stylish designs, they are sure to meet the needs of any college student.
