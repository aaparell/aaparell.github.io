What Is A Fitted Dress Called

Written by Christine Aug 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Have you ever found yourself in a situation where you are trying to describe a fitted dress, but can't seem to find the right words? It can be frustrating trying to explain what you mean without sounding awkward or confusing. In this post, we will explore what a fitted dress is called in relaxed English language.

When it comes to fashion, there are always terms and jargon that can be difficult to understand. This is particularly true when it comes to describing the style of a dress. Many people struggle with identifying the difference between a fitted dress and a bodycon dress, or even a sheath dress. This can make it challenging when trying to find a specific style of dress or communicate with others about what you are looking for.

A fitted dress is called by many terms, but the most common name for it is a "tailored dress". This term is used to describe a dress that fits closely to the body, but is not skin-tight. It is designed to hug your curves in a flattering way, without revealing too much. A tailored dress can be made from a variety of fabrics, but it is typically made from materials that have some stretch to them, such as cotton or spandex blends.

My Personal Experience with Tailored Dresses

As someone who loves fashion, I have always been drawn to tailored dresses. They are versatile, timeless, and always make me feel confident and put together. One of my favorite tailored dresses is a classic black sheath dress that I can dress up or down depending on the occasion. It has a fitted silhouette that accentuates my curves while still being appropriate for a professional setting.

The Benefits of Tailored Dresses

One of the main benefits of a tailored dress is that it can be worn by women of all shapes and sizes. Whether you have an hourglass figure, a pear-shaped body, or an athletic build, a tailored dress can be flattering and help you feel confident. Additionally, a tailored dress can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. It can be paired with heels and statement jewelry for a formal event, or dressed down with flats and a denim jacket for a more casual look.

How to Choose the Right Tailored Dress for You

When choosing a tailored dress, it is important to consider your body shape and personal style. If you have an hourglass figure, a dress with a defined waistline will accentuate your curves. If you have a pear-shaped body, a dress with a flared skirt will help balance out your proportions. Additionally, it is important to consider the occasion you will be wearing the dress for. A dress that is appropriate for a wedding may not be suitable for a day at the office.

Tips for Styling a Tailored Dress

When it comes to styling a tailored dress, the options are endless. Here are some tips to help you create a look that is both stylish and flattering:

  • Pair your dress with statement jewelry to add some sparkle and shine
  • Choose shoes that complement the style of your dress - for a formal look, opt for heels, and for a more casual look, choose flats or sandals
  • Add a blazer or cardigan to your dress to create a layered look
  • Experiment with different accessories, such as belts or scarves, to add some personality to your outfit

Question and Answer

Q: What is the difference between a fitted dress and a bodycon dress?

A: A fitted dress is designed to hug your curves in a flattering way, but is not skin-tight like a bodycon dress. A bodycon dress is made from a stretchy fabric that is intended to fit snugly to the body.

Q: Can tailored dresses be worn to a casual event?

A: Yes, tailored dresses can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. For a casual event, opt for a more relaxed style of dress and pair it with flats or sandals.

Q: Are tailored dresses suitable for all body types?

A: Yes, tailored dresses can be flattering for women of all shapes and sizes. It is important to choose a dress that is designed to flatter your specific body type.

Q: What is the best way to care for a tailored dress?

A: Always check the care label on your dress, but in general, tailored dresses should be washed in cold water and hung to dry to maintain their shape.

Conclusion of What is a Fitted Dress Called

In conclusion, a fitted dress is commonly referred to as a "tailored dress" in relaxed English language. This versatile style of dress can be worn by women of all shapes and sizes and is appropriate for a variety of occasions. By following the tips and advice outlined in this post, you can find the perfect tailored dress to help you feel confident and stylish.
