Top Indian Clothing Brands A Guide To Finding The Perfect Outfit

Written by Daniel Aug 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of spending hours searching for the perfect Indian outfit? Do you find yourself overwhelmed by the sheer number of options available? Look no further! We have compiled a list of the top Indian clothing brands to make your search easier.

When it comes to Indian clothing, there are a few pain points that many people face. One of the biggest is finding a brand that offers high-quality, trendy clothing at an affordable price. Another is finding a brand that offers a wide range of sizes to accommodate all body types. Finally, many people struggle to find a brand that offers a good mix of traditional and modern styles.

The target of this article is to provide you with a comprehensive guide to the top Indian clothing brands. We will cover everything from pricing to sizing to style, giving you all the information you need to make an informed decision.

In summary, this article will provide you with a guide to the top Indian clothing brands, addressing common pain points and offering a comprehensive overview of each brand's pricing, sizing, and style.

1. Biba

When it comes to traditional Indian clothing, Biba is one of the top brands in the country. Their collection includes a wide range of kurtas, salwar kameez suits, and lehengas, all featuring intricate designs and beautiful embroidery. What sets Biba apart from other brands is their commitment to affordability - their prices are some of the most competitive in the market.

I recently purchased a beautiful kurta from Biba for a family wedding, and I was blown away by the quality of the fabric and the attention to detail in the embroidery. Despite the affordable price tag, the kurta looked and felt expensive, and I received countless compliments on it throughout the day.

2. W

If you're looking for a brand that offers a mix of traditional and modern styles, W is the perfect choice. Their collection includes everything from classic kurtas to trendy crop tops, all featuring unique prints and patterns. What sets W apart from other brands is their commitment to sustainability - they use eco-friendly materials and processes wherever possible.

Personally, I love W's collection of fusion wear - they offer a range of outfits that are perfect for weddings and other formal events, but that also have a modern twist. I recently wore a W crop top and lehenga to a friend's wedding, and I received so many compliments on the unique design.

3. Ritu Kumar

Ritu Kumar is one of the most well-known names in Indian fashion, and for good reason. Her collection includes a wide range of traditional and modern outfits, all featuring stunning embroidery and intricate designs. While Ritu Kumar's prices are on the higher side, the quality of her clothing is unmatched.

If you're looking for a show-stopping outfit for a special occasion, Ritu Kumar is the perfect choice. I recently splurged on a Ritu Kumar lehenga for my sister's wedding, and I have never felt more beautiful. The attention to detail in the embroidery was truly breathtaking, and the outfit fit me like a glove.

4. Global Desi

Global Desi is a brand that offers a mix of traditional and bohemian styles, perfect for those who want something a little different. Their collection includes a range of kurtas, dresses, and skirts, all featuring unique prints and patterns. What sets Global Desi apart from other brands is their commitment to fair trade - they work with artisans across India to create their clothing.

I recently purchased a Global Desi dress for a friend's birthday party, and I loved how unique it was. The print was unlike anything I had seen before, and the dress was so comfortable to wear. I also appreciated the fact that the brand supports fair trade - it felt good to know that my purchase was making a positive impact.

Question and Answer

Q: Do these brands offer plus sizes?

A: Yes, all of the brands mentioned in this article offer a range of sizes to accommodate all body types.

Q: Are these brands available outside of India?

A: Yes, many of these brands have international shipping options available on their websites.

Q: Are these brands affordable?

A: Yes, all of the brands mentioned in this article offer affordable options, with some being more budget-friendly than others.

Q: Do these brands offer men's clothing?

A: While the brands mentioned in this article primarily focus on women's clothing, some of them do offer a small selection of men's clothing as well.

Conclusion

There you have it - a comprehensive guide to the top Indian clothing brands. Whether you're looking for a traditional outfit for a wedding or a trendy dress for a night out, these brands have something for everyone. With a mix of affordability, quality, and style, you're sure to find the perfect outfit for any occasion.
