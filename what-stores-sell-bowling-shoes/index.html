Where To Buy Bowling Shoes A Guide To Finding The Perfect Pair

Written by Daniel Jul 22, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of using the same old rented bowling shoes every time you hit the lanes? Do you want to take your game to the next level with a pair of your own? Look no further! In this guide, we'll show you where to buy bowling shoes that will improve your game and make you look good at the same time.

If you enjoy bowling regularly, renting shoes can be a pain. They may not fit well, and you end up wearing shoes that countless other people have already worn. Plus, they may not be the right style or design to suit your taste. Buying your own bowling shoes can solve all these problems, and we'll help you find the perfect pair.

The best places to buy bowling shoes are sporting goods stores, online retailers, and bowling pro shops. Sporting goods stores like Dick's Sporting Goods and Academy Sports + Outdoors carry a wide range of bowling shoes for men, women, and children. Online retailers like Amazon and eBay offer an even larger selection of shoes, often at lower prices than traditional stores. Bowling pro shops are also a great option, as they specialize in bowling equipment and can provide expert advice on finding the right shoes for your needs.

In summary, you can find bowling shoes at sporting goods stores, online retailers, and bowling pro shops. Each option has its own advantages and disadvantages, so it's important to consider your specific needs and preferences before making a purchase.

Sporting Goods Stores

When it comes to buying bowling shoes, sporting goods stores are a great place to start. Dick's Sporting Goods and Academy Sports + Outdoors are two popular options that offer a wide range of shoes for men, women, and children. They often have sales and discounts, which can make buying bowling shoes more affordable. You can also try on shoes in-store to ensure a proper fit and ask store associates for advice on finding the right pair.

Online Retailers

If you prefer to shop from the comfort of your own home, online retailers like Amazon and eBay offer an even larger selection of bowling shoes. You can easily compare prices and read reviews from other customers to help you make an informed decision. However, you won't be able to try on shoes before buying, so it's important to carefully read size charts and product descriptions to ensure a good fit.

Bowling Pro Shops

Bowling pro shops are another great option for buying bowling shoes. These shops specialize in bowling equipment and can provide expert advice on finding the right shoes for your needs. They may also offer custom fitting and adjustments to ensure a perfect fit. However, prices at pro shops may be higher than at other retailers, so it's important to consider your budget before making a purchase.

Tips for Buying Bowling Shoes

When buying bowling shoes, there are a few key factors to consider:

  • Fit: Make sure the shoes fit properly and feel comfortable. You'll be wearing them for several hours at a time, so it's important to get the right size.
  • Style: Choose a style that suits your taste and personality. Bowling shoes come in a variety of colors and designs, so don't be afraid to show off your individuality.
  • Type: Decide whether you want performance shoes or basic shoes. Performance shoes are designed for serious bowlers and offer features like interchangeable soles and heels for a customized fit. Basic shoes are more affordable and suitable for casual bowlers.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear regular shoes for bowling?

A: It's not recommended to wear regular shoes for bowling, as they can damage the lanes and affect your performance. Bowling shoes have special soles that allow you to slide and brake properly, which regular shoes don't have.

Q: How much should I expect to pay for bowling shoes?

A: The price of bowling shoes varies depending on the brand, style, and retailer. Basic shoes can cost as little as $20, while high-end performance shoes can cost over $200. It's important to consider your budget and needs before making a purchase.

Q: How do I know if the shoes are the right fit?

A: When trying on bowling shoes, make sure they feel snug but not too tight. You should be able to wiggle your toes comfortably. Walk around in the shoes to ensure they don't slip off or rub against your feet.

Q: Can I wear my bowling shoes outside of the bowling alley?

A: It's not recommended to wear your bowling shoes outside of the bowling alley, as they have special soles that can be damaged by concrete and other surfaces. Plus, they may not be the most stylish shoes to wear in public!

Conclusion of Where to Buy Bowling Shoes

Buying your own pair of bowling shoes can improve your game and make bowling more enjoyable. You can find bowling shoes at sporting goods stores, online retailers, and bowling pro shops. Each option has its own advantages and disadvantages, so it's important to consider your specific needs and preferences before making a purchase. Remember to choose a pair that fits well, suits your style, and meets your performance needs. Happy bowling!
