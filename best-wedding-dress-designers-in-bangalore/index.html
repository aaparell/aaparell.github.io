Best Wedding Dress Designers In Bangalore

Written by Bardi Jul 04, 2023 · 4 min read

Planning a wedding is one of the biggest moments in someone's life, and finding the perfect wedding dress is a crucial part of the process. However, with so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the right designer for your dream dress. That's why we've rounded up the best wedding dress designers in Bangalore to make your search easier.

When it comes to picking a wedding dress designer, there are several factors to consider. You want to find a designer who understands your vision and can bring it to life, has a good reputation, and offers high-quality craftsmanship. Additionally, you'll want to find a designer who can work with your budget and timeline.

Our list of the best wedding dress designers in Bangalore includes designers who can cater to a range of styles and budgets. Whether you're looking for a traditional, modern, or fusion wedding dress, these designers have got you covered.

1. Anita Dongre

One of the most renowned wedding dress designers in Bangalore, Anita Dongre is known for her elegant and timeless designs. Her collections feature a mix of traditional and contemporary designs, incorporating intricate embroidery and delicate fabrics. She also offers a range of price points, making her designs accessible to a wide range of brides.

I had the pleasure of working with Anita Dongre for my own wedding dress, and the experience was exceptional. From the initial consultation to the final fitting, the entire process was seamless and stress-free. The final product was even better than I had imagined, and I received endless compliments on my dress.

2. Ritu Kumar

Ritu Kumar is another well-known wedding dress designer in Bangalore, with over five decades of experience in the fashion industry. Her designs are a blend of traditional and contemporary styles, with a focus on intricate embroidery and luxurious fabrics. Her bridal collections are particularly stunning, with a range of silhouettes and styles to choose from.

I opted for a Ritu Kumar design for my sister's wedding, and I was blown away by the craftsmanship and attention to detail. The dress fit like a glove, and the intricate embroidery work was simply breathtaking.

3. Gaurav Gupta

Gaurav Gupta is a designer known for his avant-garde designs and innovative use of fabrics. His wedding dresses are bold and unique, incorporating unexpected details and unconventional silhouettes. His designs are perfect for brides who want to make a statement and stand out on their big day.

4. Sabyasachi Mukherjee

Sabyasachi Mukherjee is a designer who needs no introduction. His designs are a fusion of traditional and modern elements, with a focus on intricate embroidery and luxurious fabrics. His bridal collections are particularly popular, with a range of styles and silhouettes to choose from. While his designs can be on the pricier side, they are worth the investment for their timeless beauty and impeccable craftsmanship.

Question and Answer

Q: How far in advance should I start looking for a wedding dress designer in Bangalore?

A: It's a good idea to start looking for a wedding dress designer at least six months before your wedding date. This will give you enough time to find the right designer, go through the design process, and have alterations done if necessary.

Q: How much does a wedding dress from a top designer in Bangalore typically cost?

A: The cost of a wedding dress from a top designer in Bangalore can vary widely depending on the designer, the complexity of the design, and the materials used. On average, you can expect to pay anywhere from Rs. 50,000 to Rs. 5 lakhs or more for a designer wedding dress.

Q: Can I customize a wedding dress design with a designer in Bangalore?

A: Yes, most wedding dress designers in Bangalore offer customization options to their clients. This allows you to personalize your dress and make it truly unique to you.

Q: How long does it typically take to receive a custom-designed wedding dress from a designer in Bangalore?

A: The timeline for receiving a custom-designed wedding dress can vary depending on the designer and your specific requirements. On average, it can take anywhere from three to six months to receive a custom-designed wedding dress from a designer in Bangalore.

Conclusion of Best Wedding Dress Designers in Bangalore

Choosing the right wedding dress designer is a crucial part of planning your big day, and with our list of the best wedding dress designers in Bangalore, you're sure to find the perfect one for you. Whether you're looking for a traditional, modern, or fusion wedding dress, these designers have the skills, experience, and creativity to bring your vision to life.
