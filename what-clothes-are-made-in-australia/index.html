Discovering What Clothes Are Made In Australia

Written by Aprilia Sep 08, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you curious about what clothes are made in Australia? Do you want to support local brands and industries? Do you want to know more about the fashion industry in Australia? If you answered yes to any of these questions, then keep reading.

A lot of people are becoming more conscious about where their clothes come from and the impact of the fashion industry on the environment and workers. It can be overwhelming to navigate through the world of fast fashion and global supply chains. However, by choosing to buy locally-made clothes, you can help reduce your carbon footprint, support local businesses, and ensure fair labor practices.

Australia has a thriving textile and fashion industry, with many talented designers and manufacturers producing a wide range of clothing items. From swimwear to streetwear, there is something for everyone. Whether you want to invest in high-quality basics or unique statement pieces, there are many Australian brands to choose from.

In this article, we will explore what clothes are made in Australia, the benefits of buying locally-made clothes, and some of the best Australian clothing brands.

Why Choose Locally-Made Clothes?

Buying locally-made clothes has several benefits, including:

Reducing Your Carbon Footprint: When you buy clothes that are made locally, you are supporting a more sustainable and environmentally-friendly way of producing clothes. You are also reducing the carbon emissions associated with shipping clothes across the world.

Supporting Local Businesses: By buying clothes that are made in Australia, you are supporting local businesses and industries. You are helping to create jobs and stimulate the local economy.

Ensuring Fair Labor Practices: Many Australian clothing manufacturers have strict ethical and sustainable standards, ensuring that workers are paid fairly and work in safe conditions. By choosing to buy locally-made clothes, you can have peace of mind knowing that your clothes were made ethically.

Australian Clothing Brands to Check Out

Here are some of the best Australian clothing brands that make clothes locally:

1. Bassike: Bassike is a Sydney-based brand that is known for its minimalist and sustainable clothing. They use organic cotton and natural fibers in their clothing and produce their clothes locally.

2. KITX: KITX is a brand that is committed to sustainable and ethical fashion. They use eco-friendly materials and work with artisans and suppliers who share their values.

3. Arnsdorf: Arnsdorf is a Melbourne-based brand that is known for its classic and timeless clothing. They produce their clothes locally and use sustainable materials like organic cotton and linen.

4. Spell & The Gypsy Collective: Spell & The Gypsy Collective is a bohemian-inspired brand that produces its clothes locally in Byron Bay. They use sustainable materials and work with local artisans to create unique and beautiful clothing.

What Types of Clothes are Made in Australia?

Australia produces a wide range of clothing items, including:

Swimwear: Australia is known for its beautiful beaches, so it's no surprise that there are many local swimwear brands. Some of the most popular ones include Seafolly, Tigerlily, and Baku.

Sportswear: Australia has a strong sporting culture, so it's no surprise that there are many local sportswear brands. Some of the most popular ones include Lorna Jane, Running Bare, and Nimble.

Streetwear: Australian streetwear brands are known for their unique and edgy designs. Some of the most popular ones include Ksubi, Zanerobe, and Vanishing Elephant.

Ethical and Sustainable Clothing: There are many Australian brands that are committed to ethical and sustainable fashion. Some of the most popular ones include Outland Denim, Nobody Denim, and Pure Pod.

How to Find Locally-Made Clothes in Australia?

If you want to find locally-made clothes in Australia, here are some tips:

1. Look for the "Made in Australia" Label: Many Australian brands will indicate on their clothing tags whether their clothes were made locally or overseas.

2. Shop at Local Markets: Many local markets in Australia feature stalls selling locally-made clothing items. This is a great way to discover new brands and support local businesses.

3. Research Online: You can use online search engines and directories to find Australian clothing brands that produce their clothes locally. You can also read reviews and testimonials from other customers to get a sense of the brand's quality and values.

Conclusion of What Clothes Are Made in Australia

Buying locally-made clothes is a great way to support local businesses, reduce your carbon footprint, and ensure fair labor practices. Australia has a thriving textile and fashion industry, with many talented designers and manufacturers producing a wide range of clothing items. By choosing to buy clothes that are made in Australia, you can help support the local economy and invest in high-quality and ethically-made clothes.
