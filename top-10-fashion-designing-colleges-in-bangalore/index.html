Top 10 Fashion Designing Colleges In Bangalore

Written by Robby Jul 27, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you interested in pursuing a career in the fashion industry in Bangalore? Do you want to be a part of the next generation of designers, stylists, and fashion entrepreneurs? Look no further than the top 10 fashion designing colleges in Bangalore. These colleges offer comprehensive programs and training in all aspects of fashion design, from conceptualization and sketching to production and marketing.

As a prospective fashion student, you may face various pain points such as confusion about which college to choose, fear of not getting the best education, and uncertainty about the quality of education and job prospects after graduation. However, by exploring the top 10 fashion designing colleges in Bangalore, you can make an informed decision and take the first step towards your dream career.

The target of this blog post is to provide detailed information about the top 10 fashion designing colleges in Bangalore, their courses, faculties, infrastructure, placement records, and other relevant details that can help you to choose the right college for your needs.

In summary, the top 10 fashion designing colleges in Bangalore offer a wide range of programs, including diploma, undergraduate, and postgraduate courses. These colleges are known for their experienced faculties, state-of-the-art infrastructure, industry collaborations, and excellent placement records.

1. National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT)

As one of the premier fashion institutes in India, NIFT offers courses in fashion design, accessory design, textile design, fashion communication, and fashion technology. The college has a well-equipped campus, experienced faculty members, and strong industry links that provide students with hands-on training and exposure to the latest trends and technologies in the industry.

2. Vogue Institute of Art and Design

Vogue Institute of Art and Design offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in fashion design, interior design, jewelry design, animation, and multimedia. The college has a sprawling campus, modern facilities, and an experienced faculty that provides students with a holistic education and prepares them for the industry.

3. Srishti Institute of Art, Design, and Technology

Srishti Institute of Art, Design, and Technology is known for its innovative and interdisciplinary approach to design education. The college offers courses in fashion design, textile design, visual communication, product design, and more. The college has a vibrant campus, experienced faculty members, and a strong focus on sustainability and social responsibility.

4. JD Institute of Fashion Technology

JD Institute of Fashion Technology offers diploma, undergraduate, and postgraduate programs in fashion design, interior design, jewelry design, and fashion communication. The college has a network of branches across India, experienced faculty members, and a strong industry interface that provides students with practical training and exposure to the industry.

5. Indian Institute of Fashion Technology (IIFT)

Indian Institute of Fashion Technology offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and diploma courses in fashion design, textile design, and fashion communication. The college has a well-equipped campus, experienced faculty members, and strong industry collaborations that provide students with comprehensive training and exposure to the industry.

6. Inter National Institute of Fashion Design (INIFD)

Inter National Institute of Fashion Design offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in fashion design, interior design, and textile design. The college has a pan-India presence, experienced faculty members, and strong industry collaborations that provide students with practical training and exposure to the industry.

7. LISAA School of Design

LISAA School of Design offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in fashion design, interior design, and graphic design. The college has a well-equipped campus, experienced faculty members, and a strong focus on innovation and creativity that prepares students for the industry.

8. Raffles Millennium International

Raffles Millennium International offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in fashion design, fashion marketing, and fashion management. The college has a global network of campuses, experienced faculty members, and a strong focus on industry partnerships and collaborations that provide students with exposure to the global fashion industry.

9. The Oxford College of Science

The Oxford College of Science offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in fashion design, textile design, and fashion management. The college has a well-equipped campus, experienced faculty members, and a strong focus on research and innovation that prepares students for the industry.

10. Acharya Institute of Graduate Studies

Acharya Institute of Graduate Studies offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in fashion design, textile design, and fashion communication. The college has a modern campus, experienced faculty members, and a strong focus on practical training and industry exposure that prepares students for the industry.

Question and Answer Section

Q: What are the eligibility criteria for admission to these colleges?

A: The eligibility criteria vary for each college and program. Generally, candidates must have completed their 10+2 education or equivalent in any stream with a minimum of 50% marks. Some colleges may also require candidates to appear for an entrance exam or submit a portfolio.

Q: What are the job prospects after completing a fashion design course from these colleges?

A: Graduates from these colleges can pursue a variety of career paths in the fashion industry, including fashion designing, textile designing, fashion merchandising, fashion styling, fashion journalism, and more. The salary and job prospects depend on the candidate's skills, experience, and the organization they work for.

Q: What are the fees for these colleges?

A: The fees for these colleges vary depending on the program and the college. Generally, the fees range from INR 1 lakh to INR 5 lakhs per year.

Q: Do these colleges offer scholarships?

A: Yes, most of these colleges offer scholarships to deserving and meritorious candidates. The scholarship amount and eligibility criteria vary for each college.

Conclusion of Top 10 Fashion Designing Colleges in Bangalore

If you are passionate about fashion and want to build a career in the industry, the top 10 fashion designing colleges in Bangalore offer a great opportunity to learn and grow. By considering the courses, faculties, infrastructure, placement records, and other relevant details, you can choose the right college that matches your interests, skills, and career goals. Best of luck!
