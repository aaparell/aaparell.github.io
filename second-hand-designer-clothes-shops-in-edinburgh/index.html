Discover Second Hand Designer Clothes Shops In Edinburgh

Written by Robby Jul 25, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're on a budget, love designer clothes, and are looking for some great deals, then second hand designer clothes shops in Edinburgh are a great option. Not only can you find high-quality, stylish clothes at a fraction of their original price, but you're also doing your bit for the environment by reducing waste and supporting sustainable fashion.

So, where can you find these second hand designer clothes shops in Edinburgh? In this blog post, we'll answer that question and provide you with some useful tips to help you make the most of your shopping experience.

To summarize, in this blog post we'll cover:

  • What are second hand designer clothes shops in Edinburgh?
  • Where to find them in Edinburgh?
  • How to shop in them like a pro?
  • FAQs about second hand designer clothes shops in Edinburgh.

What are Second Hand Designer Clothes Shops in Edinburgh?

Second hand designer clothes shops in Edinburgh are stores that sell pre-owned designer clothing and accessories. These stores typically offer a range of high-end brands at a fraction of their original retail price, making them a great option for fashion lovers on a budget. Shopping at these stores not only saves you money but also helps reduce waste by giving used clothing a new lease of life.

One of the best things about second hand designer clothes shops in Edinburgh is the variety of items on offer. You can find everything from designer dresses and suits to handbags and shoes. With new items arriving regularly, you're bound to find something that catches your eye.

Where to Find Second Hand Designer Clothes Shops in Edinburgh

Edinburgh is home to several second hand designer clothes shops, so you're sure to find one that suits your style and budget. Some of the most popular shops include:

  • The Edinburgh Dress Agency - Located in the heart of Edinburgh, this shop offers a range of high-end designer clothing and accessories for women.
  • Armstrong's Vintage Emporium - This shop specializes in vintage clothing and accessories, including designer pieces from the 1950s to the 1990s.
  • W. Armstrong & Son - This family-run business offers a range of pre-owned designer clothing and accessories for men and women.

When shopping at these stores, it's important to keep an open mind and be prepared to spend some time browsing. You never know what hidden gems you might find!

How to Shop in Second Hand Designer Clothes Shops Like a Pro

Shopping in second hand designer clothes shops in Edinburgh can be a bit different from shopping in traditional retail stores. To make the most of your experience, here are some tips:

  • Know what you're looking for: Before you start shopping, have a clear idea of what you want to buy. This will help you stay focused and avoid getting overwhelmed.
  • Inspect items carefully: Since these items are pre-owned, it's important to inspect them carefully for any signs of wear and tear. Look for stains, holes, and loose threads.
  • Try items on: Don't be afraid to try things on! Sizing can vary between brands, so it's always a good idea to try things on before you buy.
  • Ask questions: If you're unsure about something, don't be afraid to ask the store staff. They're there to help you and can provide valuable information about the items on offer.
  • Be prepared to negotiate: Many second hand designer clothes shops in Edinburgh are open to negotiation, so don't be afraid to haggle over the price!

FAQs about Second Hand Designer Clothes Shops in Edinburgh

Q: Are second hand designer clothes shops in Edinburgh sustainable?

A: Yes! By shopping at these stores, you're helping to reduce waste and support sustainable fashion.

Q: What brands can I expect to find in second hand designer clothes shops in Edinburgh?

A: You can find a range of high-end brands, including Chanel, Dior, Gucci, and Prada.

Q: Are the items in second hand designer clothes shops in Edinburgh in good condition?

A: Yes! These stores typically only accept items that are in good condition, so you can expect to find high-quality, gently worn clothing and accessories.

Q: Can I sell my own designer clothing to these shops?

A: Many second hand designer clothes shops in Edinburgh offer a consignment service, where you can sell your own gently used designer clothing and accessories.

Conclusion of Second Hand Designer Clothes Shops in Edinburgh

Shopping at second hand designer clothes shops in Edinburgh is a great way to save money, support sustainable fashion, and find unique, high-quality items. With a little bit of patience and a willingness to explore, you're sure to find some great deals and hidden gems at these stores.
