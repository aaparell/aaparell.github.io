Question and Answer

Q: Are old fashioned oats and quick oats the same thing?

A: No, old fashioned oats (also called rolled oats) and quick oats are not the same thing. Old fashioned oats are larger and thicker, while quick oats are smaller and thinner.

Q: Can old fashioned oats be microwaved?

A: Yes, old fashioned oats can be microwaved. Simply mix 1/2 cup of oats with 1 cup of water and microwave for 2-3 minutes, or until the oats are cooked to your desired consistency.

Q: Can old fashioned oats be used in baking?

A: Yes, old fashioned oats can be used in a variety of baking recipes, such as oatmeal cookies or granola bars.

Q: Do old fashioned oats need to be soaked overnight?

A: No, old fashioned oats do not need to be soaked overnight. They can be cooked on the stovetop or in the microwave for a quick and easy breakfast.
