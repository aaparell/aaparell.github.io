How to Measure for a Men's Shirt for Women

Measuring for a men's shirt for women is a crucial step to ensure the correct fit. You can use a measuring tape to take the following measurements:

Chest

Measure around the fullest part of the chest, ensuring that the tape is parallel to the ground.

Waist

Measure around the natural waistline, which is the narrowest part of the waist. Ensure that the tape is snug but not too tight.

Shoulder

Measure from the end of one shoulder to the end of the other shoulder, across the back.

Sleeve

Measure from the shoulder seam to the end of the cuff.
