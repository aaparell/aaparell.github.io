What Products Does India Export to UAE?

The United Arab Emirates (UAE) and India have long-standing trade relations, with India being one of UAE's top trading partners. Indian products are in high demand in UAE due to their quality and affordability. However, not all Indian products are exported to UAE. Let's take a closer look at which products India exports to UAE and why.

India export a diverse range of products to UAE, including petroleum products, precious metals, textiles, chemicals, and agricultural products. The UAE market has a high demand for these products, and India is able to meet that demand with its competitive pricing and quality products.

India's major exports to UAE include refined petroleum, jewellery, rice, and organic chemicals. Refined petroleum accounts for the largest share of India's exports to UAE, followed by jewellery. Rice

India is one of the largest producers of rice in the world, and it exports a significant amount of rice to UAE. Organic Chemicals
