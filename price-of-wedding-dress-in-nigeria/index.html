Price Of Wedding Dress In Nigeria A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Oct 02, 2023 · 4 min read

Weddings are one of the most important events in a person's life, especially for the bride. However, the cost of a wedding dress can be a major pain point for many people. In Nigeria, the cost of a wedding dress can vary greatly depending on various factors such as the designer, style, and quality. In this guide, we will provide you with all the information you need to know about the price of wedding dresses in Nigeria.

If you're wondering about the average cost of a wedding dress in Nigeria, the answer is not straightforward. You can find wedding dresses for as low as N20,000 and as high as N5,000,000, depending on the factors mentioned above. However, most wedding dresses in Nigeria fall within the range of N100,000 to N500,000.

In summary, the price of a wedding dress in Nigeria can vary greatly depending on the designer, style, and quality. However, most wedding dresses fall within the range of N100,000 to N500,000.

Factors That Affect the Price of Wedding Dresses in Nigeria

When it comes to the price of wedding dresses in Nigeria, there are several factors that can affect the cost. Here are some of the most common factors:

Designer

The designer of the wedding dress can have a significant impact on the price. If you opt for a dress from a well-known designer, you can expect to pay more for it than if you choose a lesser-known designer. Some of the most popular wedding dress designers in Nigeria include Toju Foyeh, Mai Atafo, and Deola Sagoe.

Style

The style of the wedding dress can also affect the price. More intricate and elaborate styles will typically cost more than simpler styles. For example, a ball gown with intricate lace detailing will usually be more expensive than a simple A-line dress.

Quality

The quality of the wedding dress can also affect the price. Higher-quality fabrics and materials will increase the cost of the dress. Additionally, dresses that are hand-sewn will often be more expensive than those that are machine-made.

Personal Experience

When I got married, I knew that I wanted a wedding dress from a well-known designer. However, I was also on a tight budget, so I had to be careful about how much I spent. I ended up finding a beautiful dress from a lesser-known designer that was still within my budget. It was a simple A-line dress with a few embellishments, but it made me feel beautiful on my wedding day.

Where to Buy Wedding Dresses in Nigeria

If you're looking to buy a wedding dress in Nigeria, there are several places you can consider:

Wedding Dress Shops

There are several wedding dress shops in Nigeria that specialize in selling wedding dresses. Some of the most popular shops include Brides and Babies, Elizabeth R, and Bridals by Ivy.

Online Retailers

You can also find wedding dresses on online retailers such as Jumia, Konga, and Jiji. However, be careful when buying online, as you won't be able to see the dress in person before purchasing.

Tips for Buying a Wedding Dress in Nigeria

Here are some tips to keep in mind when buying a wedding dress in Nigeria:

- Set a budget before you start shopping

- Do your research and shop around to find the best deals

- Try on different styles to find the one that flatters your body type the most

- Consider buying a pre-owned dress to save money

Conclusion of Price of Wedding Dress in Nigeria

The price of a wedding dress in Nigeria can vary greatly depending on several factors such as the designer, style, and quality. However, most wedding dresses fall within the range of N100,000 to N500,000. To find the perfect wedding dress for your special day, be sure to do your research, set a budget, and try on different styles to find the one that makes you feel beautiful.

A: Yes, hand-sewn dresses are often more expensive due to the time and effort that goes into making them.
