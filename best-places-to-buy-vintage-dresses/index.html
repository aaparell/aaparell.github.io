Best Places To Buy Vintage Dresses

Written by Oliver Jun 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of searching for the perfect vintage dress but keep coming up empty-handed? Do you want to find the best places to buy vintage dresses without breaking the bank? Look no further! In this post, we will explore the top places to buy vintage dresses, providing you with tips and tricks to find the perfect dress for any occasion.

When it comes to finding the best places to buy vintage dresses, it can be overwhelming. With so many options available online and in-store, it can be challenging to determine where to start. It can also be frustrating when you spend hours browsing through racks and still can't find what you're looking for.

If you want to save time and money, the best places to buy vintage dresses are online vintage shops, thrift stores, consignment stores, and flea markets. These places offer unique and affordable vintage dresses that you won't find anywhere else.

In summary, the best places to buy vintage dresses are online vintage shops, thrift stores, consignment stores, and flea markets. These places offer a wide variety of vintage dresses at an affordable price.

Online Vintage Shops

Online vintage shops are the best places to buy vintage dresses if you're looking for convenience and a wide variety of options. You can browse through thousands of vintage dresses from the comfort of your own home and have them delivered right to your doorstep. Some popular online vintage shops include Etsy, ASOS Marketplace, and Beyond Retro.

Personally, I've had great success finding vintage dresses on Etsy. The platform offers a vast selection of vintage dresses from different eras, and the prices are reasonable. Plus, many Etsy shops offer free shipping, making it an even better deal.

Thrift Stores

Thrift stores are another great option for finding vintage dresses. They offer a unique and affordable selection of vintage dresses. Plus, shopping at thrift stores is environmentally friendly, as you're giving a second life to pre-loved clothing.

One of my favorite thrift stores to find vintage dresses is Goodwill. They have a vast selection of vintage dresses at reasonable prices. However, keep in mind that thrift stores can be hit or miss, so be prepared to spend some time browsing through racks.

Consignment Stores

Consignment stores are a step up from thrift stores and offer higher-end vintage dresses. These stores curate their selection of vintage dresses, ensuring that they're in good condition and on-trend. However, with higher quality comes a higher price tag.

One of my favorite consignment stores for vintage dresses is The RealReal. They have a beautiful selection of vintage dresses from designer brands at reasonable prices.

Flea Markets

Flea markets are an excellent option for finding unique vintage dresses. They offer a mix of vintage and handmade clothing, making it a great place to find one-of-a-kind pieces. Plus, you can often negotiate prices at flea markets, making it more affordable.

One of my favorite flea markets to find vintage dresses is the Rose Bowl Flea Market in Pasadena, CA. They have a vast selection of vintage clothing, including dresses from different eras. However, keep in mind that flea markets are often crowded, so be prepared to spend some time navigating through the crowds.

Question and Answer

Q: How can I ensure the vintage dress fits me properly?

A: When shopping online, make sure to check the measurements provided in the description. If you're shopping in-store, try the dress on and make sure it fits well before purchasing.

Q: How can I tell if a vintage dress is authentic?

A: Look for labels or tags that indicate the dress's era. You can also research the style and design of the dress to determine if it's authentic.

Q: Are vintage dresses more expensive than new dresses?

A: It depends on where you shop. Online vintage shops and thrift stores offer affordable vintage dresses, while consignment stores and high-end vintage shops may have a higher price tag.

Q: Can I alter a vintage dress to fit me better?

A: Yes, you can have a vintage dress altered to fit you better. However, make sure to work with a professional tailor who has experience working with vintage clothing.

Conclusion of Best Places to Buy Vintage Dresses

In conclusion, the best places to buy vintage dresses are online vintage shops, thrift stores, consignment stores, and flea markets. Each option offers a unique selection of vintage dresses at an affordable price point. Whether you're looking for a dress for a special occasion, a vintage-inspired outfit or want to add a unique piece to your wardrobe, these are the best places to start your search.
