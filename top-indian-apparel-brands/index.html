Top Indian Apparel Brands A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

India is known for its vibrant and diverse fashion industry, with a plethora of apparel brands to choose from. But with so many options, it can be overwhelming to know which ones are the best. In this post, we'll dive into the top Indian apparel brands and what makes them stand out.

Target of Top Indian Apparel Brands

The target of top Indian apparel brands is to provide quality clothing that reflects the country's rich culture and heritage. These brands strive to create unique designs that cater to the needs and preferences of Indian consumers, while also appealing to a global audience.

One of the biggest pain points for consumers when it comes to apparel brands is the lack of variety and originality. Many brands tend to follow the same trends and styles, leaving customers feeling uninspired and bored. Top Indian apparel brands, on the other hand, are known for their creativity and distinctiveness.

So, without further ado, let's take a closer look at some of the top Indian apparel brands and what sets them apart.

1. Fabindia

Fabindia is a brand that has become synonymous with Indian fashion. Founded in 1960, it is known for its use of traditional techniques and handwoven fabrics. The brand offers a range of clothing options, including sarees, kurtas, and dresses, as well as accessories and home decor items. What makes Fabindia unique is its commitment to sustainability and fair trade practices, making it a socially responsible choice for consumers.

2. Biba

Biba is a brand that caters to the modern Indian woman. Founded in 1988, it is known for its fusion wear that combines traditional Indian elements with contemporary designs. Biba offers a range of clothing options, including kurtas, salwar suits, and lehengas, as well as accessories and footwear. The brand is popular for its use of bright colors and bold prints, making it a favorite among fashion-forward women.

3. W

W is a brand that caters to the everyday woman. Founded in 2001, it offers a range of affordable yet stylish clothing options, including kurtas, tops, and pants. What sets W apart is its focus on comfort and functionality, making it a go-to choice for women who want to look good while going about their daily lives. The brand is also known for its use of vibrant colors and prints that add a pop of fun to any outfit.

4. Manyavar

Manyavar is a brand that caters to men who want to look their best on special occasions. Founded in 1999, it offers a range of traditional Indian clothing options, including sherwanis, kurtas, and suits. Manyavar is known for its attention to detail and use of high-quality fabrics, making it a popular choice for grooms and wedding guests. The brand also offers a range of accessories, including turbans and stoles, to complete the look.

How to Shop for Top Indian Apparel Brands

If you're interested in shopping for top Indian apparel brands, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, do your research to find out which brands align with your personal style and values. Look for reviews and testimonials from other customers to get an idea of the brand's quality and customer service.

Next, consider the occasion for which you're shopping. Some brands specialize in formal wear, while others are more casual or versatile. Make sure you choose a brand that caters to your specific needs.

Finally, pay attention to the brand's sizing and fit. Indian clothing sizes can differ from Western sizes, so it's important to check the brand's size chart and read reviews from customers who have purchased similar items.

Question and Answer

Q: Are these brands only available in India?

A: While these brands are based in India, many of them have international shipping options. Check the brand's website for more information on international orders.

Q: Are these brands affordable?

A: The price range varies depending on the brand and the item. Some brands, like W, offer more affordable options, while others, like Manyavar, are more high-end.

Q: Do these brands offer customization options?

A: Some brands, like Biba and Manyavar, offer customization options for certain items. Check the brand's website or visit a store to learn more.

Q: Are these brands sustainable and ethical?

A: Many of these brands, such as Fabindia, prioritize sustainability and fair trade practices. Check the brand's website or social media pages to learn more about their values and initiatives.

Conclusion of Top Indian Apparel Brands

India's fashion industry is full of talented designers and brands that offer unique and high-quality clothing options. Whether you're looking for traditional wear or fusion styles, there's something for everyone. By keeping in mind the brands' values, occasion-specific needs, and sizing, you can shop with confidence and find the perfect outfit for any occasion.
