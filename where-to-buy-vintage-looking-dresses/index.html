Where To Buy Vintage Looking Dresses A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Dress

Written by Daniel Jul 10, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of scouring through racks of modern clothing, only to come up empty-handed in your search for a unique and timeless dress? Do you dream of finding a vintage-inspired dress that speaks to your individual style? Look no further. In this guide, we will explore the best places to buy vintage looking dresses, so you can finally find the dress of your dreams.

It can be frustrating to search for vintage looking dresses, especially if you don't know where to start. You may have tried browsing through thrift stores or online marketplaces, only to find that the selection is limited or the quality is poor. Additionally, you may worry about the authenticity of the vintage pieces, or struggle to find a dress in your size. All of these factors can make the process of finding a vintage inspired dress overwhelming and stressful.

Fortunately, there are a variety of places where you can find beautiful vintage looking dresses that fit your style, budget, and size. Some of the best options include:

Online Vintage Clothing Stores

One of the most convenient ways to find vintage inspired dresses is to shop online. There are a variety of online vintage clothing stores that offer a wide selection of dresses from various eras, including the 1920s, 1950s, and 1970s. These stores often have a range of sizes and styles to choose from, so you can find the perfect dress for any occasion.

One of my favorite online vintage clothing stores is Unique Vintage. They offer a variety of vintage inspired dresses, ranging from flapper-style dresses to swing dresses to bohemian maxi dresses. I love that they have a size range that includes plus sizes, and they also offer free shipping on orders over $150.

Local Vintage Shops

If you prefer to shop in person, you may want to check out local vintage shops in your area. These shops often have a curated selection of vintage inspired dresses, and you can try them on before making a purchase. Additionally, you may be able to find unique pieces that you wouldn't be able to find online.

One of my favorite local vintage shops is Retro Rejuvenation in downtown Los Angeles. They have a variety of vintage inspired dresses, as well as accessories like hats and jewelry. The staff is also very friendly and helpful, and they can help you find the perfect dress for any occasion.

Etsy

Etsy is a great online marketplace for finding vintage inspired dresses. There are a variety of sellers on Etsy who specialize in vintage clothing, and you can often find unique and one-of-a-kind pieces. Additionally, many sellers offer a range of sizes, so you can find a dress that fits you perfectly.

One of my favorite Etsy sellers for vintage inspired dresses is The House of Findings. They have a wide selection of dresses from various eras, and they offer free shipping on orders over $35. I love that they have detailed measurements for each dress, so you can ensure a perfect fit.

Thrift Stores

While thrift stores may require a bit more effort in terms of searching through racks of clothing, they can be a great place to find vintage inspired dresses at an affordable price. You may need to visit several thrift stores to find the perfect dress, but the thrill of the hunt can be worth it.

One of my favorite thrift stores for vintage inspired dresses is Crossroads Trading Co. They have a variety of gently used clothing, including vintage inspired dresses from various eras. Additionally, they offer cash or store credit for clothing that you no longer want, so you can declutter your wardrobe while also finding a new dress.

Conclusion of Where to Buy Vintage Looking Dresses

Whether you prefer to shop online or in person, there are a variety of options for finding vintage inspired dresses that fit your individual style. By exploring online vintage clothing stores, local vintage shops, Etsy, and thrift stores, you can find a unique and timeless dress that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

Question and Answer

Q: How do I know if a vintage inspired dress will fit me?

A: When shopping online, be sure to check the measurements provided by the seller before making a purchase. If you are shopping in person, try on the dress before making a purchase, and don't be afraid to ask for assistance from the staff.

Q: How can I ensure the quality of a vintage inspired dress?

A: When shopping online, read reviews from other customers to get an idea of the quality of the dress. If you are shopping in person, inspect the dress for any signs of wear and tear, and ask the staff about the condition of the dress.

Q: What if I can't find a vintage inspired dress in my size?

A: Look for stores or sellers that offer a range of sizes, or consider having a dress tailored to fit you perfectly.

Q: Can I wear a vintage inspired dress to a modern event?

A: Absolutely! Vintage inspired dresses are versatile and can be dressed up or down to suit any occasion.
