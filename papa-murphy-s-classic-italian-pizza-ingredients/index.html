Crust

When it comes to pizza, the crust is the foundation. Papa Murphy's Classic Italian Pizza features a thin, crispy crust that is perfect for holding the toppings without getting soggy. The crust is made from scratch daily, using only high-quality flour, yeast, and water. This creates a flavorful base that is both crispy and chewy, providing the perfect texture for the toppings.

Personally, I love the crust on Papa Murphy's Classic Italian Pizza. It's thin and crispy, but still has that perfect chewiness that I crave. I also appreciate that they make it fresh every day, which ensures that the crust is always delicious.

Sauce

The sauce on Papa Murphy's Classic Italian Pizza is made from vine-ripened tomatoes, which are hand-crushed and blended with a blend of Italian spices. This creates a rich, flavorful sauce that complements the toppings without overpowering them. The sauce is also applied sparingly, which allows the crust to shine through.

I love the sauce on Papa Murphy's Classic Italian Pizza because it's not too heavy or overpowering. It adds the perfect amount of flavor to the pizza without being too tomatoey.

Cheese

The cheese on Papa Murphy's Classic Italian Pizza is a blend of mozzarella and parmesan. This creates a melted, gooey cheese that is both salty and creamy. The cheese is also applied generously, which adds to the overall richness of the pizza.

Personally, I love the cheese on Papa Murphy's Classic Italian Pizza. It's deliciously melted and adds the perfect amount of saltiness to the pizza.

Toppings

The toppings on Papa Murphy's Classic Italian Pizza are what truly sets it apart. The pizza features a delicious combination of Italian sausage, pepperoni, mushrooms, and onions. The sausage is made with a blend of pork and Italian seasonings, while the pepperoni is sliced thin and has a slightly spicy kick. The mushrooms and onions add a layer of earthiness and sweetness that complements the other toppings perfectly.

I love the toppings on Papa Murphy's Classic Italian Pizza because they're so flavorful and well-balanced. Each topping adds something unique to the pizza, but they all work together to create a delicious flavor profile.
