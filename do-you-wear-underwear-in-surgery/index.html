Do You Wear Underwear In Surgery A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 01, 2023 · 5 min read

Have you ever wondered if you should wear underwear during surgery? It's a question that many people have, but few are willing to ask. Whether you're going in for a minor procedure or a major surgery, it's important to know what to expect and what's expected of you. In this comprehensive guide, we'll explore the topic of underwear and surgery, so you can feel confident and prepared on the day of your procedure.

One of the most common concerns people have about wearing underwear during surgery is the risk of infection. Many people believe that wearing underwear will increase their risk of infection, but this is not necessarily true. In fact, wearing clean, comfortable underwear can actually help prevent infection by providing a barrier between your skin and the surgical site.

So, do you wear underwear during surgery? The short answer is yes, you should wear underwear during surgery. However, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, your underwear should be clean and comfortable. Avoid wearing anything too tight or restrictive, as this can cause discomfort during and after the procedure. Second, your surgeon may ask you to remove your underwear before the surgery, depending on the type of procedure you're having. In this case, you'll be provided with a hospital gown to wear during the procedure.

In summary, here are the main points to keep in mind about underwear and surgery:

  • Wearing underwear during surgery is recommended
  • Your underwear should be clean and comfortable
  • Your surgeon may ask you to remove your underwear before the surgery

Personal Experience with Underwear and Surgery

As a nurse who has worked in the operating room for over 10 years, I can tell you that wearing underwear during surgery is a must. Not only does it provide a barrier between your skin and the surgical site, but it also helps keep you warm during the procedure. I've seen patients who have refused to wear underwear, and it's always been a bit of a challenge to convince them otherwise. Trust me, wearing underwear is the way to go!

What Happens if You Don't Wear Underwear During Surgery?

While it's not recommended, it is technically possible to have surgery without wearing underwear. However, this is not something that I would advise. Without underwear, your surgical gown could become contaminated with bodily fluids, which could increase your risk of infection. Additionally, without the added layer of protection provided by underwear, you may experience discomfort or chafing during the procedure.

Can You Wear Your Own Underwear During Surgery?

In most cases, you'll be asked to wear hospital-provided underwear during surgery. This is because hospital-provided underwear is specially designed to be sterile and provide maximum coverage and protection. However, if you have a special circumstance or preference, you can speak with your surgeon or nurse to see if it's possible to wear your own underwear during the procedure.

How to Prepare for Surgery

When preparing for surgery, it's important to follow your surgeon's instructions carefully. This may include fasting for a certain period of time before the surgery, avoiding certain medications or supplements, and showering with a special antibacterial soap. Additionally, be sure to bring comfortable clothing to wear home from the hospital, as well as any necessary medical equipment or supplies.

My Personal Advice on Underwear and Surgery

As someone who has worked in the operating room for many years, I can tell you that wearing underwear during surgery is a must. It provides an extra layer of protection and helps keep you comfortable during the procedure. If your surgeon asks you to remove your underwear, don't worry – you'll be provided with hospital-provided underwear to wear instead. And if you have any concerns or questions about the procedure, be sure to speak with your surgeon or nurse.

Question and Answer

A: No, it's not recommended to wear a thong during surgery. Thongs don't provide enough coverage or protection, and can increase your risk of infection.

Q: Do men need to wear underwear during surgery?

Conclusion of Do You Wear Underwear in Surgery

Wearing underwear during surgery is recommended to provide a barrier between the skin and the surgical site, and to help prevent infection. Your surgeon may ask you to remove your underwear before the procedure, but hospital-provided underwear will be provided. If you have any questions or concerns about wearing underwear during surgery, be sure to speak with your surgeon or nurse.
