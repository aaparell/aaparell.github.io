What To Wear To A Native American Wedding A Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Attending a Native American wedding can be a unique and memorable experience. However, figuring out what to wear to such an event can be a daunting task. You want to dress appropriately and respectfully while still feeling comfortable and stylish. In this guide, we will provide you with some tips and ideas on what to wear to a Native American wedding.

The first thing to keep in mind is that Native American weddings can vary in terms of dress code depending on the tribe, location, and the couple's preferences. However, a common theme is a focus on traditional attire and cultural elements. It is also important to note that Native American cultures value modesty, so avoid wearing anything too revealing or flashy.

One option for women is to wear a long skirt or dress with a blouse or shawl. You can incorporate traditional Native American patterns or colors into your outfit, such as turquoise or beading. For men, a shirt with a bolo tie and slacks or jeans can be a good choice. Both men and women can add moccasins or boots for a more traditional look. Avoid wearing hats or headdresses unless you have been specifically asked to do so.

In summary, when figuring out what to wear to a Native American wedding, it is best to err on the side of dressing conservatively and incorporating traditional elements. Women can wear a long skirt or dress with a blouse or shawl, while men can wear a shirt with a bolo tie and slacks or jeans. Moccasins or boots can be added for both men and women.

Personal Experience

When I attended my first Native American wedding, I was unsure of what to wear. I decided to wear a long, flowy skirt with a blouse and added some turquoise jewelry for a pop of color. I also wore moccasins for a more traditional feel. I felt comfortable and stylish while also respecting the culture and traditions of the wedding.

Tips for Men

For men, it can be helpful to dress in layers, as ceremonies can take place outdoors and temperatures can fluctuate. Avoid wearing shorts or anything too casual. A shirt with a bolo tie and slacks or jeans can be a good choice. Adding a vest or jacket can also add a touch of elegance to the outfit.

Incorporating Traditional Elements

One way to show respect for the culture and traditions of the wedding is to incorporate traditional elements into your outfit. This can include adding beading or embroidery to clothing, wearing turquoise jewelry, or adding feathers or fringe to accessories. Research the tribe or region to get a better sense of the traditional dress and colors.

Hair and Makeup

When it comes to hair and makeup, it is best to keep it natural and simple. Avoid anything too bold or dramatic. Hair can be worn down or in a simple updo, and makeup should be kept minimal. If you want to add a touch of color, a red or berry lip can be a good choice.

Personal Experience

At a recent Native American wedding I attended, I wore a long dress with a shawl and added some traditional beaded jewelry. I kept my hair in a simple updo and added a red lip for a pop of color. I felt respectful of the culture while also feeling comfortable and stylish.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a headdress to a Native American wedding?

A: No, unless specifically asked to do so by the couple or tribal leader, it is not appropriate to wear a headdress as it is a sacred and ceremonial item.

Q: Can I wear jeans to a Native American wedding?

A: Yes, jeans can be appropriate for men as long as they are not too casual or distressed. Women should opt for a long skirt or dress instead.

Q: Can I wear black to a Native American wedding?

A: While black is generally acceptable for formal events, it is best to avoid wearing all black to a Native American wedding as it can be seen as a sign of mourning.

Q: Can I wear high heels to a Native American wedding?

A: While not explicitly prohibited, it is best to avoid wearing high heels as ceremonies can take place outdoors and the terrain may be uneven. Opt for comfortable shoes such as moccasins or boots instead.

Conclusion of What to Wear to a Native American Wedding

Attending a Native American wedding can be a wonderful experience, and dressing appropriately can show respect for the culture and traditions. Incorporating traditional elements into your outfit, dressing conservatively, and avoiding anything too flashy or revealing are all good guidelines to follow. With these tips and ideas, you can feel comfortable and stylish while honoring the couple and their culture.
