What Color Suits Are In Style A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby May 31, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a new suit to add to your wardrobe but don't know what colors are currently in style? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the latest trends in suit colors and provide you with some tips on how to choose the perfect suit for any occasion.

When it comes to choosing a suit, many people struggle with finding the right color. It can be overwhelming to navigate the different shades and hues available on the market. Additionally, wearing an outdated or out-of-style suit color can make you appear unfashionable and out of touch.

So, what color suits are currently in style? The answer is simple: earth tones, navy blue, and gray. These colors are versatile, timeless, and suitable for any occasion.

Earth tones, such as beige, khaki, and olive green, are perfect for casual events and outdoor occasions. They are warm, inviting, and pair well with other earthy colors, such as brown and tan.

Navy Blue Suits

Navy blue is a classic suit color that will never go out of style. It is a versatile color that is suitable for both formal and casual events. Navy blue suits can be paired with a variety of dress shirts and ties, making them a great option for any occasion.

Personally, I love wearing a navy blue suit to job interviews. It is a professional color that exudes confidence and competence. I always feel my best when I'm wearing a navy blue suit.

Gray Suits

Gray suits are another classic option that are currently in style. They are elegant, sleek, and perfect for formal events, such as weddings and business meetings. Gray suits come in a variety of shades, ranging from light gray to charcoal, making them a versatile choice for any occasion.

I recently attended a wedding where the groom and his groomsmen wore light gray suits. It was a beautiful color choice that complemented the bride's white dress perfectly.

How to Choose the Perfect Suit Color

When choosing a suit color, it's important to consider the occasion and your personal style. If you're attending a formal event, such as a wedding or business meeting, a navy blue or gray suit is a safe and professional choice. However, if you're attending a more casual event, such as a dinner party or outdoor gathering, an earth tone suit may be more appropriate.

It's also important to consider your personal style when choosing a suit color. If you prefer a more traditional look, a navy blue or gray suit may be the best option for you. However, if you're more adventurous with your fashion choices, an earth tone suit or a bold color, such as burgundy or forest green, may be more your style.

Tips for Accessorizing Your Suit

Once you've chosen the perfect suit color, it's time to accessorize! Here are some tips for accessorizing your suit:

  1. Choose a dress shirt in a complementary color.
  2. Add a tie or bowtie for a pop of color.
  3. Wear dress shoes that match your suit color.
  4. Add a pocket square for a touch of elegance.

The Bottom Line

When it comes to what color suits are in style, earth tones, navy blue, and gray are currently the most popular choices. However, it's important to choose a suit color that complements your personal style and the occasion you're attending.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a black suit to a wedding?

A: While black suits are a classic option, they may not be the best choice for a wedding. Black suits can be too formal and may clash with the wedding colors. It's best to choose a navy blue, gray, or earth tone suit for a wedding.

Q: Can I wear a bold-colored suit to a job interview?

A: It's best to stick to more traditional suit colors, such as navy blue or gray, for a job interview. Bold-colored suits may be seen as unprofessional or distracting.

Q: Can I wear a suit without a tie?

A: Yes, you can wear a suit without a tie. However, it's important to choose a dress shirt with a collar that will look good without a tie. Additionally, wearing a tie is more professional and may be required for certain events or occasions.

Q: Can I wear a suit with sneakers?

A: While it's not the most traditional option, it is possible to wear a suit with sneakers. However, it's important to choose a clean and stylish pair of sneakers that will complement your suit. Additionally, this look may not be appropriate for all occasions.

Conclusion of What Color Suits are in Style

Choosing the perfect suit color can be challenging, but by following the latest trends and considering your personal style and the occasion, you can find the perfect suit for any event. Whether you choose an earth tone, navy blue, or gray suit, accessorizing with complementary colors and styles can elevate your look and make you feel confident and stylish.
