Have you ever wondered how much it would cost to make a real-life Iron Man suit? Well, Adam Savage, the former co-host of Discovery Channel's Mythbusters, did just that. In this article, we'll explore the answer to that question and more.

Building an Iron Man suit is no easy feat, and it certainly doesn't come cheap. From the materials used to the labor involved, there are a lot of factors that go into the cost of building a suit like this.

So, how much did Adam Savage Iron Man suit cost? The total cost of the suit is estimated to be around $250,000. This includes all the materials, labor, and design work that went into building the suit.
