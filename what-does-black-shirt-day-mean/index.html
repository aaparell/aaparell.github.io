However, many people are still not aware of the significance of this day and the reasons behind its observance.

Black Shirt Day is observed on January 15th each year to commemorate the birthday of civil rights activist and Canadian politician, the late Honourable Jean Augustine. Jean Augustine was a trailblazer in the fight for racial equality and diversity in Canada. She was the first Black woman to be elected to the House of Commons in 1993 and served as the Minister of State for Multiculturalism and Status of Women.
