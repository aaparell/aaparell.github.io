Step-by-Step Guide on How to Put Laces in Shoes Vans

When I first got my Vans shoes, I was a little overwhelmed by the lacing system. But after a few tries, I figured it out. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to put laces in shoes Vans:

Step 1: Remove Old Laces

The first step is to remove the old laces from your shoes. This will give you a clean slate to work with and ensure that your new laces fit properly.

Step 2: Choose Your Laces

Make sure you have the right length of laces for your shoes. Vans shoes typically have seven or eight pairs of eyelets, so make sure your laces are long enough to reach all of them.

Step 3: Start Lacing Up Your Shoes

Begin at the bottom of your shoe and thread one end of the lace through the bottom pair of eyelets, starting from the inside of the shoe and going out. Make sure the lace is even on both sides.

Step 4: Criss-Cross the Laces

Take the end of the lace that is on the right side of the shoe and cross it over to the left side, threading it through the eyelet that is directly above it. Then, take the end of the lace that is on the left side of the shoe and cross it over to the right side, threading it through the eyelet that is directly above it. Continue this criss-cross pattern until you reach the top of the shoe.

Step 5: Tie Your Shoes

Once you've laced up your shoes, tie them in a knot or a bow, depending on your preference. Make sure they're snug but not too tight, and you're ready to go!

Conclusion of How to Put Laces in Shoes Vans

Putting laces in shoes Vans doesn't have to be a difficult or frustrating experience. By following these simple steps, you'll be able to lace up your shoes quickly and easily, and you'll be back to your day in no time.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I use any type of laces for my Vans shoes?

A: Yes, you can use any type of laces that fit your shoes. However, it's best to use laces that are similar to the original ones that came with your shoes to ensure a proper fit.

Q: How do I know if my laces are the right length?

Q: What if I can't get the laces through the eyelets?
