Best Leather Hiking Boots Australia Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 21, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you planning a hiking trip in Australia but don't know which boots to choose? Do you want to make sure your feet are protected and comfortable during your adventure? Choosing the right hiking boots can make all the difference, and leather hiking boots are a great option. In this post, we'll guide you through the best leather hiking boots in Australia and help you make an informed decision.

When it comes to hiking, wearing the wrong shoes can lead to blisters, sore feet, and even injuries. That's why it's crucial to choose the right hiking boots, especially if you're planning a long hike or a multi-day trek. The best hiking boots should provide support, comfort, and protection, while also being durable and weather-resistant. Leather hiking boots are a popular choice among hikers, as they offer all these benefits and more.

The best leather hiking boots in Australia are those that fit your feet properly, provide the right amount of support, and can withstand the conditions you'll be hiking in. Some of the top brands to consider include Scarpa, Salomon, and Merrell, as they all have a range of high-quality leather hiking boots that are suitable for different types of hiking.

How to Choose the Right Size

When choosing hiking boots, it's essential to get the right size. Wearing boots that are too big or too small can cause blisters, sore feet, and even injuries. To find the right size, you should measure your feet and try on different boots before making a purchase. Make sure there is enough room in the toe box for your toes to move freely, but not so much room that your foot slides around inside the boot.

How to Break in Your Hiking Boots

Before you go on a long hike, it's a good idea to break in your hiking boots. This involves wearing them around the house and on short walks to allow the leather to soften and mold to your feet. You can also use a leather conditioner to help soften the leather and prevent it from cracking. It's important to break in your boots gradually, rather than wearing them on a long hike straight away.

Merrell Moab 2 Mid Waterproof Boots

The Merrell Moab 2 Mid Waterproof boots are another great option for leather hiking boots in Australia. These boots are made from a combination of suede leather and mesh, which makes them breathable and lightweight. They also have a waterproof membrane, which keeps your feet dry in wet conditions. The Vibram sole provides excellent traction, and the boots have a comfortable fit that doesn't require much breaking in.

Question and Answer

Q: Are leather hiking boots better than synthetic ones?

A: Leather hiking boots are generally more durable and offer better support than synthetic ones. However, synthetic boots are often lighter and more breathable, which can be beneficial in hot weather.

Q: How do I care for my leather hiking boots?

A: To care for your leather hiking boots, you should clean them regularly with a soft brush and mild soap. You can also use a leather conditioner to help maintain their suppleness and prevent cracking. It's important to avoid getting your boots too wet, as this can damage the leather.

Q: How long do leather hiking boots last?

A: The lifespan of leather hiking boots depends on several factors, including how often you wear them and the conditions you use them in. With proper care, high-quality leather hiking boots can last for several years.

Q: Can I wear leather hiking boots in hot weather?

A: Yes, you can wear leather hiking boots in hot weather, but you should choose boots that are lightweight and breathable. Look for boots with mesh panels or ventilation to help keep your feet cool.

Conclusion of Best Leather Hiking Boots Australia

Choosing the best leather hiking boots in Australia can be a daunting task, but with the right information, it doesn't have to be. When choosing boots, consider factors such as support, comfort, durability, and weather-resistance. Some of the top brands to consider include Scarpa, Salomon, and Merrell, as they all have a range of high-quality options that are suitable for different types of hiking. Remember to choose the right size and break in your boots gradually before going on a long hike. With the right pair of leather hiking boots, you'll be ready to tackle any adventure in Australia.
